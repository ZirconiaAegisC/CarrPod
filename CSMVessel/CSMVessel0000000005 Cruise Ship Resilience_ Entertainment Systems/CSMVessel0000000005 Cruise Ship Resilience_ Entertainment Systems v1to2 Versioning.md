## V1.0 vs V2.0 Parameter Changes

| Parameter | V1.0 | V2.0 | Consequence if Not Updated |
|---|---|---|---|
| **GaN audio amplifiers** | Not specified | GaN Class-D: 97% efficiency (same as CSMFAB005 technology) | V1.0 had no specific amplifier specification for retrofit |
| **LRAM theater acoustics** | Not in V1.0 | MIT 2025 LRAM at theater scale: -65 dB at 250 Hz in 15mm panel | Theater acoustic isolation from ship vibration unaddressed in V1.0 |
| **Casino slot machines** | General EMP vulnerability | V2.0: BFRP case + MXene shield: $445/machine; 500 machines = $222,500/ship | V1.0 had no per-machine protection cost estimate |
| **EMI enclo SE** | V1.0 copper cage SE=45 dB | MXene SE=92 dB in BFRP enclosures | 47 dB shielding deficit in all AV server rooms |
| **Signal wiring** | Copper AV cables assumed | PMMA POF optical fiber signal bus (CSMFAB013 marine adaptation) | Metallic AV signal cables form GIC antennas in ship — structural failure of entertainment |

## Unchanged Elements
All core design philosophy, threat physics (GIC induction equations), material selection rationale,
and fundamental architecture are carried forward unchanged from V1.0.
Only the parameters listed above were revised.
