## How to Use This Compendium

This compendium is the authoritative fabrication and design reference for the five-vessel
**Charlemagne Class Fleet** — an electromagnetic-immune, biomimetic, hexapedal electric
vehicle family derived from CSMVessel0000000009.

Each vessel inherits the **Dielectric Citadel** architecture: all structural components maintain
electrical resistivity ρ ≥ 10¹⁰ Ω·m, zero ferromagnetic assemblies, and complete immunity to
Geomagnetically Induced Currents (GICs) as documented in the Carrington Storm Motors
Aegis Protocol. The five vessels are named after minerals whose elemental chemistry directly
relates to the fabrication materials used:

| F2 Doc Range | Vessel ID | Mineral Name | Vessel Type | Occupancy | Scale sf |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| F2-052 to F2-135 | CSMVessel0000000010 | **TOURMALINE** | Child Toy Fun Vessel | 1–2 children | 0.35 |
| F2-136 to F2-219 | CSMVessel0000000011 | **OBSIDIAN** | Adult Motorcycle-Class | 1–2 adults | 0.55 |
| F2-220 to F2-303 | CSMVessel0000000012 | **BERYL** | 4-Seat Passenger Car | 4 adults | 1.0 |
| F2-304 to F2-387 | CSMVessel0000000013 | **AMETHYST** | 8-Seat Passenger SUV | 8 adults | 1.5 |
| F2-388 to F2-421 | CSMVessel0000000014 | **RHODONITE** | ¾-Bus Transit | 24–30 adults | 3.2 |

*Mineral rationale: TOURMALINE contains boron (→ ZrB₂ ceramic); OBSIDIAN is volcanic
basalt glass (→ BFRP fiber feedstock); BERYL is a Be–Al–Si oxide (→ ALON chemistry
family); AMETHYST is crystalline SiO₂ (→ SiC precursor); RHODONITE is manganese
silicate MnSiO₃ (→ Mn in YInMn Blue pigment).*
