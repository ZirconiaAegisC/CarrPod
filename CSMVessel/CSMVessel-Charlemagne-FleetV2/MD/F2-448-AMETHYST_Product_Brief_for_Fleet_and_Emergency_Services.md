**CSMVessel0000000013 AMETHYST**
*Deploy After the Disaster*

AMETHYST is the 8-seat institutional Charlemagne. It is the vehicle your agency sends
into the situation after the conventional fleet has been disabled.

G5 geomagnetic storm disables one-third of the national power grid. Standard EVs are
non-functional within minutes. AMETHYST is still moving people, because its SiC ceramic
structure carries no eddy current. It carries people instead.

Full amphibious capability: Ford flooded access roads? AMETHYST crosses them.
90-minute sealed life support for all 8 occupants. Sealed against toxic gases, particulates,
electromagnetic interference. A mobile safe room that moves at 140 km/h.

**Specifications:**
- 8 × 22 kW hub motors, 176 kW total
- Top speed: 140 km/h
- Range: 240 km highway / 300 km city (8 fully loaded)
- Battery: 58 kWh / 400 V
- Full amphibious, 8 km/h marine paddle propulsion
- 90-min sealed O₂ for 8 occupants
- Dual-AAJ articulating spine for extreme terrain
- ALON extended dome, r = 1,500 mm

**Named for:** Amethyst — crystalline SiO₂ with Fe³⁺ impurity.

in AMETHYST's ZrB₂–SiC ultra-high-temperature ceramic battery vault.
