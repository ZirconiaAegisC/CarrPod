# F2-042 — VARTM and Filament Winding Composite Fabrication
**Series:** CSMVessel-Charlemagne-FleetV2 · **Author:** Carrington Storm Motors · **Rev:** 1.0 · **Date:** June 2026
---


## 1. VARTM (Vacuum-Assisted Resin Transfer Molding)

**Used for:** all flat/moderately curved BFRP hull panels, ALON dome subframes, floor pans

**Equipment needed:**
- Single-sided mold (BFRP tooling for production, Corian tooling acceptable for prototypes)
- Vacuum pump: 0–5 acfm, −97 kPa target
- Resin infusion lines: PEEK tubing (no metallic fittings in mold zone)
- Vacuum bag film: nylon/PVDF bilayer
- Flow media: PVDF peel-ply, polyolefin distribution mesh

**Process parameters (all vessels, same process):**
1. Dry basalt preform cut to mold geometry (±2 mm)
2. Place in mold with flow media, connect inlet/outlet
3. Apply vacuum to −97 kPa (check leak rate <0.5 kPa/min before infusing)
4. Mix Elium® 175 + TBPEH initiator (0.5 wt%), degas 10 min
5. Open resin inlet; flow front should advance at 50–80 mm/min
6. When flow front reaches outlet, close inlet; hold vacuum throughout cure
7. Gel time at 23°C: 45–90 min (adjust initiator 0.3–0.7 wt% for gel control)
8. Post-cure: 60°C / 2 h in oven
9. Demold: minimum 2 h after post-cure at <50°C panel temperature

**NDT acceptance:**
- Ultrasonic C-scan (5 MHz): reject delaminations >6 mm diameter
- LOI (ASTM D2584): FVF 55–65%
- Coupon tensile (ASTM D3039): ≥1,050 MPa, 5 coupons per batch

## 2. Filament Winding

**Used for:** leg tubes, CMBC vault reinforcement shells, pressure cylinders, AAJ bellows reinforcement rings

**Recommended layup angles:**
- Structural tubes (legs): [±12/0/90]₂ → balanced axial + torsional strength
- Pressure vessels (CMBC shell): [±18/90]₃ → Netting analysis optimized for BERYL
- Hoop rings (AAJ reinforcement): [90/90/90] → maximum hoop strength

**CNC mandrel winder settings (BERYL leg tube reference):**
- Mandrel diameter: 80 mm (ID target)
- Band width: 6 mm (basalt roving, 2k fiber per band)
- Tension: 45 N ± 5 N per roving band
- Resin bath temperature: 35°C (Elium® 175, maintained)
- Line speed: 0.8 m/min helical pass
