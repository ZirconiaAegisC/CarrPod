## 1. Executive Summary

The Phoenix Protocol is CSM's circular economy commitment: every material in every
Charlemagne fleet vehicle is recoverable and re-usable at end of life.

## 2. Material Recovery Matrix

**Total recoverable value per BERYL at EOL:** ~$15,220
**BERYL MSRP:** ~$62,000
**EOL recovery as % of original purchase price:** **24.5%**

This means approximately 1 in 4 dollars paid for a BERYL returns to the materials economy
without generating any waste. For a $62,000 vehicle, $15,220 is recoverable at end of life —
approximately twice the value of a comparable steel EV's scrap yield ($6,000–8,000).

## 3. CSM Take-Back Program

All Charlemagne fleet vehicles purchased new include:
- Registration with CSM Phoenix Protocol database
- Geo-tagged serial number linking to material lots
- At EOL: CSM arranges collection and pays $10,000 BERYL / $3,000 TOURMALINE recovery credit
- Collected vehicles → CSM Aegis-Forge I material recovery (CSMFAB000000000108)
  (The Aegis-Forge thermal cascade separator processes the entire vehicle body in 4 hours)

This makes the Charlemagne Fleet the world's first closed-loop vehicle program where
the manufacturer buys back the raw materials it originally sold.
