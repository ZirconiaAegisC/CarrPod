## 2. ALON Single-Source Risk

**Surmet Corporation (Burlington MA) is the sole US commercial ALON producer.**
Mitigation:
1. Year 1: Place 6-month strategic order at program start (hedge against lead-time slippage)
2. Year 2: Initiate development of MgAl₂O₄ spinel as backup (40% cost reduction possible)
3. Year 3: Qualify alternate ALON from Coorstek or other emerging suppliers
4. Year 4+: In-house ALON hot-press capability if volume justifies $1.2M investment
