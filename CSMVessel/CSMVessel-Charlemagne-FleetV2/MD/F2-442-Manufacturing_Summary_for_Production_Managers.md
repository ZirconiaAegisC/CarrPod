## What You Need to Know

You are building five versions of the same vehicle. The manufacturing process is the SAME
for all five — same materials, same methods, same quality checks — just at different scales.

## The Three Things That Make This Different From Any Other EV

1. **No metal structure:** Every structural member is ceramic, composite, or polymer.
   You will never hold a steel subframe or aluminum chassis extrusion. If metal appears
   in a structural role, it's wrong and the part must be rejected.

2. **Megger test is the most important quality check:** The Si₃N₄ bearing installation Megger
   (F2-045, F2-047) is the pass/fail gate that determines if the vehicle is GIC-immune.
   ALL technicians who install bearings MUST be trained and certified in this test.
   No exceptions, no shortcuts.

3. **ALON arrives 14-20 weeks after you order it:** Plan production around ALON lead time.
   The dome is never on the critical path if orders are placed at project start.
