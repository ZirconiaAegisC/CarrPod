## Primary Ceramic Materials References

1. Johnson, R.A. et al., "Flash Sintering of ZrB₂–SiC Ultra-High Temperature Ceramic
   Composites," Acta Materialia, 278, 120223 (2024).
   DOI: 10.1016/j.actamat.2024.120223
   [Definitive reference for flash sintering protocol at 1,800°C, 5 min, 50 MPa]

2. Fahrenholtz, W.G., Hilmas, G.E., Talmy, I.G., Zaykoski, J.A., "Refractory Diborides of
   Zirconium and Hafnium," Journal of the American Ceramic Society, 90(5), 1347–1364 (2007).
   DOI: 10.1111/j.1551-2916.2007.01583.x
   [Foundational UHTC material properties and characterization]

3. Barsoum, M.W., MAX Phases: Properties of Machinable Ternary Carbides and Nitrides,
   Wiley-VCH, Berlin, 2013. ISBN 9783527330119.
   [Ti₃AlC₂ MAX Phase properties; kinking nonlinear elastic behavior; machinability]

4. Ching, W.-Y., Mo, S.-D., Ouyang, L., "Electronic and Optical Properties of Aluminum
   Oxynitride (ALON)," Physical Review B, 63, 245110 (2001).
   [ALON crystal electronic structure, optical absorption edge physics]

5. Patel, P.J., Gilde, G.A., Dehmer, P.G., McCauley, J.W., "Transparent Armor,"
   CTIAC Report, Journal of the American Ceramic Society Special Issue, 2000.
   [ALON ballistic certification and comparison to conventional glazing]

6. Surmet Corporation, "ALON Optical Ceramic: Material Properties and Specifications"
   Technical Data Sheet, Burlington MA, 2025. Available: surmet.com

7. NSK, Si₃N₄ Ceramic Bearing Product Catalog, 2025. Available: nskresearch.com
   [6004 through 6320 series Si₃N₄ bearing specifications used in fleet assembly]

8. ASTM F2919-14 (reapproved 2021), "Standard Practice for the Optical Material
   Characterization of Optical Ceramics," ASTM International.
   [Incoming inspection protocol for ALON panels per F2-047]
