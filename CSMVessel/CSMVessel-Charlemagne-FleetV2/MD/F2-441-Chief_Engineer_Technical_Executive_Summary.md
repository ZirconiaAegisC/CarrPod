## Critical Technical Risks

1. **TOURMALINE f_n detuning (ECO-TOUR-002):** The standard Sorbothane A20 mount
   gives dome f_n = 6.9 Hz (inside 4–8 Hz danger band). A10 is required. VERIFY before production.

2. **RHODONITE CMBC vault size:** sf=3.2 gives 2,100 mm OD — single piece impractical.
   4-vault cluster design (ECO-RHOD-001) must be engineering-validated before fabrication.

3. **ALON procurement:** All dome panels require Surmet approval. Order at program Month 1
   for every vessel prototype simultaneously. Do not assume 14-week lead time will hold 
   during high demand periods.

4. **Si₃N₄ bearing contamination:** The Megger test failure mode (contaminated installation)
   is the most likely production defect. Enforce strict protocol from F2-045, zero exceptions.
