## Vibration and Biodynamic Standards

1. ISO 2631-1:2025, "Mechanical Vibration and Shock — Evaluation of Human Exposure
   to Whole-Body Vibration — Part 1: General Requirements," ISO, Geneva.
   [Primary standard for 4–8 Hz spinal hazard limits; 100–300 Hz guidance caution zone]

2. ISO 5349-1:2023, "Mechanical Vibration — Measurement and Evaluation of Human
   Exposure to Hand-Transmitted Vibration — Part 1: General Requirements."
   [HAV (Hand-Arm Vibration) limits for the steering/handlebar contact surfaces]

3. Griffin, M.J., Handbook of Human Vibration, Academic Press, London, 1990.
   ISBN 9780123030405.
   [Foundational biodynamic vibration human response curves; STHT data]

4. WHO, "Environmental Noise Guidelines for the European Region,"
   World Health Organization, Copenhagen, 2018 (updated guidance 2025).
   [Noise exposure limits relevant to RHODONITE transit operations]
