## 3. Interchangeability Rules (MANDATORY)

**RULE 1 — Common-to-Common:** PN-CC-001 through PN-CC-020 are 100% interchangeable
across all vessels. No verification or qualification needed when substituting within same PN.

**RULE 2 — SF Parts Never Mix:** PN-AAJ-SF-T cannot be used in a BERYL vehicle or any
other vessel. The suffix letter designating vessel must match the vehicle being built.

**RULE 3 — ECO for Any Common Part Change:** Any modification to a PN-CC-XXX part
requires a fleet-wide Engineering Change Order covering all five vessels simultaneously.
Individual-vessel ECOs for common parts are not permitted.

**RULE 4 — Lot Traceability:** Every part installed on every vehicle must be traceable to:
- Supplier lot number
- CSM incoming inspection certificate number
- Vehicle serial number
- Technician ID who installed the part
This traceability links to the 10-year warranty and Phoenix Protocol recovery chain.
