## 1. Application Standard Sequence

All Charlemagne fleet vehicles follow this exterior coating sequence:

### Step 1: MXene Interior EMI Layer (INTERIOR SURFACES ONLY)
- Product: Ti₃C₂Tₓ colloidal suspension (PN-CC-008)
- Application: PEEK CF40 template stencil spray, 5×5 cm tile, 1 mm gaps
- Dry film: 50 µm per tile
- Cure: 80°C / 2 h
- Topcoat: PVDF-TrFE protective 100 µm (within 24 h, prevents oxidation)

### Step 2: CoAl₂O₄ Node Coat (ALON DOME NODES AND STRUCTURAL JOINTS)
- Product: CoAl₂O₄ in polyurethane binder (PN-CC-005)
- Color: Argentum Cyan #1ABC9C
- Purpose: SI = 98, 1,060 nm laser absorption (DEW countermeasure)
- DFT: 35 µm

### Step 3: YInMn Blue + QD Topcoat (ALL EXTERIOR SURFACES)
- Product: YInMn Blue + CsPbBr₃ QD (PN-CC-004)
- Hex Code: Stellar Aegis Blue #1B4F72
- DFT: 50 µm
- SRI: 130
- Cure: 80°C / 45 min

### Step 4: Accent Colors (ZONE-SPECIFIC)
- Heat warning zones, TOURMALINE accents: Cobalt Forge Orange #FF6500 (CoAl₂O₄ thermochromic)
- Emergency egress handles: Iron Carrington Red #C0392B (high-visibility)
- Amphibious wet zones: Argentum Cyan #1ABC9C (cool, water-indicating)
- Interior cabin: Schumann Earth Green #27AE60 (therapeutic, calming)
