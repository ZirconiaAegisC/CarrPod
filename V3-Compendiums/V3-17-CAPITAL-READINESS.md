## 9.0 THE WILLIAMS HEURISTIC — INVESTOR COMMUNICATION PHILOSOPHY

Every financial document in the CSMInvestor package follows three heuristics simultaneously:

**The Williams Heuristic (Mork):** Every technical and financial claim is paired with a plain-language translation. "92 dB shielding effectiveness" becomes "blocks 99.99999999% of electromagnetic energy." "$25M Series A" becomes "we need $25 million to move from lab bench to pilot production." The reader never has to choose between technical precision and comprehension — both are provided, side by side.

**The El Segundo Calm (Chester):** All projections use probability-weighted expected values, not aspirational headlines. The $1.2B Year 5 revenue target is the base case — the bull case is $1.8B, the bear case is $480M. The 65-75% gross margins assume learning curves that have been validated across comparable industries. No panic. No doom-saying. No "the sky is falling." Just "here's the physics, here's the math, here's the manufacturing pathway, here's what could go wrong, and here's what we do when it does."

Every product cost is based on supplier quotes. Every revenue projection is mapped to specific customer segments with documented market sizing analysis. Nothing is rounded to a nice number because it looks good in a deck — numbers are what the math produces.
