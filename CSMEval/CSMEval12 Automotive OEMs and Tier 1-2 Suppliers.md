## Outreach & Distribution Dossier
### Prepared June 2026 | Tier 2: Private Industry | Priority: HIGH

## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Modern vehicles — especially EVs — are dense, GPS-dependent, electronically-controlled platforms whose occupants are simultaneously exposed to two CSM research domains at once: vehicle-level GIC/EMI vulnerability and occupant-level vibration/biodynamic hazard (HAVS-adjacent pedal/steering/seat vibration, documented across the CSMHuman corpus). The May 2024 Gannon Storm already demonstrated real-world consumer-facing GPS degradation (farm equipment "dancing" off-course); the same GNSS-dependent systems increasingly used for lane-keeping, adaptive cruise, and navigation in passenger vehicles are not immune to the same effect. This is the single sector on this list with the most fully-realized, ready-to-license CSM product catalog: thirteen distinct Aegis Embark interior components already exist as complete design briefs.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector

- **Automotive OEMs** (ICE and EV, passenger and commercial)
- **Tier 1 suppliers** — seating systems, interior trim, HMI/infotainment, powertrain electronics
- **Tier 2 suppliers** — component-level (dampers, actuators, films, foams, coatings)
- **EV-specific powertrain and battery-system integrators**
- **Automotive aftermarket / accessory manufacturers**

## 4. Target Contacts — Roles & Departments to Reach

- Director of Interior/Seating Systems Engineering
- NVH (Noise, Vibration, Harshness) Engineering Lead
- HMI/Human Factors Engineering Director
- Advanced Materials / Innovation Scouting group (many OEMs run dedicated open-innovation teams)
- Supplier Diversity / New Technology Sourcing office

## 5. Key Associations, Regulators & Events to Engage

- **SAE International** — automotive engineering standards body, WCX (World Congress Experience) annual event
- **CES (Consumer Electronics Show)** — increasingly automotive-HMI-focused
- **Society of Automotive Analysts** and OEM-specific supplier days/innovation summits
- **NHTSA** (for HMI/safety-adjacent research relevant to Camera-Monitor-System design, per CSMHuman00000000001 V2.0)

## 6. Suggested Outreach Angle

"Thirteen fully-specified interior components, each backed by dedicated biodynamic research, are ready for supplier-day or innovation-scouting review — from pedal-cover vibration damping to post-GPS haptic wayfinding, positioned as a complete NVH-and-resilience interior system rather than a single point product."

## 7. Priority Level & Timing

**HIGH.** Best entry points are OEM supplier days, SAE WCX, and CES; no single regulatory trigger, but the May 2024 Gannon Storm and its farm-equipment GPS coverage give a concrete, recent, consumer-relatable talking point.
