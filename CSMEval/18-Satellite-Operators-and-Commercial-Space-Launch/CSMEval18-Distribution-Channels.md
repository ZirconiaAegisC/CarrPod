paper-submission channels; aiaa.org.

**Space Symposium** (Space Foundation) — engage via its public exhibitor/
speaker-submission channel; spacesymposium.org.

**Satellite Industry Association (SIA)** — engage via SIA's public
industry-affairs channel; sia.org.

**International Astronautical Congress (IAC)** — engage via the
International Astronautical Federation's public paper-submission channel;
iafastro.org.

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Robotics-hardening research referencing a named commercial platform should
be framed carefully to avoid implying an existing relationship with that
platform's manufacturer unless one actually exists — present it as
independent research inspired by a public platform, not an endorsed
partnership.
