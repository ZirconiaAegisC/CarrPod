## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**DOE National Laboratories** — each lab (Sandia, NREL, Lawrence Livermore,
Oak Ridge, etc.) maintains a public Technology Transfer / Partnerships office
— the correct, formal entry point; engage via each lab's public tech-transfer
web page, not an individual researcher's direct line.

**DOE Critical Materials Institute (CMI)** — engage via CMI's public annual
review process and partnership-inquiry channel; cmi.ameslab.gov (hosted at
Ames Laboratory).

**NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology)** — Gaithersburg,
MD-based; engage via NIST's public Electromagnetics Division inquiry channel;
nist.gov.

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

This sector rewards narrow, specific requests far more than a broad corpus
dump — approach the one or two labs whose existing portfolio most closely
matches a specific claim, rather than treating all national labs as one
audience.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

role-holders through each lab's own public staff directory or Technology
