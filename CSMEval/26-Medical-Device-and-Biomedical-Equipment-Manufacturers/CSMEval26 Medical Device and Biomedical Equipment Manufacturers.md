## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

This sector is unique on this list: rather than needing to be introduced to the underlying science, medical device manufacturers are the *direct commercial audience* for a large share of the CSMHuman research corpus's citations, which already draw on cardiac electrophysiology, defibrillation strategy, ultrasonic Doppler vibrometry, and vibration-exposure biomechanics — all core medical-device domains. The CSMHuman00000000031 V2.0 update specifically incorporates the DOSE-VF trial and 2025 systematic review on Double Sequential External Defibrillation (DSED) and Vector-Change (VC) defibrillation strategies, directly relevant to defibrillator manufacturers' clinical-evidence and product-design teams.
