# CSMEval26 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Medical Device & Biomedical Equipment Manufacturers
### Ref: CSMEval26-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually
named clinicians, manufacturer staff, or regulatory officials are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Director of Clinical Affairs / Clinical Evidence | Owns literature-synthesis evaluation |
| VP of Product Development, Cardiac Rhythm Management | Owns defibrillator product relevance |
| R&D Director, Diagnostic Ultrasound | Owns vibrometry diagnostic relevance |
| Regulatory Affairs Lead | Owns FDA 510(k)/PMA pathway relevance |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**AdvaMed** — medical device industry trade association; engage via its
public industry-affairs channel; advamed.org.

**American Heart Association (AHA)** — engage via AHA's public Scientific
Sessions abstract-submission channel; heart.org.

**Heart Rhythm Society (HRS)** — engage via HRS's public annual-meeting
submission channel; hrsonline.org.

**AAMI** — engage via AAMI's public standards/conference channel; aami.org.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| AHA Scientific Sessions | Annual | Primary defibrillation-strategy venue |
| HRS Annual Meeting | Annual | Cardiac-rhythm-specific audience |
| AAMI conferences | Annual | Instrumentation/diagnostic-equipment audience |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Hold any clinical-literature claim to the same evidentiary standard this
audience already applies to its own work — this is the one sector in the
series where overstating confidence would be immediately, professionally
obvious to the reader.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named clinicians, device-company R&D staff, or FDA
officials. Identify current role-holders through each organization's public
directory at time of actual outreach.
