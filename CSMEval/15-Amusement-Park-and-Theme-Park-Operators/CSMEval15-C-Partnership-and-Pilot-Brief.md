PURPOSE
Identify the primary and secondary channels for introducing Project Aegis-K
to theme park operators, ride manufacturers, and safety-certification firms.

PRIMARY VENUE — IAAPA EXPO (ANNUAL, TYPICALLY NOVEMBER)
The primary global industry trade show; the single best distribution event
for this entire sector, reaching operators, ride manufacturers, and
inspection firms in one place, one week a year.

STANDARDS CHANNEL — ASTM F24 COMMITTEE
The American Society for Testing and Materials' F24 Committee on Amusement
Rides and Devices — the correct technical-standards body for any
ride-support-structure materials conversation (BFRP rebar, composite
monopoles).

REGULATORY NOTE
State-level ride-safety regulatory agencies vary by state — relevant for any
operator-specific compliance conversation, but not a single national channel
the way IAAPA and ASTM F24 are.

SUGGESTED SEQUENCING
1. IAAPA Expo exhibitor/technical-session presence (broadest, highest-
   leverage single event).
2. ASTM F24 Committee engagement in parallel (standards credibility for the
   structural-materials angle specifically).
3. Direct facilities-engineering-team follow-up with operators who show
   interest at IAAPA.
