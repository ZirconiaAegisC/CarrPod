## Outreach & Distribution Dossier
### Prepared June 2026 | Tier 2: Private Industry | Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH

## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Amusement parks combine three risk factors other venues rarely combine at the same density: large numbers of the public in a fixed location, ride systems that are simultaneously electro-mechanically complex and structurally massive (steel coasters, motor-driven attractions), and — for many major parks — coastal or floodplain-adjacent siting. The CSM corpus contains a complete, named worked example (Knott's Berry Farm) explicitly combining Carrington-event GIC hardening with amphibious/flood-risk redesign, giving this sector a ready-made technical briefing rather than a hypothetical pitch.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector

- **Major theme park operators** — regional and destination-park operating companies
- **Ride manufacturers** (steel/wood coaster builders, dark-ride and motion-simulator OEMs)
- **Park engineering and maintenance departments** (in-house structural/electrical teams)
- **Independent ride-inspection and safety-certification firms**

## 4. Target Contacts — Roles & Departments to Reach

- VP of Facilities Engineering / Park Operations
- Director of Ride Engineering & Maintenance
- Risk Management / Safety Director
- Chief Sustainability/Resilience Officer (larger operators increasingly have this role)

## 5. Key Associations, Regulators & Events to Engage

- **International Association of Amusement Parks and Attractions (IAAPA)** — annual IAAPA Expo, the primary global industry trade show
- **American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM)** F24 Committee on Amusement Rides and Devices
- State-level ride-safety regulatory agencies (varies by state)

## 6. Suggested Outreach Angle

"We've completed a full Carrington-and-flood dual-hazard resilience redesign for a named major regional park — available as a template case study for presentation at IAAPA Expo or direct briefing to any park operator's facilities engineering team."

## 7. Priority Level & Timing

**MEDIUM-HIGH.** IAAPA Expo (annual, typically November) is the single best distribution event for this entire sector.
