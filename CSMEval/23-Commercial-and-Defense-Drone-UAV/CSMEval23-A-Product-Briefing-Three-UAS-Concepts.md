PURPOSE
Summarize three CSM UAS concepts spanning propulsion research, emergency-
response payload capability, and small-platform research applications.

1. ARCHIMEDES SCREW PROPULSION DRONE REDESIGN — wind-energy-harvesting
   dual-regime propulsion concept (CSMFAB0102), with supporting airborne
   autorotation/ballistics physics research (CSMFAB0109).

2. AEGIS-ASCENSION WEARABLE HEAVY-LIFT DRONE BACKPACK — human evacuation
   payload capability for tsunami/Carrington dual-event scenarios
   (CSMFAB050) — already detailed in full in the emergency-management
   sector's own briefing series (CSMEval01).

3. ARCHIMEDEAN SPIRALING BIRD UAS (ASB-1) — a sub-250g regulatory-class
   wind-research micro-drone (CSMFAB0114), sized specifically for university
   and hobbyist-adjacent research platforms.

MATCHING CONCEPT TO AUDIENCE
• Commercial/delivery/inspection manufacturers: the Archimedes Screw
  propulsion concept.
• Emergency-response and search-and-rescue manufacturers: the Aegis-
  Ascension backpack.
• University aerospace/mechatronics programs: the sub-250g ASB-1 research
  platform.
