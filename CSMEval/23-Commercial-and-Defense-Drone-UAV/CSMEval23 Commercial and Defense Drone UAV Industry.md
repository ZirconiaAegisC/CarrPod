## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Drones are simultaneously GPS-dependent (and therefore exposed to the exact GNSS-disruption effect documented in the May 2024 Gannon Storm) and, in emergency-response contexts, potentially load-bearing for human evacuation — a use case most consumer/commercial drone platforms were never designed for. The CSM corpus contains three distinct, complementary UAS concepts spanning wind-energy-harvesting propulsion, wearable heavy-lift evacuation, and small-platform wind-research applications, giving this sector a range of entry points from hobbyist/university platforms to emergency-response-grade heavy-lift systems.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector

- **Commercial drone manufacturers** (delivery, inspection, agriculture, mapping)
- **Defense/military UAS manufacturers**
- **Emergency-response and search-and-rescue drone operators/manufacturers**
- **University and hobbyist-adjacent small-UAS research platforms** (see also CSMEval35)
