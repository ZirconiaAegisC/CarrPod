                 AWWA ACE IS WHERE THE FIRST MOVER GETS NOTICED
                 CSMEval29-C | June 2026 | v1.0
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PURPOSE
Identify the channels best suited to introducing the Aegis-Hydro framework
to a water utility willing to be an early adopter.

PRIMARY VENUE — AWWA ACE (AMERICAN WATER WORKS ASSOCIATION ANNUAL CONFERENCE)
The primary technical-session venue for this sector; the right place for a
first-adopter case study to get technical-community visibility.

SECONDARY CHANNELS
• Water Environment Federation (WEF) — broader water/wastewater technical
  audience.
• Association of Metropolitan Water Agencies (AMWA) — larger metropolitan
  utility audience specifically, useful given metro utilities' typically
  larger capital budgets.
• EPA Water Infrastructure and Cyber Resilience Division — engage via EPA's
  public stakeholder-engagement channel; relevant given this division's
  broader resilience mandate, even though GMD specifically isn't yet a named
  focus area.

SUGGESTED SEQUENCING
1. AWWA ACE technical-session presence (primary venue, first-adopter
   visibility).
2. AMWA outreach in parallel (targeting utilities with the capital capacity
   to be a credible first adopter).
3. WEF and EPA engagement as broader/longer-horizon awareness-building
   tracks.
