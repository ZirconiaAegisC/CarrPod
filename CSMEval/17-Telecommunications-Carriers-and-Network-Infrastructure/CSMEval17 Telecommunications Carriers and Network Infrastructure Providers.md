## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Telecommunications networks are simultaneously a hazard *victim* (long buried/aerial copper and fiber runs with copper armor/repeaters are GIC-exposed, and HF radio propagation is directly disrupted by the same ionospheric effects that degrade GPS) and a hazard *responder* (communications are the first thing emergency management, utilities, and the public need restored). The CSM corpus contains a genuinely novel research thread directly relevant to this sector's long-term resilience: a proton (H⁺)-based communication and logic architecture explicitly framed as a GIC-immune alternative to electron/copper-based systems — independent of whether it reaches commercial maturity, it is exactly the kind of exploratory research telecom carriers' network-innovation labs track.
