# CSMEval16 — Data Center, Cloud & Colocation Industry
## Outreach & Distribution Dossier
### Prepared June 2026 | Tier 2: Private Industry | Priority: CRITICAL

---

## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Every other sector on this list increasingly depends on data centers being up — GPS/GNSS correction services, insurance underwriting systems, utility SCADA/EMS platforms, and financial transaction processing all run on cloud and colocation infrastructure. A Carrington-class event's grid impact (transformer damage, multi-day-to-multi-week regional outages per the Lloyd's scenario in CSMEval11) is precisely the failure mode data-center backup-power and site-hardening design is supposed to survive — but most current backup-power design assumes *short* utility outages (hours), not the *extended, region-wide, transformer-replacement-timescale* outages a real Carrington event implies. Rare-earth-permanent-magnet supply is also explicitly identified by magnet manufacturers themselves as an "AI Infrastructure & Data Centers" application category, tying the CSM critical-minerals research directly to this sector's supply chain.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector
