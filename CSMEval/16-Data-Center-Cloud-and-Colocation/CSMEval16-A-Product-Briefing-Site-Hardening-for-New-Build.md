PURPOSE
Summarize CSM hardening options addressing the mismatch between typical
data-center backup-power design assumptions and true Carrington-event outage
timescales.

1. AEGIS-C COMPOSITE SHIELDING PANEL — site-level EMI/GIC shielding for
   critical electronics halls (CSMFAB01).
2. NON-CONDUCTIVE BUILDING ENVELOPE — whole-building EMI shielding kit and
   non-conductive materials suite for new-build facilities (CSMFAB011,
   CSMFAB010).
3. RARE-EARTH-FREE MAGNET SUPPLY — cooling-fan and power-electronics magnet
   supply option, directly tied to magnet manufacturers' own "AI
   Infrastructure & Data Centers" application category (CSMMagTech 2026).
4. CHROMATIC FIELD MONITOR — passive on-site GIC/EMF early-warning
   monitoring (CSMFAB021).

WHY NEW-BUILD TIMING MATTERS MORE HERE THAN ALMOST ANYWHERE ELSE
The current AI-driven data-center buildout means new-build design decisions
are being made right now. Site-hardening specifications are dramatically
cheaper to include at design time than to retrofit after a facility is
operating.
