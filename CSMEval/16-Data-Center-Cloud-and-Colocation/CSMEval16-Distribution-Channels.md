## Data Center, Cloud & Colocation Industry
### Ref: CSMEval16-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually named

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| VP of Data Center Engineering / Critical Facilities | Owns site-hardening specification |
| Director of Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery | Owns extended-outage planning |
| Head of Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) infrastructure risk | Owns operational risk modeling |
| Procurement lead, Critical Power & Cooling Systems | Owns component sourcing |
| Sustainability/Supply Chain Resilience Director | Owns magnet/critical-minerals sourcing |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**Uptime Institute** — engage via their public standards/certification
inquiry channel; uptimeinstitute.com.

**Data Center Dynamics (DCD)** — engage via DCD's public conference
exhibitor/speaker channel; datacenterdynamics.com.

**7x24 Exchange International** — critical facilities reliability
association; engage via their public membership/conference channel;
7x24exchange.org.

**AFCOM** — data center management professional association; engage via
their public membership/conference channel; afcom.com.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| DCD conference series | Multiple/year, various regions | Broadest industry reach |
| 7x24 Exchange events | Annual + regional | Reliability-engineering focus |
| AFCOM events | Annual + regional | Data-center management focus |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Given the active buildout, timing outreach to coincide with a specific
operator's announced new-build project (public information, per press
releases) is more effective than generic conference-only outreach.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named data-center engineers or procurement leads. Identify
current role-holders through each operator's public leadership page at time
of actual outreach.
