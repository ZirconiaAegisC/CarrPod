## 1. Roles Most Likely to Engage

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Director, Advanced Concepts / internal innovation division | Owns exploratory-research intake |
| Chief Engineer, Composite Structures | Owns materials-substitution evaluation |
| Program Manager, Legacy Platform Sustainment | Owns A-10-class redesign relevance |
| Director, Space Systems Advanced Development | Owns launch-assist/space concept review |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA)** — engage via
AIAA's public SciTech Forum paper-submission channel; aiaa.org.

**National Defense Industrial Association (NDIA)** — engage via NDIA's
public event/membership channel; ndia.org.

**Space Symposium** — organized by the Space Foundation; engage via its
public exhibitor/speaker-submission channel; spacesymposium.org.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| AIAA SciTech Forum | Annual | Technical paper submission pathway |
| Space Symposium | Annual, Colorado Springs | Broad cross-industry space event |
| NDIA symposia | Annual, various | Defense-industrial-base networking |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Most primes maintain an internal advanced-concepts or "X-division" with its
own review process, often reachable only via conference presence or an
existing relationship — cold corporate-contact-page outreach is a weak
channel for this specific audience.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named advanced-concepts directors or chief engineers.
Identify current role-holders through each company's public leadership page
or conference speaker list at time of actual outreach.
