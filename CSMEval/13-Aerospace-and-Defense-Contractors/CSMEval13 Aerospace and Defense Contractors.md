## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Aerospace primes operate at the intersection of nearly every hazard class in this corpus: airframe EM hardening, satellite/space-vehicle radiation and GIC-adjacent charging effects, and increasingly, novel launch-assist concepts. The CSM corpus contains a directly relevant, fully worked legacy-airframe case study (A-10 Thunderbolt II civilian/military dielectric redesign) and an entire speculative R&D program (the "Smart Rope" segmented piezoelectric actuator and Archimedes-screw-based launch-assist concept) that, whatever its ultimate technical maturity, represents exactly the kind of moonshot materials/actuation research aerospace primes maintain internal advanced-concepts divisions to evaluate.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector

- **Major aerospace/defense primes** — airframe, satellite, and launch-system manufacturers
- **Advanced concepts / X-divisions** within primes (internal moonshot R&D groups)
- **Satellite bus and payload manufacturers**
- **Composite materials and structures subcontractors** serving the aerospace supply chain
- **Launch services providers** (see also CSMEval18 for the space-specific angle)
