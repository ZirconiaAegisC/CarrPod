# CSMEval08 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Municipal Emergency Management, Public Works & Parks/Attraction-Safety Departments
### Ref: CSMEval08-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and associations only — no individually named city/
county officials are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Where Found | Why |
|---|---|---|
| City/County Emergency Manager | Local government | Local implementation tier beneath state EMAs |
| Director of Public Works | Local government | Owns water/sanitation/local-grid-interface infrastructure |
| Parks and Recreation Director / Attractions Safety Officer | Local government | Owns attraction infrastructure |
| Chief Building Official / Fire Marshal | Local government | Ride/electrical permitting authority |
| City Engineer | Local government | Owns municipal structural/civil decisions |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**National League of Cities (NLC)** — Washington, D.C.-based; engage via
NLC's public conference/exhibit channel; nlc.org.

**National Association of Counties (NACo)** — Washington, D.C.-based; engage
via NACo's public conference/exhibit channel; naco.org.

**International Association of Amusement Parks and Attractions (IAAPA)** —
Orlando, FL-based global trade association; engage via IAAPA's public
exhibit/sponsorship channel; iaapa.org.

**American Public Works Association (APWA)** — Kansas City, MO-based;
engage via APWA's public conference/exhibit channel; apwa.net.

**State-level chapters** of city/county officials associations — each state
maintains its own chapter; engage via the state chapter's general contact
channel, distinct from the national bodies above.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| NLC City Summit | Annual | Broad municipal-official audience |
| NACo Annual Conference | Annual | Broad county-official audience |
| IAAPA Expo | Annual | Attraction-industry-specific (see also CSMEval15) |
| APWA PWX (Congress) | Annual | Public works operations audience |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Given the sheer number of individual municipalities, conference-based
distribution is a better fit here than any per-jurisdiction contact strategy
— a single exhibit or session at NLC/NACo reaches more relevant officials than
weeks of individual outreach would.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named city/county emergency managers, public works directors,
or building officials — there are simply too many jurisdictions for this to
be anything other than a conference/association-first strategy by design.
