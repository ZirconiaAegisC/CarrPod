## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Match the channel to ownership type first — a pitch calibrated for a
rate-case-driven IOU capital process will land differently with a
member-governed cooperative board. DistribuTECH and IEEE PES are the
safest first stop when ownership type is not yet known.
