# CSMEval35 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Electric-Vehicle Enthusiast, Maker & DIY/3D-Printing Community
### Ref: CSMEval35-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles, associations, and event types only — no individually
named makerspace directors, forum moderators, or club faculty advisors are
listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Engage

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Local makerspace/hackerspace program directors | Own local build-community access |
| EV conversion hobbyist forum moderators and build-log creators | Own EV-conversion sub-community reach |
| University mechatronics/robotics club faculty advisors | Own capstone-project access |
| High-power rocketry club technical committee members | Own rocketry-specific sub-community |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**Maker Faire** — engage via its public regional/flagship exhibitor
channel.

**Tripoli Rocketry Association** — engage via its public
membership/technical-committee channel; tripoli.org.

**National Association of Rocketry (NAR)** — engage via its public
membership channel; nar.org.

**EAA (Experimental Aircraft Association)** — engage via its public
chapter/event channel for DIY-aerospace-adjacent reach; eaa.org.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Maker Faire | Regional + flagship, multiple/year | Broadest maker-community reach |
| University academic year start | Annual | Capstone-program recruiting window |
| Tripoli/NAR events | Regional + annual | Rocketry-specific sub-community |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Match each open build challenge to its best-fit sub-community rather than
broadcasting all three everywhere — Lattice-Lock and Smart Rope to general
maker/DIY channels, ASB-1 to university robotics programs, and the
Archimedes-screw physics research to rocketry associations specifically.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named makerspace directors, forum moderators, or faculty
advisors. Identify specific contacts through each organization's public
directory at time of actual outreach.
