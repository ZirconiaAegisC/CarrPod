# CSMEval12 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Automotive OEMs & Tier 1/2 Suppliers
### Ref: CSMEval12-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually named
OEM or supplier staff are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Director of Interior/Seating Systems Engineering | Owns component integration decisions |
| NVH Engineering Lead | Owns vibration/noise specification |
| HMI/Human Factors Engineering Director | Owns navigation-redundancy relevance |
| Advanced Materials / Innovation Scouting group | Owns external-technology intake |
| Supplier Diversity / New Technology Sourcing office | Owns formal vendor-intake process |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**SAE International** — engage via SAE's public WCX technical-session
submission channel; sae.org.

**Consumer Technology Association (CTA)** — organizes CES; engage via CTA's
public exhibitor/innovation-award submission channel; cta.tech.

**NHTSA** — engage via NHTSA's public research-partnership and rulemaking
comment channels; nhtsa.gov.

Most OEMs additionally maintain a public open-innovation or supplier-scouting
submission portal (varies by company) — the correct first channel where one
exists, rather than a general corporate contact address.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| SAE WCX | Annual | Automotive engineering standards audience |
| CES | Annual | Increasingly automotive-HMI-focused |
| OEM-specific supplier days | Varies | Highest-conversion channel, requires portal/relationship access |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Cold outreach to a named OEM executive is a weak channel in this industry —
open-innovation submission portals exist specifically to route external
pitches to the right internal reviewer without needing a personal contact.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named OEM or Tier 1/2 supplier engineers. Identify the
correct open-innovation portal or supplier-day contact through each
company's own public website at time of actual outreach.
