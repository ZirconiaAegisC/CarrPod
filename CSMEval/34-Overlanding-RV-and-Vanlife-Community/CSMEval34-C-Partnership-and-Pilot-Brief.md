                 CSMEval34-C | June 2026 | v1.0
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PURPOSE
Identify the trade show, industry association, and club-network channels
best suited to reaching the overlanding, RV, and vanlife community.

PRIMARY VENUE — OVERLAND EXPO
The major annual trade show/gathering for the overlanding community; engage
via its public exhibitor channel — the strongest single introduction point
for the full Aegis Embark vehicle-interior line.

INDUSTRY CHANNEL — RV INDUSTRY ASSOCIATION (RVIA)
Engage via RVIA's public industry-affairs channel for RV-manufacturer-side
reach specifically, distinct from the overlanding-community-focused Overland
Expo audience.

CLUB NETWORK CHANNEL — REGIONAL 4X4 AND OFF-ROAD CLUB COUNCILS
Engage via each regional council's public contact channel; useful for
reaching the safety/trail-preparedness-focused segment of this community
specifically.

SECONDARY AMPLIFIER — COMMUNITY CONTENT CREATORS
YouTube/Instagram build channels are a strong secondary amplifier,
consistent with the content-driven distribution pattern also seen in
CSMEval32 — engage as an ongoing relationship, not a single placement.

SUGGESTED SEQUENCING
1. Overland Expo (primary, highest-leverage single event).
2. RVIA and regional 4x4 club councils in parallel (differentiated
   sub-audiences).
3. Community content-creator relationships as an ongoing amplifier layer.
