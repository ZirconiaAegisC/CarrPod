# CSMEval34 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Overlanding, RV & Vanlife Community
### Ref: CSMEval34-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles, associations, and event types only — no individually
named club organizers, shop owners, or influencers are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Engage

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Overlanding club event/expedition organizers | Own club-level introductions |
| Vanlife conversion shop owners and build-community influencers | Own build-community reach |
| RV manufacturer-brand owner club presidents | Own brand-specific club reach |
| Off-road/4x4 club safety and trail-preparedness coordinators | Own safety-focused sub-audience |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**Overland Expo** — engage via its public exhibitor channel.

**RV Industry Association (RVIA)** — engage via its public industry-affairs
channel; rvia.org.

**Regional 4x4 and off-road club councils** — engage via each council's own
public contact channel (varies by region).

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Overland Expo | Multiple annual regional events | Primary community gathering |
| RVIA events | Annual + ongoing | RV-manufacturer-side reach |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Pair expo/association outreach with community content-creator relationships
(YouTube/Instagram build channels) — this sector's audience discovery
happens substantially through content, not just trade shows.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named club presidents, shop owners, or content creators.
Identify specific individuals and their current public contact process at
time of actual outreach.
