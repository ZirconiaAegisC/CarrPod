# CSMEval11 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Property & Casualty Insurance and Reinsurance Industry
### Ref: CSMEval11-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually named
underwriters, brokers, or exposure managers are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Head of Exposure Management (catastrophe/systemic risk modeling) | Owns model inputs |
| Head of Space, Energy, or Marine underwriting practice | Owns line-specific pricing |
| Chief Underwriting Officer, Specialty Lines | Owns underwriting policy |
| Broker, Risk Engineering / Client Advisory practice | Owns client-facing mitigation recommendations |
| Product Manager for emerging perils | Catastrophe model vendor | Owns model feature roadmap |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**Lloyd's Market Association / Lloyd's Futureset** — engage via Lloyd's
public systemic-risk-research program channel; lloyds.com.

**Reinsurance Association of America (RAA)** — Washington, D.C.-based;
engage via RAA's public industry-affairs channel; reinsurance.org.

**RIMS (Risk and Insurance Management Society)** — engage via RIMS's public
annual-conference exhibitor/session channel; rims.org.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| RIMS Annual Conference | April/May | Risk managers and brokers |
| Monte Carlo Rendez-Vous | September | Reinsurance treaty-cycle audience |
| Bermuda Risk Summit | Annual | Specialty/catastrophe reinsurance |
| Lloyd's Futureset publications | Ongoing | Systemic-risk research program |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

This sector's calendar is unusually rigid — treaty renewal seasons and named
annual gatherings (Monte Carlo, Bermuda) concentrate attention into specific
weeks. Missing the window has a real cost here that's less true in most
other sectors on this list.
