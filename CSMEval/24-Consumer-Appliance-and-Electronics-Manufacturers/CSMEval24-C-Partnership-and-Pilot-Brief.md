New appliance categories launch at specific shows, on a predictable annual
calendar — so let's use that calendar instead of fighting it.

                 TIMED TO THE SHOWS THAT ALREADY LAUNCH NEW CATEGORIES
                 CSMEval24-C | June 2026 | v1.0
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PURPOSE
Identify the trade shows and associations best suited to launching a
resilience/emergency-preparedness appliance product line extension.

PRIMARY VENUES
• CES (Consumer Technology Association) — the broadest new-product-category
  launch venue; strong fit for the more electronics-forward items (Safe-Wave
  microwave, Conductive-Proof tool design).
• International Housewares Association (IHA) Annual Show — the more
  kitchen/housewares-specific venue, strong fit for Stellar-Chill,
  Stone-Bake, and Ancient Hearth.

ASSOCIATION CHANNEL
Association of Home Appliance Manufacturers (AHAM) — engage via AHAM's
public industry-affairs channel for broader manufacturer reach beyond the
two show calendars above.

TIMING NOTE
The emergency-preparedness product angle benefits from proximity to any
widely-reported grid-outage news event — worth holding launch timing loosely
flexible around the CES/IHA calendar to catch a relevant news cycle when
possible.

SUGGESTED SEQUENCING
1. IHA (kitchen-specific fit) and CES (broader electronics fit), run in the
   same show season where possible.
2. AHAM engagement in parallel for manufacturer-association credibility.
3. Public-facing launch timing flexed toward any relevant grid-outage news
   cycle.
