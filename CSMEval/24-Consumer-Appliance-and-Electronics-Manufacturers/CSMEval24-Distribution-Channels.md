# CSMEval24 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Consumer Appliance & Electronics Manufacturers
### Ref: CSMEval24-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually
named manufacturer staff are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| VP of Product Innovation / New Category Development | Owns new-line launch decisions |
| Director of Sustainability & Resilient Design | Owns resilience-framing alignment |
| Chief Engineer, Refrigeration/Cooking Platforms | Owns technical feasibility review |
| Emergency-preparedness product line manager (where one exists) | Owns direct category fit |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**Association of Home Appliance Manufacturers (AHAM)** — engage via AHAM's
public industry-affairs channel; aham.org.

**Consumer Technology Association (CTA)** — organizes CES; engage via CTA's
public exhibitor/innovation-award channel; cta.tech.

**International Housewares Association (IHA)** — engage via IHA's public
exhibitor channel for its Annual Show; housewares.org.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| CES | Annual, January | Broadest consumer-electronics launch venue |
| IHA Annual Show | Annual, March | Kitchen/housewares-specific launch venue |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

This sector rewards timing to a relevant news cycle (grid-outage coverage)
more than most others on this list — hold launch timing loosely flexible
around the CES/IHA calendar rather than locking to a fixed date far in
advance.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named appliance-company product managers or engineers.
Identify current role-holders through each company's public leadership page
at time of actual outreach.
