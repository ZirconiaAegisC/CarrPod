PURPOSE
List the specific working groups, associations, and technical venues best
positioned to receive physical-layer GIC-hardening data ahead of NERC's
TPL-007/EOP-010 guidance revision.

PRIMARY CHANNEL — NERC GMD TASK FORCE / PROJECT 2013-03
The standing working group most directly responsible for GMD mitigation
standard language. Best entry point for hardening data intended to influence
the post-Gannon-Storm guidance revision specifically.

TRADE ASSOCIATION CHANNELS (BY UTILITY OWNERSHIP TYPE)
• Edison Electric Institute (EEI) — investor-owned utilities
• American Public Power Association (APPA) — public/municipal utilities
• National Rural Electric Cooperative Association (NRECA) — cooperatives
Each represents a different ownership structure with different capital
approval processes (PUC-regulated vs. board-governed vs. member-governed) —
worth tailoring the pitch to which one you're speaking with.

TECHNICAL/RESEARCH CHANNEL
• EPRI GMD/SUNBURST program annual workshops — the most credible technical
  venue for hardware validation data, given EPRI's own operational role in
  post-Gannon GIC monitoring.
• IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) GMD/GIC technical committees and annual
  General Meeting — peer-reviewed technical presentation pathway.

SUGGESTED SEQUENCING
1. EPRI GMD/SUNBURST workshop (technical credibility first)
2. NERC GMD Task Force / Project 2013-03 (regulatory relevance second)
3. EEI/APPA/NRECA (commercial adoption third, matched to utility type)
