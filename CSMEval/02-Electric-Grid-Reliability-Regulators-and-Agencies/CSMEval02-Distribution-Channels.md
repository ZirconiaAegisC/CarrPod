**NERC (North American Electric Reliability Corporation)** — engage via NERC's
public stakeholder-comment process for open standards projects (Project
2013-03 GMD Mitigation), not a direct individual — NERC standards development
is explicitly a public-comment, docketed process by design.

**EPRI** — engage via the GMD/SUNBURST program's public program-membership
channel (EPRI membership office), which routes to the correct technical
program manager.

**IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES)** — engage via IEEE PES technical
committee paper-submission process for the annual General Meeting — a
peer-review channel, not a cold-contact channel.

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

NERC standards projects are explicitly public-comment processes — this is the
one channel on this list where "cold outreach" isn't even the right framing;
the correct action is submitting a comment during an open comment period,
which is a matter of public record, not a personal contact.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named NERC standards staff, utility compliance managers, or
PUC engineers. Those roles are best identified, at time of actual outreach,
through each organization's own current staff directory or docket filer list.
