PURPOSE
Summarize Project Aegis-Rail and related CSM research from the private
operator's capital-planning perspective, rather than the federal-regulatory
perspective.

1. PROJECT AEGIS-RAIL — dielectric hardening framework for catenary and
   trackside signaling circuits (CSMProRail2026).
2. WAY-FINDER HAPTIC/LORA NAVIGATION CONCEPT — PTC GNSS-timing disruption
   resilience (CSMFAB038), directly relevant given the May 2024 Gannon
   Storm's documented effect on GPS-guided agricultural equipment.
3. NON-METALLIC STRUCTURAL COMPONENTS — BFRP rebar and composite monopole
   precedent, applicable to bridges, stations, and right-of-way structures
   an operator owns directly (CSMFAB09, CSMFAB0105).
4. CREW OCCUPATIONAL VIBRATION RESEARCH — CSMHuman vibration/HAVS research,
   relevant to rolling-stock crew-comfort and safety engineering.

identical to CSMEval07; the capital-approval conversation is completely
different, which is why this briefing exists on its own.
