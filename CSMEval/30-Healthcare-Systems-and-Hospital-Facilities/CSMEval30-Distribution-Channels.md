# CSMEval30 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Healthcare Systems & Hospital Facilities Management
### Ref: CSMEval30-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually
named hospital staff, clinicians, or administrators are listed. This is a
safety-of-life-critical sector; any actual outreach should go through
formal institutional channels, not informal personal contact.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| VP of Facilities & Engineering (hospital system) | Owns facility-hardening decisions |
| Director of Emergency Preparedness (healthcare-specific) | Owns continuity planning |
| Chief Biomedical Engineer | Owns device/equipment resilience |
| Chief Medical Officer / ED Medical Director | Owns clinical-evidence relevance |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**American Society for Health Care Engineering (ASHE)** — engage via
ASHE's public Annual Conference channel; ashe.org.

**Joint Commission** — engage via its public standards-inquiry channel;
jointcommission.org.

**NFPA, Health Care Facilities Code committee** — engage via NFPA's public
committee-participation process for NFPA 99; nfpa.org.

**AHA / HRS / ACEP** — see CSMEval26-Distribution-Channels for full detail
on the clinical-side channels shared with the medical-device sector.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| ASHE Annual Conference | Annual | Primary facilities-engineering venue |
| NFPA 99 committee cycle | Ongoing | Standards-development track |
| AHA Scientific Sessions / HRS Annual Meeting | Annual | Clinical-side audience (shared with CSMEval26) |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Reach facilities and clinical audiences through separate, appropriately
targeted channels rather than a single combined pitch — a facilities VP and
a Chief Medical Officer read very different kinds of technical framing.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named hospital administrators, biomedical engineers, or
physicians. Identify current role-holders through each health system's
public leadership page at time of actual outreach.
