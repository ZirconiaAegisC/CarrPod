# CSMEval34 — Overlanding, RV & Vanlife Community
## Outreach & Distribution Dossier
### Prepared June 2026 | Tier 3: Hobbyist, Enthusiast & Community Groups | Priority: MEDIUM

---

## 1. Why This Community Cannot Wait

Overlanders, RVers, and vanlife practitioners already live with the exact dependency profile the CSM Aegis Embark vehicle-interior line addresses: extended time in a vehicle cabin, heavy reliance on GPS/GNSS navigation in remote areas with no fallback infrastructure, and — for many builds — deliberately independent power and climate systems that parallel the Aegis Home appliance philosophy at vehicle scale. This community was also directly, if indirectly, affected by the May 2024 Gannon Storm's GPS disruption, since the same GNSS degradation that confused farm-equipment autosteer systems affects any GPS-dependent overlanding navigation unit.

## 2. Organization & Group Types in This Community

- **Overlanding clubs and regional expedition groups**
- **RV owner associations and manufacturer-brand owner clubs**
- **Vanlife conversion builder communities and forums**
- **Off-road and 4x4 club networks** with overlapping remote-travel interest
