Summarize CSM magnet and critical-materials research as a complementary

   landscape mapping (CSMMagEco2026).

WHAT'S ACTUALLY HAPPENING RIGHT NOW IN THIS INDUSTRY
Niron Magnetics broke ground on its Sartell, Minnesota facility in September
2025 (targeting 1,500 tons/year of iron-nitride magnets by 2026, backed by a
$52.2 million DOE/Treasury Section 48C tax credit). MP Materials announced a
$1.25 billion rare-earth magnet manufacturing campus in Northlake, Texas in
February 2026. China still produces roughly 90% of global rare-earth
permanent magnets.

WHY WE'RE OFFERING RESEARCH, NOT A PRODUCT
This corpus doesn't compete with either announced facility — it's a
complementary research contribution (landscape mapping, recycling process,
supply-chain risk data) offered alongside an already-fast-moving industry
trend.
