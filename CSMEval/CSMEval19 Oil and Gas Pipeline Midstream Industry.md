## 1. Why This Sector Cannot Wait

Long steel pipelines are a well-documented GIC exposure pathway distinct from the power-grid case: induced currents accelerate cathodic-protection-system disruption and pipe-to-soil potential swings, contributing to accelerated corrosion at exactly the joints and valve stations operators depend on for leak prevention. Fuel distribution is also a first-order dependency for every other emergency-response sector on this list (generators, emergency vehicles, evacuation transport all need fuel), making pipeline/midstream resilience a force multiplier for broader disaster response, not just a sector-specific concern. The CSM corpus contains a dedicated fuel-infrastructure hardening proposal built around exactly this risk.

## 2. Company Types in This Sector

- **Midstream pipeline operators** (crude, refined products, and natural gas transmission)
