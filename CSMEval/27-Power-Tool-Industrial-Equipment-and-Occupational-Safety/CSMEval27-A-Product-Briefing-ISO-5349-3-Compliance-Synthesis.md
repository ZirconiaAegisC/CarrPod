PURPOSE
Summarize the CSMHuman corpus's synthesis of ISO 5349-3:2025 and related
2025-2026 vibration-exposure research.

1. ISO 5349-3:2025 SHOCK/IMPULSE VIBRATION STANDARD — published December
   2025, adding a dedicated Vibration Peak Magnitude metric to the existing
   continuous-exposure ISO 5349-1 framework. Tracked across more than a
   dozen CSMHuman documents.
2. LOW-COST WEARABLE HAVS EXPOSURE MONITORING — a 2025 wearable-sensor
   transfer-function validation method that could materially lower the cost
   of compliant exposure monitoring.
3. PIEZO1 MOLECULAR MECHANISM RESEARCH — vibration-induced vascular injury
   mechanism research, relevant to the biological rationale underlying
   exposure limits.
4. DAMPED TOOL/EQUIPMENT INTERFACE DESIGN PRECEDENT — the Aegis Embark
   vibration-mitigation product line (armrest, pedal, steering wrap;
   CSMFAB026, 029, 036), relevant as design precedent for PPE and tool-
   interface manufacturers specifically.

WHY THE TIMING HERE IS UNUSUALLY NARROW
Most of this outreach series has a multi-year or evergreen timeline. This
one doesn't — manufacturers and consultancies are actively updating
compliance documentation right now, this quarter, which makes this a
genuinely narrow window rather than a standing invitation.
