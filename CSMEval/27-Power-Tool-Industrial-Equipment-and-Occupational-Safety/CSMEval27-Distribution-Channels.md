# CSMEval27 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Power Tool & Industrial Equipment Manufacturers / Occupational Safety Consultancies
### Ref: CSMEval27-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually
named manufacturer or consultancy staff are listed.

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Director of Product Safety & Compliance (manufacturers) | Owns vibration-emission declarations |
| VP of Engineering, Vibration & Ergonomics | Owns technical compliance response |
| Principal Consultant, Occupational Vibration Exposure | Owns exposure-assessment methodology |
| Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH) practice leads | Owns field-level compliance implementation |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**ISO Technical Committee 108** — owns the ISO 5349 series; engage via
ISO's public committee-participation process; iso.org.

**NIOSH** — engage via its public research-partnership channel;
cdc.gov/niosh.

**American Industrial Hygiene Association (AIHA)** — engage via AIHA
Connect's public conference channel; aiha.org.

**Power Tool Institute** — engage via its public industry-affairs channel;
powertoolinstitute.com.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| AIHA Connect | Annual | Fastest, broadest reach for this narrow window |
| ISO TC 108 meetings | Ongoing | Authoritative standards-development channel |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Given the narrow compliance-update window, prioritize AIHA Connect and
direct outreach over waiting for a full annual-conference cycle across every
channel — speed matters more here than in almost any other sector in this
series.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named compliance officers or industrial hygienists. Identify
current role-holders through each organization's public directory at time
of actual outreach.
