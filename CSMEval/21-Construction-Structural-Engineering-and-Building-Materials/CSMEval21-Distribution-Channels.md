# CSMEval21 — Distribution Channel & Role Guide
## Construction, Structural Engineering & Building Materials Industry
### Ref: CSMEval21-Channels | June 2026

---

**Scope note:** Roles and institutional channels only — no individually
named contractors, engineers, or manufacturer staff are listed (the CSM
corpus's own existing reference to T.Y. Lin International as an audit
recipient, noted in CSMEval05, is not extended here to other firms).

---

## 1. Roles Most Likely to Act

| Role | Why |
|---|---|
| Principal / Director of Structural Resilience Practice | Owns specification recommendations |
| VP of Preconstruction / Materials Innovation (general contractors) | Owns project-level material choices |
| Product Development Director (composite/rebar manufacturers) | Owns manufacturing/diversification decisions |
| Building code committee member (ICC, ACI committees) | Owns code-adoption pathway |

## 2. Institutional Contact Channels

**American Concrete Institute (ACI), Committee 440** — engage via ACI's
public committee-membership process; concrete.org.

**International Code Council (ICC)** — engage via ICC's public code-
development process; iccsafe.org.

**American Institute of Steel Construction (AISC)** — engage via AISC's
public technical-inquiry channel; aisc.org.

**World of Concrete / Greenbuild** — engage via each show's public
exhibitor/speaker-submission channel.

## 3. Conferences & Standing Venues

| Venue | Cadence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| ACI Committee 440 meetings | Ongoing | Primary FRP-reinforcement standards venue |
| World of Concrete | Annual | Concrete-industry-specific trade show |
| Greenbuild | Annual | Sustainability-framed audience |
| ICC code-development cycle | Ongoing | Longer-horizon code-adoption track |

## 4. Channel-Matching Notes

Standards-committee engagement (ACI 440) matters more here than in most
other sectors — code-adoption pathways gate real-world use regardless of
how compelling a trade-show pitch is.

## 5. What Was Deliberately Left Out

No individually named engineers, contractors, or manufacturer staff.
Identify current role-holders through each organization's public committee
roster at time of actual outreach.
