PURPOSE
Summarize CSM non-conductive building materials, with emphasis on their
proximity to existing commercial supply chains rather than novel chemistry.

1. BFRP REBAR — Basalt-Fiber-Reinforced-Polymer rebar fabrication plan
   (CSMFAB09), positioned against ACI 440.11-25 compliance already
   referenced within the CSM corpus.
2. AEGIS GEOPOLYMER CONCRETE — ZrB₂ nano-whisker-doped dielectric concrete
   formulation, addressing pumpability alongside dielectric performance
   (CSMFAB0107).
3. NON-METALLIC STRUCTURAL MONOPOLE/TOWER PRECEDENT — composite power-line
   monopole (CSMFAB0105), a real-load structural proof point.
4. FULL NON-CONDUCTIVE BUILDING SYSTEMS CATALOG — piping, wiring, hardware,
   and ventilation suite (CSMFAB010-020), plus aerogel thermal-break
   insulation (CSMFAB03).

WHY "ADJACENT TO EXISTING SUPPLY CHAINS" IS THE WHOLE PITCH
Materials that require inventing an entire new manufacturing base take a
decade to reach a real project. Materials adjacent to an existing supply
chain can reach a real project in a much shorter window — which is exactly
the argument this sector, more than any other on this list, can actually
evaluate and act on.
