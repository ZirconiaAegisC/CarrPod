**Subject:** Rwanda's ICT ambitions need grid protection to match

Hi The New Times team,

Rwanda's vision of becoming Africa's technology hub — Kigali Innovation City, data centers, smart city initiatives — depends on a power grid that has never been assessed for solar storm protection. CSM-Covenant sees an opportunity for Rwanda to lead the continent in this overlooked dimension of infrastructure resilience.

Dr. Grace Ndlovu, CSM-Covenant's Africa Director, can discuss:
- 8 priority substations for HYDRA hardening
- Why ICT leadership demands grid resilience leadership
- How Rwanda's track record of strategic infrastructure investment makes it an ideal early adopter
- Cost: RWF 12 billion — a modest investment for protecting the foundation of Vision 2050

This is a development and technology story that positions Rwanda as a potential pioneer. Dr. Ndlovu is available for interview.
