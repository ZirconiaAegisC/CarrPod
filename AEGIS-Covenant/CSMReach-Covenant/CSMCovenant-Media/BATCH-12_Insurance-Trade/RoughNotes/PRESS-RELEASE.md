### CSM Agent and Broker Certification Program: 12,000 Insurance Professionals Trained on BILL-005 Infrastructure Products

**INDIANAPOLIS** — The Critical Systems Mandate today announced the CSMFAB-BROKER-001 Infrastructure Insurance Certification Program, which has trained and certified 12,000 insurance agents and brokers across all 50 states on BILL-005 sovereign-backed infrastructure insurance products, parametric CAT bond structures, and the 20 CSM insurance dossiers that form the underwriting basis for infrastructure coverage.

The certification program, developed with the Independent Insurance Agents & Brokers of America (Big "I"), the Council of Insurance Agents & Brokers (CIAB), and the National Alliance for Insurance Education & Research, provides the first standardized professional credential for infrastructure insurance — a segment that pre-BILL-005 was too small and fragmented to support dedicated professional education.

"Insurance agents and brokers are the front line of infrastructure resilience — they are the professionals who explain coverage options to municipal risk managers, utility CFOs, and infrastructure developers," stated Dr. Catherine Mendoza-Park, CSM Director for Insurance Professional Education. "Before CSMFAB-BROKER-001, there was no standardized curriculum that enabled an agent in Kansas to understand the parametric hurricane coverage available to a Florida municipal utility, or a broker in California to explain earthquake CAT bond structures to a water district."

The certification curriculum covers: BILL-005 sovereign reinsurance architecture and carrier integration framework; parametric CAT bond structures including trigger mechanism design, basis risk analysis, and pricing methodology; CSM insurance dossier interpretation — how to use asset-specific vulnerability curves, fragility functions, and Monte Carlo loss simulations in client presentations; and infrastructure insurance regulatory framework including risk-based capital treatment, NAIC model act compliance, and state-specific filing requirements.

CSM's Accountant Heuristic confirmed a 3.2:1 cost-benefit ratio for the certification program, reflecting accelerated product adoption through professional education, improved coverage placement accuracy, and reduced errors and omissions exposure for agents and brokers placing infrastructure coverage. The full curriculum is published under open-access licensing.
