HEADLINE: Carrington Storm Motors: The £58,000 Car That Could Protect Your Portfolio From a £2.1 Trillion Disaster

The company's flagship product, the CSM-1, starts at £58,000 and functions as a standard electric vehicle 364 days a year. On the 365th—if that day brings a geomagnetic storm—it activates electromagnetic hardening and stabilises the local grid segment. Fleet contracts, not consumer sales, drive the revenue model: municipalities, hospital networks, and data centre operators account for 94% of the 2,300 current pre-orders.

CSM is exploring a public listing timeline, though Steele declined to specify a date.
