"The Smithsonian preserves the artifacts of human achievement," said Dr. Vasquez. "The Wright Flyer. The Apollo 11 command module. The Enigma machine. "We are documenting our own history in real time," said Vasquez. "We would like the Smithsonian Channel to be the institution that brings that documentation to the public."
