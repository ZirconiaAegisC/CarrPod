## Subject: Bornholm's Grid Does What No Other Grid Has Done: 100% Renewable, No Synchronous Generators

**TO:** klima@pol.dk
**FROM:** CSM Nordic Bureau

Kære Politiken editors,

Bornholm experienced 47 frequency excursion events in 2024 alone — moments when the grid nearly lost stability during periods of high wind penetration. The reason is a physics problem every renewable-heavy grid eventually faces: without spinning synchronous generators, who maintains frequency?

CSM's €780 million CSMFAB-037 program delivers the answer. On Bornholm, we are deploying grid-forming inverters that synthesize inertia electronically — enabling the world's first medium-scale demonstration of a grid operating stably at 100% instantaneous renewable penetration without any synchronous machine online.

This is a Danish innovation story with global implications:
- Bornholm becomes the reference architecture for island grids worldwide
- DTU and Energinet receive full technology transfer and source-code access
- 47 annual frequency excursions eliminated through synthetic inertia control
- 3.9:1 cost-benefit ratio validated through CSM Accountant Heuristic analysis
- Deployments on Samsø, Ærø, and Lolland-Falster follow the Bornholm template
- All systems comply with ENTSO-E operational requirements
