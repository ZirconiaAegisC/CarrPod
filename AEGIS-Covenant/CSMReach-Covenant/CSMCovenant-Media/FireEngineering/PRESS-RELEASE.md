CHICAGO — The AEGIS Covenant has released a fire-service-specific electromagnetic preparedness protocol addressing the unique vulnerability of modern fire apparatus to geomagnetic disturbance and EMP events.

"When everything else fails," said Patterson, "the fire service still shows up. This protocol ensures we can."
