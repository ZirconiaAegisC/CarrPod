SUBJECT: 82,000 Southern Baptist volunteers, 1,800 mobile units, and one question: what happens when the fuel pumps don't work?

Baptist Press Editorial Team,

Southern Baptist Disaster Relief runs toward the storm. But a Carrington Event is a storm everywhere at once — no unaffected region to stage from, no working fuel pumps, no electronic dispatch. SBDR's 1,800 mobile kitchen and recovery units need protection from the very event they'd be responding to.

The AEGIS Covenant and SBDR have partnered to close that gap: MXene hardening for the fleet, a geomagnetic training module for 5,000 volunteers by Q3 2027, and pre-positioned staging sites coordinated with the Veteran Response Corps.
