**YOUTUBE ORIGINALS: "AEGIS" — THE PLANETARY DEFENSE SERIES FOR A GLOBAL DIGITAL AUDIENCE**

SAN BRUNO, Calif. — The AEGIS Covenant has proposed a YouTube Originals series designed specifically for the platform's global, digital-native audience — short-form, high-production-value episodes following the 18 Agents as they build humanity's electromagnetic defense architecture in real time.

"YouTube's audience is the audience that needs this story most," said Dr. Vasquez. "Young, global, digitally connected, and instinctively aware that the infrastructure they depend on is more fragile than they've been told. These are people who will share a climate documentary. They will share this too — because the story is fundamentally about protecting the internet, the grid, the connectivity that defines their lives."

The proposed series would consist of 10 episodes, each 15-22 minutes, following a single Agent or location per episode: "The General" (Brig. Gen. Chen navigating Congress), "The Material" (the Korean MXene foundry), "The Fleet" (the Charlemagne amphibious exercises), "The Vatican" (the Covenant's presentation to the Pontifical Academy of Sciences), "The Veterans" (a Corps training weekend in rural Pennsylvania), "The Aurora" (the Tromsø observatory during a CME watch), "The Coalition" (Geneva negotiations), "The Grid" (a substation hardening demonstration), "The Warning" (NOAA's Boulder operations center), and "The Future" (the 18 Agents reflecting on what comes next).

Each episode would be optimized for YouTube's distribution algorithms — strong thumbnails, chapter markers, community engagement prompts — and the Covenant has committed to supporting the series with its own social media amplification across all platforms.
