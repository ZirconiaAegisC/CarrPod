Religion News Service Editorial Team,

Six religious leaders. Six theological traditions. One conclusion.

Cardinal Turkson (Catholic), Rev. Dr. Samuel Park (Evangelical), Rabbi David Stern (Reform), Imam Abdullah Hassan (Sunni), Swami Sarvapriyananda (Vedanta), and Venerable Thubten Chodron (Tibetan Buddhist) have signed the Interfaith Declaration on Geomagnetic Stewardship — the first document to unite the world's major faiths behind electromagnetic planetary defense.

Each leader contributed a theological reflection: pikuach nefesh (Judaism: preservation of life above all), zakat and ummah protection (Islam), ahimsa (Hinduism: non-harm), compassion (Buddhism), stewardship (Catholicism), dominion-keeping (Evangelical). Different languages. Same endpoint.
