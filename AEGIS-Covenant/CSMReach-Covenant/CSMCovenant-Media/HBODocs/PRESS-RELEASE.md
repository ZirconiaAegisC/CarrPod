**HBO DOCUMENTARY FILMS: "AEGIS" — THE 18 PEOPLE PROTECTING CIVILIZATION FROM THE SUN**

NEW YORK — The AEGIS Covenant has submitted a feature documentary proposal to HBO Documentary Films, offering an intimate, character-driven portrait of the 18 individuals leading humanity's first coordinated planetary defense against an astronomical threat.

"Every great HBO documentary begins with unforgettable characters," said Dr. Vasquez. "We have 18 of them. A Nobel-laureate materials scientist who discovered MXene and now races to scale it from laboratory grams to industrial tons. A retired Brigadier General who walked the halls of the Pentagon for forty years and now walks the halls of Congress for a different mission. A Catholic nun who brought the Covenant's dossier to the Pontifical Academy of Sciences. A South Korean factory engineer who figured out how to manufacture 45-micron shielding films at a kilometer a minute."

The proposed feature documentary would follow six of the 18 Agents across six filming windows, capturing the geopolitical negotiations in Geneva, the Charlemagne fleet's amphibious exercises off the Portuguese coast, the MXene foundries operating at industrial scale, the veterans training at VFW posts across the American heartland, and the aurora observatories capturing real-time CME data under the Arctic sky.

"HBO has defined the modern documentary with films that are simultaneously intimate and epic," said Vasquez. "This story is both. It's about 18 people. It's also about the entire electrical civilization they're trying to protect. It's 'Citizenfour' meets 'Apollo 11' — with a ticking clock and a happy ending that is not yet guaranteed."

The Covenant has pre-cleared all six filming locations and secured the participation commitments of all proposed principal characters.
