**APPLE TV+: "THE 18" — A CHARACTER-DRIVEN LIMITED SERIES ON THE PEOPLE PROTECTING CIVILIZATION**

CULVER CITY, Calif. — The AEGIS Covenant has submitted a limited documentary series proposal to Apple TV+, focused on the personal stories of the 18 Agents — a character-driven narrative designed for the platform that has defined prestige documentary storytelling.

"Apple TV+ has built its documentary reputation on intimate character stories with global stakes," said Dr. Vasquez. "'The 18' is exactly that. Over six episodes, viewers meet the Nobel-laureate scientist racing to scale a quantum material from lab to factory floor, the retired general navigating a Congress more polarized than the one he served for forty years under, the Catholic nun who brought scientific evidence to the Vatican, the South Korean engineer working 18-hour days to produce shielding film, and the Portuguese naval veteran who looks at 47 amphibious craft and sees an ark."

Each episode would profile three Agents, with overlapping timelines and intersecting narratives that converge in the final episode at the signing of the Global Geomagnetic Crisis Response Coalition in Geneva. The series would be shot across 14 countries, with Apple's signature production values — cinematic vérité, composer-driven scoring, and the kind of intimate cinematography that makes viewers feel they are in the room.

The Covenant has committed all 18 Agents to on-camera participation, secured location access across all filming sites, and pre-cleared archival material including personal journals, laboratory notebooks, and diplomatic correspondence.

"Prestige documentary lives on Apple TV+," said Vasquez. "This is the next one."
