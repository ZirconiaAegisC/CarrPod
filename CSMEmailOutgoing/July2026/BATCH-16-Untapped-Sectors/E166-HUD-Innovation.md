**PARAGRAPH 2 — THE CDBG-DR TIMELINE PROBLEM**

After Hurricane Maria (2017), Puerto Rico received $20 billion in CDBG-DR funds. The first replacement housing unit was completed 42 months after the hurricane. Lonsdaleite Communities bypass that bottleneck: factory-manufactured BFRP panels flat-packed on pallets, shipped to the recovery site, and assembled by a 20-person crew with hand tools at a rate of 2 Nest-Hold dwellings (36m², 1BR) per week. A 50-dwelling Lonsdaleite community can be completed and occupied within 90 days of pallet delivery — compared to 24–60 months for the equivalent site-built housing.
