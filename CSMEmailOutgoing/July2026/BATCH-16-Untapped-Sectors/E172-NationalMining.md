The Aegis-Forge solves this by replacing the acid with heat: a 17-stage thermal cascade that separates REEs by selective vaporization at their characteristic boiling points (800°C to 2,200°C) in a closed-loop argon atmosphere, producing 99.5%+ purity separated oxides with zero liquid waste and only trace solid thorium/uranium oxide captured by the gas recovery system.
