1. Non-conductive shelter containers: deployable emergency shelters that do not conduct GIC — pre-positionable at state EMAC depots, ready for rapid deployment to affected counties.

3. Ascension drone backpack: aerial situational awareness relay — maps damage, locates survivors, and relays communications when ground infrastructure is down.
