## 1. Peripheral Blood Flow: Updated Meta-Analytical Context

V1.0 integrates meta-analytical reviews of peripheral blood flow with cellular mechanotransduction pathways and biodynamic safety standards. V2.0 updates the peripheral blood flow component with corroborating 2025 findings: a systematic review of controlled whole-body-vibration intervention on peripheral circulation, and a 2023 systematic review (with continued citation through 2025–2026) on WBV exercise in cardiovascular disease management, both consistent with V1.0's synthesis that vibration exposure produces measurable, frequency-dependent peripheral hemodynamic effects — in some controlled therapeutic contexts beneficial, in occupational contexts potentially harmful, consistent with the dual-nature emphasis already present in V1.0.
