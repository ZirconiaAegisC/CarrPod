## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Causal evidence superseded correlational evidence** — a 2026-published two-phase controlled study establishes that mirror-gazing *causes* measurable sense-of-agency impairment, moving the topic from descriptive phenomenology to a quantifiable safety variable.
2. **Self-face network model updated** — 2024 cross-disciplinary synthesis replaces the V1.0 two-hub (MedPFC/ACC) model with a 17-region distributed, redundant network.
3. **Applied relevance emerged** — the 2025–2026 regulatory shift toward Camera-Monitor-Systems (CMS) in vehicles creates a direct engineering use-case for V1.0's neuroscience that did not exist at time of original drafting.
