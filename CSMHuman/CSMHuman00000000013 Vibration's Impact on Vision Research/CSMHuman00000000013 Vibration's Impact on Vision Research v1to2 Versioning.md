# CSMHuman00000000013 Vibration's Impact on Vision Research
## V1.0 to V2.0 Versioning Delta
**Type:** Scientific Change Log | **Date:** June 2026 | **Applies to document:** `CSMHuman00000000013 Vibration's Impact on Vision Research V2.0`

---

## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Second pathway discovered** — 2025 review shows WBV degrades vision-adjacent cognitive function via neuroinflammatory/synaptic mechanisms, not just ocular biomechanics.
2. **Modern validation available** — a 2026 HMI study directly confirms the 1998 acuity-decrement finding remains relevant to touchscreen-era vehicle interfaces.
3. **Design margin now quantifiable** — Aegis-C dashboard specifications can be directly justified against the updated frequency-response data.
