## V1.0 to V2.0 Versioning Delta
**Type:** Scientific Change Log | **Date:** June 2026 | **Applies to document:** `CSMHuman00000000025 Vibration Effects on Blood Flow V2.0`

---

## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Mechanosensor identification** — 2025 review work identifies Piezo1 as the specific receptor for the shear-stress changes V1.0's CFD model predicts.
2. **Systemic-scale context added** — 2025 whole-body vibration hemorheology data situates V1.0's localized model within a broader physiological response.
3. **Methodology unchanged** — the CFD approach itself required no revision, only biological-context extension.
