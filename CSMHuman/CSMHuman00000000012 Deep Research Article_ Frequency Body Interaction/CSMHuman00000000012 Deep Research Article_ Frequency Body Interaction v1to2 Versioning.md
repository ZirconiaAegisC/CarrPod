## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Molecular target specified** — Piezo1/Piezo2 frequency-filtering research provides a concrete channel-level mechanism where V1.0 had only generic "voltage-gated ion channel" language.
2. **Dosimetry standards reconfirmed and stress-tested** — ICNIRP 2020 remains current, but a 2026 real-world case study (AM-EAS retail systems) demonstrates a documented exceedance scenario V1.0 could not have cited.
3. **Two-filter model formalized** — macroscopic mechanical resonance and molecular channel transduction are now understood as independent, serial filters rather than a single combined phenomenon.
