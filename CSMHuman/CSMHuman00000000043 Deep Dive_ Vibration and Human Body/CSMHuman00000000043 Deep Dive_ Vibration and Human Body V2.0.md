## 1. Etiological Complexity: A Partial Resolution

V1.0 emphasizes the etiological complexity of HAVS — a century of industrial vibrating-tool use generating a debilitating, progressive, multifactorial disorder resistant to simple causal explanation. V2.0 does not claim to resolve this complexity, but adds one concrete, well-confirmed molecular contributor: **Piezo1-mediated NF-κB/p65 chemokine signaling** under vibration exposure, which plausibly explains a portion of the chronic, progressive vascular remodeling component of HAVS etiology, without displacing the sensorineural and musculoskeletal components that remain mechanistically less well understood.
