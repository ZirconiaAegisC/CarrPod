## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Molecular mechanism confirmed** — 2025 Piezo1 research provides the first specific cellular pathway for the vascular component of the 1994 clinical HAVS classification.
2. **Standards expansion** — ISO 5349-3:2025 covers shock/impulse exposure outside V1.0's continuous-vibration scope.
3. **Field-monitoring cost barrier reduced** — 2025 wearable-sensor method removes dependency on handle-mounted instrumentation.
