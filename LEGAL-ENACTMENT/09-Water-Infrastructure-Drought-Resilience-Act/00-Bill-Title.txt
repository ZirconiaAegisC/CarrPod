SECTION 3. DEFINITIONS.
================================================================================

  In this Act:

    (1) ADMINISTRATOR. — The term "Administrator" means the Administrator of
        the Environmental Protection Agency.

    (2) COMMUNITY WATER SYSTEM. — The term "community water system" has the
        meaning given such term in section 1401 of the Safe Drinking Water
        Act (42 U.S.C. 300f).

    (3) DIRECT POTABLE REUSE. — The term "direct potable reuse" means the
        introduction of advanced treated recycled water directly into a
        drinking water distribution system without an environmental buffer.

    (4) DISADVANTAGED COMMUNITY. — The term "disadvantaged community" means
        a community identified by the Administrator as having an annual
        median household income less than 80 percent of the State median
        household income, or a community with a service population of fewer
        than 10,000 individuals that lacks adequate water infrastructure.

    (5) LEAD SERVICE LINE. — The term "lead service line" means a pipe and
        its fittings that connect the water main to the building inlet and
        are made of lead, galvanized iron or steel that is or was downstream
        of lead components, or are of unknown material.

    (6) PFAS. — The term "PFAS" means perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl
        substances, including perfluorooctanoic acid (PFOA), perfluorooctane
        sulfonic acid (PFOS), and any other substance identified by the
        Administrator pursuant to section 301.

    (7) SECRETARY. — The term "Secretary" means the Secretary of the
        Interior, unless otherwise specified.

    (8) APPROPRIATE CONGRESSIONAL COMMITTEES. — The term "appropriate
        congressional committees" means the Committee on Environment and
        Public Works of the Senate and the Committee on Transportation and
        Infrastructure of the House of Representatives.
