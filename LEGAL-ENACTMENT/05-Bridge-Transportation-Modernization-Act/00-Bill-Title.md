                          A BILL
================================================================================

    To authorize appropriations for a comprehensive national program of
    bridge infrastructure assessment, preservation, seismic hardening,
    advanced corrosion protection, digital monitoring, and related
    research and workforce development; to establish mandatory coating
    and cathodic protection standards for federally funded bridge
    structures; to create a Federal Bridge Fleet Condition Database;
    and for other purposes.
