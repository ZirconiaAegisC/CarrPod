    (b) ACCELERATED SCHEDULE. — The Secretary is encouraged to accelerate
    milestones where feasible and may establish interim milestones not
    specified in this section to support program management.

    (c) QUARTERLY REPORTING. — The BTIMA Program Director shall submit
    quarterly progress reports to the Secretary and to the BTIMA
    Interagency Coordinating Council on the status of each milestone
    established under this section.

    (d) PUBLIC DASHBOARD. — The Secretary shall maintain a publicly
    accessible online dashboard tracking progress against the milestones
    established under this section and against the performance metrics
    established under section 804(c).
