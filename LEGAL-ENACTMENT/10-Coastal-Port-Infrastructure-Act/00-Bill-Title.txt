SECTION 3. DEFINITIONS.
================================================================================

  In this Act:

    (1) ADMINISTRATOR. — The term "Administrator" means the Administrator
        of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.

    (2) BLUE CARBON. — The term "blue carbon" means carbon captured and
        stored in coastal and marine ecosystems, including salt marshes,
        seagrass meadows, and mangrove forests.

    (3) COLD IRONING. — The term "cold ironing" means the practice of
        providing shore-side electrical power to a ship at berth so that
        its auxiliary engines may be shut down, eliminating emissions
        during port stay.

    (4) LIVING SHORELINE. — The term "living shoreline" means a protected,
        stabilized coastal edge made of natural materials such as plants,
        sand, oyster shells, or rock that provides shoreline erosion
        control while maintaining or enhancing coastal habitat.

    (5) PORT AUTHORITY. — The term "port authority" means a State,
        municipal, or other public or private entity responsible for the
        administration, operation, or security of a port facility.

    (6) SECRETARY. — The term "Secretary" means the Secretary of the
        Army, acting through the Chief of Engineers, unless otherwise
        specified.

    (7) APPROPRIATE CONGRESSIONAL COMMITTEES. — The term "appropriate
        congressional committees" means the Committee on Environment and
        Public Works of the Senate and the Committee on Transportation
        and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives.
