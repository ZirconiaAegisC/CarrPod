                               H.R. _______

    To enhance the resilience of the electric grid against wildfire ignition, 
    to establish a national program for wildfire prevention and electric 
    infrastructure hardening, to provide federal cost-sharing for utility 
    undergrounding, advanced detection, and weather monitoring systems, and 
    for other purposes.
