SECTION 3. DEFINITIONS.
================================================================================

  In this Act:

    (1) ADVANCED PACKAGING. — The term "advanced packaging" means semiconductor
        packaging technologies including Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate (CoWoS),
        Embedded Multi-Die Interconnect Bridge (EMIB), Foveros, hybrid bonding,
        and other three-dimensional integration technologies identified by the
        Secretary of Commerce.

    (2) ADVANCED SEMICONDUCTOR. — The term "advanced semiconductor" means logic
        chips manufactured at process nodes of 7 nanometers or below, high-
        bandwidth memory (HBM), and AI accelerator chips.

    (3) CRITICAL MINERAL. — The term "critical mineral" has the meaning given
        such term in section 7002 of the Energy Act of 2020 (30 U.S.C. 1606).

    (4) SEMICONDUCTOR FABRICATION FACILITY. — The term "semiconductor
        fabrication facility" means a facility for the front-end fabrication
        of integrated circuits on semiconductor wafers.

    (5) SECRETARY. — The term "Secretary" means the Secretary of Commerce,
        unless otherwise specified.

    (6) APPROPRIATE CONGRESSIONAL COMMITTEES. — The term "appropriate
        congressional committees" means the Committee on Commerce, Science,
        and Transportation of the Senate and the Committee on Energy and
        Commerce of the House of Representatives.
