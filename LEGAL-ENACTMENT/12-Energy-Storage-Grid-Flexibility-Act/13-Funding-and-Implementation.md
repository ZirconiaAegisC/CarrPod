### SECTION 1103. OVERSIGHT, REPORTING, AND GAO AUDIT.

**(a) Annual Storage Deployment Report.** — Not later than March 31 of each year, the Secretary of Energy shall submit to Congress a report detailing:

(1) energy storage capacity deployed in the preceding year by technology, duration class, and region;
(2) cost trends for each storage technology category;
(3) manufacturing capacity additions and domestic content percentages;
(4) grid reliability and flexibility metrics;
(5) job creation and workforce development outcomes; and
(6) progress toward the 600 GW deployment target.

**(b) GAO Independent Evaluation.** — The Comptroller General shall conduct evaluations at years 3, 5, and 8 after enactment.

**(c) Public Dashboard.** — The Secretary shall maintain a publicly accessible online dashboard providing real-time data on all metrics specified in subsection (a).

**(d) Sunset.** — Authority to provide new grants and tax credits shall expire on September 30, 2036, except that obligations incurred before such date shall remain in effect.

## TITLE XII — IMPLEMENTATION AND ECONOMIC IMPACT

### SECTION 1201. IMPLEMENTATION SCHEDULE.

**(a) Phase I — Program Establishment (FY 2027-2028).**
- 90 days: publish Federal Register notices for all grant, loan, and tax credit programs;
- 180 days: FERC initiates Order 2222 compliance and interconnection reform rulemakings;
- 270 days: LDES Program and Gigafactory Program solicitations published;
- 365 days: first LDES demonstration projects awarded; VPP standards published;
- Dec 31, 2028: FERC Order 2222 fully implemented in all RTOs/ISOs.

**(b) Phase II — Early Deployment (FY 2029-2030).**
- FY 2029: first iron-air and flow battery manufacturing facilities under construction; 50 GW cumulative storage deployed;
- FY 2030: VPP procurement mandates in effect for large utilities; interconnection average wait time reduced to 24 months.

**(c) Phase III — Scale-Up (FY 2031-2033).**
- FY 2032: 200 GW cumulative storage deployed; 20 GW VPP aggregated capacity;
- FY 2033: domestic battery supply chain meeting 50 percent of domestic demand; LDES cost target of $75/kWh.

**(d) Phase IV — Full Capacity (FY 2034-2036).**
- FY 2035: 600 GW cumulative storage deployed; 80 GW VPP aggregated; LDES cost target of $50/kWh;
- FY 2036: domestic supply chain meeting 80 percent of demand; program review and reauthorization.

### SECTION 1202. MILESTONE ACCOUNTABILITY.

The Secretary shall submit quarterly progress reports. If any milestone is projected to be missed, a corrective action plan shall be submitted to Congress not later than 90 days before the milestone deadline.
