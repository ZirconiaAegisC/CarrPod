Teaching the World to Read the Storm. Featuring Mork, Chester, and Zirconia.

THE QUICK PITCH

Research without training is a library no one reads. CSMSOPP is the educational engine — where the Williams, El Segundo, and Accountant heuristics are taught as operational skills. Three training tracks: BAKER-STREET for insurance adjusters, EL-SEGUNDO for aerospace engineers, STARLIGHT for executives and officials. Each track includes GUIDE, MODULE, MODULE-V2, and RESEARCH materials. A trainee who completes a track in four hours emerges capable of making a defensible recommendation about Carrington preparedness in their domain. The BAKER-STREET track is the source of the Baker Street Adjuster character from SiblingFrequency Episode 5 — the metafictional actuary whose dialogue was reviewed by the actual loss-control engineers who helped design the curriculum. Step into the training center.

THE CONVERSATION

A training facility. Three classrooms are visible through glass walls: BAKER-STREET, EL-SEGUNDO, STARLIGHT. Mork is moving between classrooms. Chester is reviewing feedback forms. Zirconia stands at the curriculum index.

ZIRCONIA: Welcome to CSMSOPP. I am Zirconia, director of education, and these are Mork and Chester. The tradition of professional training — from trade apprenticeships to modern certification programs — provides the framework. CSMSOPP applies it to a discipline that did not exist before CSM created it. Mork, BAKER-STREET.

MORK: Named for 221B Baker Street. The adjuster's job is detection: distinguish GIC-induced insulation degradation from ordinary aging, identify magnetostrictive fatigue crack patterns, price the residual risk after hardening. The MODULE provides five case studies — three GIC-induced failures, two ordinary aging, one mixed — with annotated photographs and actuarial exercises. The MODULE-V2 added fifty annotated photographs and a notation guide based on first-cohort feedback.

CHESTER: EL-SEGUNDO trains engineers to integrate dielectric materials into existing platforms. Satellite bus protonic backup. UAV GaN power hardening. Faraday-cage avionics bay design. STARLIGHT trains executives through a four-hour tabletop Carrington exercise — forty-five minutes of warning, escalating through grid collapse, water loss, hospital generator exhaustion. The training improves because we measure it. For sure.
