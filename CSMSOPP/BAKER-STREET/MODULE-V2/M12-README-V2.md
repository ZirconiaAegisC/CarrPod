## Deployment Architecture

### Required Files
- `GUIDE/CSMSOPP000003-Baker-Street-Heuristic-SOP.md` — Core SOP
- `RESEARCH/` — 70 pre-research documents (R00-R70)
- `MODULE-V2/` — 12 operational modules (M00-M12)

### Skill Activation
The `baker-street-adjuster` skill is activated through Kilo's progressive disclosure:
1. Level 1 loads the YAML frontmatter (~100 tokens)
2. Level 2 loads the full SKILL-V2.md (<5,000 tokens)
3. Level 3 loads specific research documents and templates on demand
