## Source Material
Watson's publications in The Strand Magazine — the public-facing documentation of Holmes's cases. Written for a general audience, maintaining engagement while preserving factual accuracy and client confidentiality.

## Insurance Application: The Watson Blog Standard

Every Baker Street agent deliverable intended for client consumption follows the Blog Standard:

### The Blog Standard Checklist
1. **Accessible:** Written at a 10th-grade reading level. No unnecessary jargon.
2. **Accurate:** Every number, every date, every policy reference is verified against source documents.
3. **Confidential:** Client identity, specific loss details, and financial information are protected per clearance protocols (R05).
4. **Actionable:** The reader knows exactly what to do next. No ambiguity.
5. **Engaging:** The document respects the reader's time. Information is structured hierarchically (summary first, detail following).

### The Blog Format

```
TITLE: [Clear, one-line summary of content]
DATE: [ISO 8601]

SUMMARY: [3 sentences maximum — the entire document in miniature]

BODY: [Organized by topic, clear section headers, bullet points for lists]

NEXT STEPS: [Numbered list of actions required]

APPENDIX: [Supporting data, references, methodology notes — for those who want to go deeper]
```
