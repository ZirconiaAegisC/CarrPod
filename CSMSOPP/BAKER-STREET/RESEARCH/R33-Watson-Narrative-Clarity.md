## Source Material
Every Sherlock Holmes story is narrated by Watson. Holmes observed; Watson explained. This division created the most effective investigative partnership in literary history.

## Insurance Application: The Watson Narrative Protocol

Every Baker Street agent deliverable includes a Watson Narrative section:

### Structure

```
WATSON NARRATIVE — [Claim Number]
DATE: [ISO 8601]

1. WHAT HAPPENED
   [Plain-language summary of the loss event, 3-5 sentences]

2. WHAT THE POLICY SAYS
   [Relevant coverage parts, limits, and exclusions in plain language]

3. WHAT THE INVESTIGATION FOUND
   [Key evidence, witness statements, expert opinions, summarized]

4. WHAT WE'RE DOING
   [Current status, next steps, expected timeline]

5. WHAT THE INSURED NEEDS TO DO
   [Actionable items for the policyholder, if any]

6. WHEN THIS WILL BE RESOLVED
   [Estimated completion date with contingency padding]
```

### The Watson Standard
"Could a non-insurance professional read this and understand exactly what's happening with their claim?" If no: rewrite. If yes: deliver.
