## Escalation Levels for Catastrophic Claims

| Level | Trigger | Response | Timeframe |
|-------|---------|----------|-----------|
| ROUTINE | Standard claim, within limits | Normal adjustment | 30 days |
| ELEVATED | Claim exceeds 50% of limit; coverage question | Senior adjuster assigned | 14 days |
| ALERT | Multi-line exposure; potential reserve inadequacy | Legal + actuarial notified | 7 days |
| CAVALRY | Catastrophic event; systemic exposure breach | Full response team; director briefed | 24 hours |
| OMEGA | Grid-down scenario; market-wide event | Government liaison; treaty activation | Immediate |

## Cavalry Activation Criteria
- Single event generating >50 claims in one LOB
- Aggregate exposure exceeding 75% of reinsurance cover
- Regulatory inquiry coupled with active litigation
- Solar weather event exceeding G4 classification with confirmed grid impacts
