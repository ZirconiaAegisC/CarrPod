## Shield Persona Research | CSMSOPP000003

## Source Material
The Index — S.H.I.E.L.D.'s database of enhanced individuals. 084s (objects of unknown origin).

## Insurance Application

The Index becomes the Baker Street agent's **Policy and Claims Registry**:

- Every policy is catalogued with full metadata (limits, exclusions, endorsements, effective dates)
- Every claim is tracked from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) to final settlement
- 084 analogues: claims with unusual characteristics flagged for senior adjustment
- Enhanced individual analogues: high-value or high-exposure accounts requiring specialized handling

## Tracking Standards

| Index Field | Insurance Equivalent | Format |
|-------------|---------------------|--------|
| Subject ID | Policy Number | Alphanumeric unique |
| Classification | Line of Business | Standard LOB code |
| Threat Level | Exposure Rating | 1 (low) to 10 (catastrophic) |
| Containment Status | Claims Status | Open / Reserved / Closed |
| Handler | Adjuster of Record | Name + License # |
| Last Review | File Audit Date | ISO 8601 timestamp |
