## Insurance Application: The Monograph Library

The Baker Street agent maintains a monograph library of specialized insurance knowledge:

### Monograph Categories

1. **Peril Monographs:** Deep-dive analyses of specific perils — hurricane, earthquake, flood, wildfire, cyber, solar storm. Each monograph contains the physics of the peril, historical loss data, and mitigation strategies.

2. **Industry Monographs:** Sector-specific underwriting guides — construction, healthcare, manufacturing, transportation, energy. Each monograph maps the regulatory environment, common claims patterns, and risk selection criteria.

3. **Regulatory Monographs:** Jurisdiction-specific compliance guides — California, New York, Florida, Texas, London Market, Bermuda. Each monograph summarizes filing requirements, rate regulation, and market conduct standards.

4. **Fraud Monographs:** Scheme-specific detection guides — staged accidents, provider fraud, premium fraud, arson. Each monograph details indicators, investigation protocols, and prosecution strategies.
