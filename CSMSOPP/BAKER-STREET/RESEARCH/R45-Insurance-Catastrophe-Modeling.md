### Model Components
1. **Hazard Module:** Frequency and severity of events
2. **Vulnerability Module:** Damage as function of event intensity
3. **Exposure Module:** Values at risk (replacement costs, BI values)
4. **Financial Module:** Policy conditions applied to modeled losses

### Lloyd's Futureset
The program that modeled a severe solar storm at $2.4 trillion global economic loss. Key findings:
- Expected direct insurance loss: ~$17 billion
- Primary impacted lines: power generation, satellite, aviation, marine, BI
- Model gap: no physical mitigation layer included in the model

### The CSM Mitigation Layer
The Baker Street agent integrates CSM's hardening research into catastrophe model outputs:
- Aegis-C SE 148-165 dB provides the physical mitigation layer
- Charlemagne Fleet research informs marine exposure quantification
- Post-mitigation loss estimates feed underwriting credits and client risk-engineering
