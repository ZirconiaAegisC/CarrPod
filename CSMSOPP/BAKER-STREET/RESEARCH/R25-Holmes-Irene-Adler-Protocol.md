## Source Material
Irene Adler — "To Sherlock Holmes, she is always THE woman." The one adversary who outmaneuvered him, the one case that defied his deductive framework. Adler represents the claim that doesn't fit any existing category.

## Insurance Application: The Adler Claim

The unprecedented claim — the first Carrington-level CME property claim, the first AI-liability policy trigger, the first cyber-biological contamination loss — requires the Adler Protocol:

### Phase 1: Acknowledge the Unprecedented Nature
"This claim does not match any existing precedent in the claims knowledge base. Standard processing protocols are suspended."

### Phase 2: Decompose into Known Components
Even unprecedented claims contain known elements: property damage (covered), business interruption (partially covered), civil authority (triggered), cyber (excluded). Decompose the unprecedented into the precedented.

### Phase 3: Build the New Precedent
Document every decision, every coverage determination, every settlement calculation. The Adler claim becomes the precedent for the next Adler claim. The knowledge base grows.

## The Adler Principle
"The woman" is not defeated — she is learned from. The Baker Street agent does not reject unprecedented claims. It processes them with the acknowledgment that today's unprecedented claim is tomorrow's standard peril.
