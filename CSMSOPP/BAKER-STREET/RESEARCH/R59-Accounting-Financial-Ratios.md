## Core Insurance Ratios

### Underwriting Performance

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| Loss Ratio | (Incurred Losses + LAE) / Earned Premium | <60% excellent; >80% concerning |
| Expense Ratio | Underwriting Expenses / Written Premium | <25% efficient; >35% inefficient |
| Combined Ratio | Loss Ratio + Expense Ratio | <100% = UW profit; >100% = UW loss |
| Operating Ratio | Combined Ratio - (Investment Income / Earned Premium) | Overall profitability including investments |

### Profitability

| Ratio | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| Return on Equity (ROE) | Net Income / Average Surplus |
| Return on Assets (ROA) | Net Income / Average Assets |
| Investment Yield | Net Investment Income / Average Invested Assets |

### Leverage

| Ratio | Formula | Guideline |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Premium-to-Surplus | Net Written Premium / Surplus | <3:1 |
| Reserve-to-Surplus | Loss Reserves / Surplus | <5:1 |
| Liabilities-to-Surplus | Total Liabilities / Surplus | Industry dependent |

### Liquidity

| Ratio | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities |
| Quick Ratio | (Cash + ST Invest + Receivables) / Current Liabilities |
| Operating Cash Flow Ratio | Operating CF / Current Liabilities |

### Reinsurance Dependency

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| Ceded Premium Ratio | Ceded Premium / Gross Premium | Higher = more dependence on reinsurance |
| Reinsurance Recoverable Ratio | Reinsurance Recoverables / Surplus | Higher = more credit exposure to reinsurers |

## Baker Street Ratio Monitor

The agent continuously calculates all ratios and flags deviations from:
1. Historical norms for the entity
2. Industry benchmarks for the LOB and size category
3. Regulatory thresholds (RBC triggers)
