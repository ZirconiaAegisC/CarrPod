## The Principle

Raymond does not make claims based on preference, emotion, or marketing. He makes claims based on statistical fact. Qantas had, at the time of the film, zero fatal jet airliner accidents. This is not an opinion about Qantas. This is a data point. Raymond reports the data point without embellishment.

## The "Never Crashed" Limitation

Qantas had never crashed — until it did. The Baker Street agent acknowledges survivorship bias. Historical data is informative but not predictive. Tail risk exists. The Framework Protocol (R07) models the crash that hasn't happened yet.
