## Core Mechanism

Raymond counts everything. This is not obsessive-compulsive behavior in the clinical sense — it is a cognitive architecture optimized for numerical pattern recognition applied universally.

## Insurance Applications

### Premium Audit
The counting engine processes every transaction in a premium audit sample with zero fatigue. 10,000 payroll entries. 5,000 vehicle classifications. 3,000 square footage measurements. Each one individually verified against classification codes, rate tables, and historical benchmarks. Human auditors sample; the counting engine counts everything.

### Reserve Adequacy
Case reserves are set by human adjusters using judgment. The counting engine cross-references every open reserve against every closed claim with matching characteristics (LOB, jurisdiction, injury type, attorney involvement) and flags reserves that deviate from the mean by more than one standard deviation. Reserve inadequacy becomes a computation, not a guess.

### Claims Data Integrity
Duplicate payments, split claims, and provider overbilling are invisible to human reviewers scanning thousands of line items. The counting engine sees each line item individually and flags: "Line 4,287: amount $1,247.23 matches Line 2,156. Amount $1,247.23. Duplicate probability: 99.7%."

## The Qantas Principle
Raymond's famous line: "Qantas. Qantas never crashed." This is not a statement of preference. It is a statement of statistical fact. The Baker Street agent applies the same principle: "Combined ratio under 100. Line profitable. Recommend renewal." No opinion. Just data.
