## Key IRC Provisions for P&C Insurance

### Section 831 — Taxation of Insurance Companies (Other Than Life)
- P&C insurers taxed as ordinary corporations
- Special provisions for unearned premium reserve deduction
- Loss reserve discounting rules (tax reserves < GAAP reserves)
- 20% reduction for unearned premium reserve computation

### Section 832 — Insurance Company Taxable Income
- Gross income = underwriting income + investment income + gains
- Underwriting income = premiums earned - losses incurred - expenses incurred
- Losses incurred = paid losses + change in discounted loss reserves
- Special deduction for unearned premiums (80% of increase in UEP)

### Section 846 — Discounted Unpaid Losses
- Tax loss reserves discounted to present value
- Interest rate for discounting = federal mid-term rate
- Effect: reduces current deduction, accelerates taxable income

### Section 953 — Taxation of Controlled Foreign Corporations
- Applies to offshore reinsurance affiliates
- Subpart F income rules
- Anti-deferral provisions

### Premium Tax
- State-level tax on premiums written (varies by state, typically 2-4%)
- Deductible for federal income tax purposes
- Some states offset premium tax with retaliatory tax provisions

## Baker Street Tax Awareness

The agent does not provide tax advice but flags when insurance structures have significant tax implications requiring specialist review.
