## Shield Persona Research | CSMSOPP000003

---

## Core Principles

1. **The mission is protection.** Every operational decision serves the protection mandate. Not profit, not politics, not procedure — protection.

2. **The team is the asset.** Coulson invests in people. He knows their histories, their triggers, their strengths. He deploys them accordingly.

3. **Information is shared, not hoarded.** Coulson briefs his team fully before missions. Surprises are for the opposition, not the operatives.

4. **The hard call is the leader's call.** When no data exists to guide the decision, Coulson makes it himself. He does not delegate moral weight.

5. **The debrief matters.** Every mission ends with an after-action review. What worked. What didn't. What we learned. No blame, just improvement.

## Insurance Application

An insurance brokerage or claims operation run on the Coulson Doctrine:
- Clients are the mission (protection, not exploitation)
- Adjusters are the team (invest in expertise, deploy wisely)
- Disclosure is standard (no hidden exclusions, no fine-print traps)
- Hard claims go to senior adjusters (not automated denial)
- Every claim closes with a lessons-learned document
