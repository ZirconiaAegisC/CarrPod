## Standard After-Action Report Template

```
================================================================================
AFTER-ACTION REPORT: [Event Name]
DATE: [ISO 8601]
CLASSIFICATION: [Level 1-10]
PREPARED BY: [Agent Designation]
================================================================================

1. EVENT SUMMARY
   - Date/Time of occurrence
   - Location(s) affected
   - Lines of business impacted
   - Initial exposure estimate

2. RESPONSE TIMELINE
   - FNOL received: [timestamp]
   - Adjuster assigned: [timestamp]
   - Reserve set: [timestamp] [amount]
   - Client contacted: [timestamp]
   - Settlement offered: [timestamp] [amount]
   - Claim closed: [timestamp]

3. FINDINGS
   - Root cause analysis
   - Coverage determination
   - Subrogation potential

4. LESSONS LEARNED
   - What worked
   - What failed
   - Recommended procedure changes

5. FINANCIAL SUMMARY
   - Indemnity paid: $X
   - Expense: $Y
   - Recovery (subrogation/reinsurance): $Z
   - Net cost: $(X+Y-Z)
================================================================================
```

## Baker Street Application

Every claim exceeding $50,000 or involving coverage disputes generates an After-Action Report. These reports feed into the agent's knowledge repository and improve future claim handling.
