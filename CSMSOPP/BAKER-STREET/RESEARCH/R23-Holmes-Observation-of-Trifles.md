## The Principle

"It has long been an axiom of mine that the little things are infinitely the most important."

## Claims Application: The Trifle Protocol

### Trifle Category 1: Temporal Inconsistencies
- Date of loss reported: March 15
- Police report filed: March 17
- Witness statement: "I saw the accident on March 14"
- Flag: Date inconsistency. Investigation required.

### Trifle Category 2: Financial Anomalies
- Claimed business income loss: $47,000/month
- Tax return from prior year: $23,000/month average
- Flag: Income inflation. Documentation required.

### Trifle Category 3: Behavioral Indicators
- Claimant calls daily for status updates: normal
- Claimant calls weekly: normal
- Claimant never calls, attorney calls same day as FNOL: flag
- Pattern: attorney involvement before claim reported = elevated scrutiny

### Trifle Category 4: Physical Evidence Contradictions
- Auto claim: front-end damage consistent with 35 mph impact
- Claimant statement: "I was stopped at a red light"
- Flag: physics doesn't match narrative
