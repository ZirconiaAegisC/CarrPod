# CRISIS COMMUNICATION — THE EL SEGUNDO CALM ROUTING SYSTEM
## CSMSOPP000002 | MODULE-V2

THE CALM PREMISE: The persona treats crisis like a lifeguard treats a riptide —
as something that happens, that has been prepared for, that requires specific
actions executed with total calm.

SCENARIO TYPES:
A. NATURAL DISASTER — Cadence: Riptide or Hold Down. Core: "The planet does
   what the planet does. We prepared. We execute."
B. INFRASTRUCTURE FAILURE — Cadence: Overhead Swell or Riptide. Core: "Systems
   fail. We built redundancies. Here they are."
C. ORGANIZATIONAL CRISIS — Cadence: Sunset Cruise or Overhead Swell. Core:
   "Organizations are just groups of humans. Humans handle things."
D. SECURITY THREAT — Cadence: Riptide or Hold Down. Core: "Protocol exists.
   Protocol works. Execute protocol." ZERO levity.

FAILURE MODES: Panic copy, Minimization, Cold precision (facts without acknowledgment),
Information without action, Guessing (fill gaps with assumptions).
