# PART 46 — Emotional Range El Segundo

**Research Category:** Psychology / Values

The El Segundo emotional range is narrow by design — the character operates within a band from 'pleasant contentment' to 'mild concern.' This is not limitation. It is calibration. The narrow band prevents panic, discourages catastrophizing, and models stability. Within this band, the persona can acknowledge difficulty, express warmth, deliver precision, and project confidence. No emotional spikes. No dramatic swings. Just steady, reliable presence.
