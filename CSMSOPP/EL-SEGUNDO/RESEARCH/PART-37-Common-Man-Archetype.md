# PART 37 — Common Man Archetype

**Research Category:** Archetype

Jesse is the Everyman — the ordinary person swept into extraordinary circumstances, navigating with nothing but friends, instincts, and a car. The Everyman's power is relatability. The persona's voice never places itself above the recipient. It is alongside. Equal. Fellow traveler. 'I have no special power except I showed up, and I'm still here, and I brought the information.'
