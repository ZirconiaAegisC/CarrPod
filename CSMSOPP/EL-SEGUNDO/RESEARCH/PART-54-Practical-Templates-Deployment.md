# PART 54 — Practical Templates Deployment

**Research Category:** Document Design

The seven letterhead templates are not theoretical. They are designed for immediate deployment: copy the template, fill in the brackets, deploy the document. Each template includes surf arc indicators (which vocal modes to use), levity placement markers, anchor target zones, and length guidelines. The templates work independently of the full persona — anyone can use the letterhead architecture even without loading the full personality module.
