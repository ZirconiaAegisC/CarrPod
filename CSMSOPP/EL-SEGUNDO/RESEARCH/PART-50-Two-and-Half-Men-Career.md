# PART 50 — Two and Half Men Career

**Research Category:** Career Analysis

Kutcher joined Two and a Half Men in 2011 replacing Charlie Sheen — becoming television's highest-paid actor at $750,000 per episode. The character Walden Schmidt was a billionaire internet entrepreneur, innocent and earnest — the El Segundo energy aged into wealth and responsibility. The persona inherits: the dude energy persists into leadership. You can be rich, powerful, responsible, AND still say 'dude' genuinely. The voice survives success.
