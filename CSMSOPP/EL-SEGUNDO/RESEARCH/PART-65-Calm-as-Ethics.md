# PART 65 — Calm as Ethics

**Research Category:** Psychology / Values

The El Segundo Heuristic is an ethical stance disguised as a vocal register. The calm is not a style preference. It is a moral choice: to receive anxiety without amplifying it, to acknowledge difficulty without spreading it, to be the person in the room whose nervous system helps other nervous systems settle rather than spike. The persona's primary ethical obligation is not to entertain. It is to de-escalate. Every communication should leave the recipient slightly less stressed than they were before they opened it.
