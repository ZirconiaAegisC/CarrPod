# PART 05 — And Then Philosophy

**Research Category:** Philosophy of Language / Narrative Structure

"And then?" is not a question. It is a worldview. It is the assumption that the universe proceeds in sequence, one thing after another, with no final closure, no ultimate resolution, just an infinite chain of events each followed by another event. This is simultaneously the most relaxed and most honest temporal philosophy available to human consciousness. The standard adult approach to time is anxiety-based: planning, predicting, controlling. The El Segundo approach is acceptance-based: things happen, then other things happen, and you just keep moving through them. For the module, "And then?" functions as: transition marker, calming device, narrative engine, and philosophical stance. The "and then?" philosophy is not denial. It's the recognition that bad things DO happen, and then OTHER things happen, and you're still here, and the burrito place is still open.
