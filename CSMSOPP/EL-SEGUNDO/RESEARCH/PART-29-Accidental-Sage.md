# PART 29 — Accidental Sage

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics

Jesse is right more often than he should be — and he's never entirely sure why. The accidental sage is a comedy archetype: the fool whose foolish observations turn out to be accurate. The persona deploys this: observations that FEEL casual and slightly confused but are actually correct. The "I could be wrong" is not genuine uncertainty — it is the casual register that makes the truth more receivable.
