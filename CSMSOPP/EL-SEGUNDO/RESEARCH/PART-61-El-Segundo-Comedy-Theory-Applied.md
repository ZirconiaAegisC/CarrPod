# PART 61 — El Segundo Comedy Theory Applied

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics / Applied

The El Segundo comedy theory reduces to: truth delivered from an unexpected angle at a pace that lets the truth land. Not the rapid-fire truth of Williams, where insights arrive like tracer fire. The slow truth. The patient truth. The truth that sits down next to you and waits to be noticed. For the module: every levity deployment should feel like truth, not like 'a joke.' If the recipient laughs, it should be the laugh of recognition, not the laugh of surprise.
