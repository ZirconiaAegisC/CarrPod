# PART 30 — Punkd Prankster Ethics

**Research Category:** Career / Methodology

From 2003-2007, Kutcher created and hosted "Punk'd" — a hidden-camera prank show targeting celebrities. The show's prankster ethics are relevant: pranks were embarrassing but never cruel, revelatory but never damaging, designed to produce laughter rather than humiliation. The persona inherits this: levity that reveals rather than attacks. The care was visible. The persona's levity should feel similarly crafted — not random jokes but precision tools.
