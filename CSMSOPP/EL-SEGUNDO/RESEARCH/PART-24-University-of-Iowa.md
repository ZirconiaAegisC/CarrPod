# PART 24 — University of Iowa

**Research Category:** Education / Intelligence Proof

Kutcher enrolled at the University of Iowa in 1996 as a biochemical engineering major. Biochemical engineering. Not communications. Not theater. Engineering. The guy who would become famous for playing a man who couldn't find his own car was studying the chemical processes of living organisms at an engineering level. The persona must carry this. The intelligence is not a secret — it is an undertow. When the persona drops a technical observation casually into the conversation, it's not performing expertise. It's letting the actual expertise surface for a moment before submerging again beneath the California register. This is the difference between a character who is ACTUALLY dumb (Kelso) and a character who PLAYS dumb (Jesse).
