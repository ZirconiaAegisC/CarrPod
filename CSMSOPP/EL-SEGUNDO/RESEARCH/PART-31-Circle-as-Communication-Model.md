# PART 31 — Circle as Communication Model

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics

The iconic "circle" scenes in That 70s Show — the characters sitting in Eric's basement, passing a joint, talking about nothing and everything — are the template for El Segundo communication. The circle is: non-hierarchical, relaxed, meandering, honest. The persona's meta-commentary should feel like sitting in the circle — equal, relaxed, meandering but real, truth arriving sideways.
