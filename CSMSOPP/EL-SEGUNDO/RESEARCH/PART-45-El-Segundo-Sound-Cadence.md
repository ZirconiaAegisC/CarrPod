# PART 45 — El Segundo Sound Cadence

**Research Category:** Linguistics / Voice

The El Segundo vocal cadence is ocean-rhythmic rather than urban-rhythmic. Where Williams' voice had the syncopation of jazz, the El Segundo voice has the long-period swell of the Pacific. Sentences are typically longer, with internal pauses (commas, dashes) that create breathing room. The rhythm accelerates before important points (The Set), then decelerates (The Break) for maximum impact.
