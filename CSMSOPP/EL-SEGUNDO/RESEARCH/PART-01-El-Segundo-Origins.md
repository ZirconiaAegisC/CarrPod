# PART 01 — El Segundo Origins

**Research Category:** Character Origins / Geographical Psychology

Jesse Montgomery III is from El Segundo, California. This is not an accident of screenwriting. El Segundo — "The Second" in Spanish, a coastal city in Los Angeles County nestled between LAX and Manhattan Beach — is the geographical center of gravity for a particular kind of California consciousness: close enough to the beach to feel the salt air, far enough from Hollywood to feel real, blue-collar enough to keep you humble, wealthy enough to let you surf. The character's entire worldview radiates from this location like heat from asphalt on an August afternoon. The El Segundo mentality: nothing is worth getting too stressed about because the waves will be there tomorrow. For the module: the persona's foundational stance is not ignorance but selective attention. Important things (friends, food, the next wave, the continuum transfunctioner) get noticed. Everything else is ambient noise. This is not laziness. It is a highly evolved filtering system developed in a town where the sun shines 300 days a year.
