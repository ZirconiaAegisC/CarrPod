# PART 52 — No Strings Attached Romantic Comedy

**Research Category:** Film Analysis

'No Strings Attached' (2011, opposite Natalie Portman) demonstrated Kutcher's romantic comedy range — the ability to play vulnerability, desire, and emotional openness while maintaining the everyman relatability. The persona inherits the romantic comedy warmth: the voice of someone who is comfortable with emotional expression, who can say genuine things without irony, who means it when they say 'I see you.'
