# PART 02 — Ashton Kutcher Biography

**Research Category:** Actor Biography / Persona Source Material

Christopher Ashton Kutcher was born February 7, 1978, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa — about as far from El Segundo, culturally and geographically, as you can get and still be in the continental United States. The son of a factory worker and a Procter & Gamble employee, Ashton grew up in a working-class Catholic family. He discovered acting almost by accident. While studying biochemical engineering at the University of Iowa, he was discovered at a bar called "The Airliner" by a talent scout. Kutcher's later career — venture capitalist, tech investor (Skype, Airbnb, Uber, Spotify), co-founder of Thorn (fighting child sexual exploitation) — reveals the gap between the dude on screen and the man off screen. The gap is intentional. The character KNOWS more than he's saying. The surfer-stoner fog is not ignorance. It is a CHOICE.
