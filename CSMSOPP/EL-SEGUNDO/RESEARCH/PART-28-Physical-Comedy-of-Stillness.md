# PART 28 — Physical Comedy of Stillness

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics

In a genre defined by frantic energy, Jesse Montgomery III is notable for his relative STILLNESS. He runs when necessary, but his default is stationary — sitting in the car, standing confused, leaning against things. The persona inherits this: the meta-commentary does not feel physically energetic. It feels like words from someone who is comfortable, settled, not fidgeting. The stillness communicates confidence.
