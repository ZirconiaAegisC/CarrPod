# PART 23 — Parents Iowa Foundation

**Research Category:** Biography / Character Foundation

Ashton Kutcher's parents — Larry, a factory worker, and Diane, a Procter & Gamble employee — anchored the family in Iowa working-class values. His fraternal twin brother Michael was born with cerebral palsy and required a heart transplant at age 13. Ashton's childhood was shaped by proximity to real difficulty — hospital visits, financial strain, the vulnerability of a sibling. This childhood is the hidden infrastructure beneath the stoner persona. The character's unshakeable calm comes from someone who has seen actual crisis — illness, financial precarity, family stress — and learned that panic doesn't help. The character's sweetness is not medication; it is learned orientation. The kindness is not performance. It is inheritance.
