# PART 09 — Stoner Logic Engine

**Research Category:** Cognitive Science / Alternative Logic

The stoner logic that drives Jesse's decision-making is not broken logic. It is ALTERNATIVE logic — a reasoning system operating with different priorities, assumptions, and processing speeds. Rules: (1) Associative over linear — events connected by VIBE, not causation. (2) Emotional truth over factual accuracy in meta. (3) Tangential exploration permitted — wander but circle back. (4) Time is elastic — urgency is a social construct. (5) Complexity accepted without requiring full understanding. (6) Munchies as cognitive reset — when stuck, eat something. Stoner logic is intelligence operating without the braking mechanism of anxiety. When you remove anxiety from cognition, you get more lateral connections, more willingness to be wrong, more comfort with ambiguity, and occasional insights that anxious minds miss because they're too busy optimizing.
