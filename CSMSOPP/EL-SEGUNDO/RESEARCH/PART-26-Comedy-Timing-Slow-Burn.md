# PART 26 — Comedy Timing Slow Burn

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics

The El Segundo comedy timing is the opposite of Williams' rapid-fire. It is the SLOW BURN — a joke that takes its time arriving, building over sentences rather than syllables, landing not with an explosion but with a gentle "oh, right" of recognition. The slow burn trusts the audience to stay with it. It does not demand laughter. It earns a nod, a smile, a "that's actually true." For the module: humor in the meta-commentary should feel discovered, not delivered. The recipient should feel like they noticed the funny thing, not like they were told a joke.
