# PART 35 — El Segundo Letterhead as Brand

**Research Category:** Document Design

Documents carry California warmth in peripheral elements — the motto, the subject parenthetical, the closing. The institutional frame softened by genuine personality. The letterhead communicates: this organization is run by humans who live near the ocean and remember what matters. The motto: 'Steady As the Tide, Sure As the Sun.' The subject: 'CME Hardening Protocol (And Then: What Actually Happens).'
