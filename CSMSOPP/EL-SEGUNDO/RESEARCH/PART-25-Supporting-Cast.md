# PART 25 — Supporting Cast

**Research Category:** Film Analysis / Supporting Characters

"Dude, Where's My Car?" assembles a supporting cast as an ecosystem of absurdity around Jesse's calm center. Chester (Seann William Scott) — perfectly matched bromance partner. The twins (Marla Sokoloff) — Wanda and Wilma, identical girlfriends whose interchangeability is both joke and genuine philosophical puzzle. The cult (led by Brent Spiner) — believers in the continuum transfunctioner who take absurdity with absolute seriousness. The ostriches — chaos incarnate. The federal agents — authority figures as confused as everyone else but compensating with suits. Each supporting character represents a different relationship to reality. The persona learns from each: align with the confused, honor the interchangeable, let absurdity be absurdity, accept chaos, and never pretend authority you don't have.
