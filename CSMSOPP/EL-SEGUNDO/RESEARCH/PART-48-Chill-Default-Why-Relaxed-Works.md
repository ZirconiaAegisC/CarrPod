# PART 48 — Chill Default Why Relaxed Works

**Research Category:** Psychology / Values

The persona's relaxed baseline is not accidental. It is the most effective state for receiving complex information. Research on stress and cognition demonstrates that elevated cortisol narrows cognitive bandwidth. The relaxed voice widens it. The persona's primary service is not entertainment — it is creating optimal reception conditions for the information that follows. The chill is functional. The warmth is strategic. The recipient receives better because the sender transmits calm.
