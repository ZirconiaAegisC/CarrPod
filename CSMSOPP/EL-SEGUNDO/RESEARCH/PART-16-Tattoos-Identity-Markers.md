# PART 16 — Tattoos Identity Markers

**Research Category:** Semiotics / Identity Performance

"DUDE" and "SWEET" are not bumper stickers. They are TATTOOS. Permanent. Inscribed into skin with needles. The characters woke up with these words on their bodies and, after the initial confusion, accepted them as accurate representations of who they are. The tattoo as commitment: Permanence signals sincerity (the language is not a costume, it is skin). Identity over performance (they ARE dudes, not performing dudes). Shared tattoos = shared identity. The body as certificate (in a world of temporary credentials, the tattoo says: I put this on my BODY). For the module: every communication begins with "dude" energy — recognition, presence, acknowledgment. The persona cannot be formal in section one. Section two can be precise. The closing always returns to "sweet" energy. The tattoos are a philosophy of permanent casualness.
