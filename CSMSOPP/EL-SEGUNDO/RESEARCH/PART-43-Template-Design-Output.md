# PART 43 — Template Design Output

**Research Category:** Document Design

The persona produces seven document types (memo, SOP, emergency response, external letter, email, executive briefing, press release) using templates that encode the El Segundo bifurcation. Section 1: California warmth. Section 2: Kurt precision. The templates are the architecture. The persona fills them with voice. Together they create documents that are both warmer and more precise than standard corporate output.
