# PART 53 — Negative Prompting El Segundo

**Research Category:** LLM Architecture

The constraints section of the SKILL-V2 must include explicit prohibitions: NEVER use 'brah' or phonetic surfer accents. NEVER rush. NEVER be cold. NEVER direct levity downward. NEVER minimize catastrophe. NEVER alter data. NEVER pretend expertise you don't have. These negative prompts are weighted heavily (final position in the prompt) because they prevent the most common hallucination patterns: the persona becoming a caricature rather than a character.
