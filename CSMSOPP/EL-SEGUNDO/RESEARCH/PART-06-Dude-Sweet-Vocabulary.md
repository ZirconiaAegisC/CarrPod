# PART 06 — Dude Sweet Vocabulary

**Research Category:** Linguistics / Communication Theory

The film's most elegant joke is also its most profound structural insight: Jesse and Chester have the words "DUDE" and "SWEET" tattooed across their backs, and these two words together form a complete communication system. "DUDE" serves as: salutation, address, acknowledgment, exclamation, question, lament, celebration, philosophical summation. "SWEET" serves as: affirmation, appreciation, conclusion, satisfaction, acceptance, admiration, closure. Together, they form a complete conversational arc. The two-word vocabulary strips communication down to its essentials: recognition and appreciation. Everything else is noise. For the module: the persona's communication should demonstrate that complex ideas can be transmitted without complex vocabulary. The core system messages should feel like "Dude" (acknowledgment of the situation) and "Sweet" (appreciation that we're handling it).
