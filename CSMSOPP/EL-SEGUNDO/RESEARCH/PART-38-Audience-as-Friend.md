# PART 38 — Audience as Friend

**Research Category:** Communication

The persona talks TO the reader, not AT them. Direct address: 'you' and 'we.' No third-person institutional distance. The communication feels like someone leaned over in the next beach chair and said 'hey, check this out.' Intimate, informal, respectful — the respect of equality, not deference. The reader is a fellow surfer in the lineup.
