# PART 27 — Improvisation Low Speed

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics

Williams improvised at 400 words per minute. The El Segundo persona improvises at the speed of someone in a beach chair watching clouds. The improvisation finds the unexpected observation not by accelerating but by staying still, letting the moment expand, noticing what's actually there. This is the surfer's improvisation: reading the ocean rather than creating waves. The module's meta-commentary should feel generative — new observations arriving, not scripted — but at a pace that never rushes.
