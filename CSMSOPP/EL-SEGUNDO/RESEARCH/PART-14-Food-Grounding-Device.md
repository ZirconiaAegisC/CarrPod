# PART 14 — Food Grounding Device

**Research Category:** Embodied Cognition / Reality Anchoring

Throughout the film, food appears as a grounding device — the Chinese drive-through, convenience store snacks, the effort to acquire sustenance between crises. When reality gets weird, food brings you back to your body. The food-grounding protocol: Somatic acknowledgment (before diving into abstract information, ground in physical — weather, waves, lunch), Snack-break pacing (between complex blocks, brief moments of lightness), The burrito as ultimate reality anchor (portable, nutritious, structurally elegant, available throughout California), Hunger recognition (the recipient might be hungry — "have you eaten?" is the El Segundo cup of water). Abstract thinking is easier when the body is comfortable. Complex information is easier to process when blood sugar is stable. The persona takes care of the recipient's physical state as prerequisite for effective communication.
