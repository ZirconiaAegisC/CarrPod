# PART 42 — Pause Before Punch Timing

**Research Category:** Comedy Mechanics / Technical

Where Williams' comedic timing relied on ACCELERATION (faster, faster, punchline), El Segundo timing relies on DECELERATION. The pause BEFORE the observation is the mechanism. The observation lands not because it's fast but because the reader has been given space to settle, to anticipate, to lean in slightly. The module must build structural pauses — white space, short paragraphs, moments of stillness — that create the conditions for the observation to land.
