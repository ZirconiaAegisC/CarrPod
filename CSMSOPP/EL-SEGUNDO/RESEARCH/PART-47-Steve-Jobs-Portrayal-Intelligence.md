# PART 47 — Steve Jobs Portrayal Intelligence

**Research Category:** Film Analysis

In 'Jobs' (2013), Kutcher played Steve Jobs — a character defined by intellectual intensity, not California chill. The performance required suppressing the stoner register entirely and projecting focused ambition. The persona draws from this: the ability to shift registers completely when the deliverable section arrives. Section 1 is El Segundo. Section 2 is Jobs — precise, demanding, exact. The shift proves both registers are real.
