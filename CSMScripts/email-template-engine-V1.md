# CSM Email Template Engine V1.0
## Purpose: Generate handcrafted outreach emails combining Baker Street (CSMSOPP000003) + Williams (CSMSOPP000001) heuristics

---

## TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

### Section 1: Header Block
```
# E[XXX] — [COMPANY/ORGANIZATION NAME]
## [ROLE/TITLE]
### CSMEmailOutgoing | [BATCH-XX] | July 2026
```

### Section 2: Meta-Block (Williams Heuristic)
```
**TO:** [Role], [Organization]
**FROM:** Jason Brodsky, Director of External Affairs | Carrington Storm Motors | Safe Pod Engineering Company
**REPLY-TO:** zirconia@aegisc.space
**SUBJECT:** [customized subject line]
```

### Section 3: Williams Meta-Commentary (~60-80 words, ROUTINE/ELEVATED severity as appropriate)
Pattern: "GOOOOOOOD [time of day], [SECTOR REFERENCE]! [warm acknowledgment of what they already do]. Now — [pivot: what we're offering]. [not a sales pitch — a data delivery / partnership invitation]."

### Section 4: Core Deliverable (Baker Street Adjuster precision)
- KEY FACTS: bulleted list with exact figures
- SECTOR-SPECIFIC: pain points addressed, CSM products mapped
- ATTACHMENT: specific briefing packet or compendium file
- NEXT STEP: venue, meeting, or call suggestion

### Section 5: Closing (Williams warmth)
Pattern: "[Sector-appropriate appreciation closing]. — Jason Brodsky"

### Section 6: Footer
```
*Email prepared under Baker Street Heuristic (CSMSOPP000003) + Williams Heuristic (CSMSOPP000001) communication standards | July 2026*
```
