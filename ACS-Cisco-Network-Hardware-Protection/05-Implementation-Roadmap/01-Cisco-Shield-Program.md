
# IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
## ACS-CISCO-NETWORK-HARDWARE-PROTECTION
### Cisco Shield Product Development Program

**CLASSIFICATION: COMMERCIAL-IN-CONFIDENCE**

**Phase 1 — Joint Engineering (Months 1-12):**
- Month 1: Kickoff at Cisco San Jose HQ. MXene material sample delivery. Joint engineering specification document.
- Months 2-4: PSU redesign for Catalyst 9300 (target platform). Prototype fabrication. MXene DC-link capacitor qualification testing — temperature cycling, ripple current, GIC injection.
- Months 5-8: PHY protection design — nanocrystalline common-mode choke integration. Signal integrity testing at 10GBASE-T (100 MHz).
- Months 9-12: Enclosure shielding validation. Prototype switch assembly. Full GIC injection testing at Cisco EMC qualification lab. First prototype Catalyst 9300 switch with complete Cisco Shield protection.

**Phase 2 — Product Qualification (Months 13-18):**
- IEEE 1613/IEC 61850-3 qualification testing
- MIL-STD-461G EMI testing
- UL/CSA safety certification
- NEBS Level 3 compliance
- Beta customer deployment (5-10 utility/defense customers)

**Phase 3 — Market Launch (Months 19-24):**
- Cisco Shield product line launch at Cisco Live 2028
- Initial product: Catalyst 9300 Shield (24/48-port PoE+)
- Follow-on: ISR 4400 Shield router, Nexus 9300 Shield data center switch
- Marketing: "When the grid fails, the network survives"

**Phase 4 — Portfolio Integration (Year 3+):**
- ISR 8000, Catalyst 9400/9600, Meraki MR/MT, IoT industrial switches
- GIC protection becomes standard feature (like PoE or stacking)
- Competitive moat established

**ACS Resource Commitment:** 8 MXene specialists, 4 power electronics engineers, 2 EMC test engineers, 2 certification specialists, 2 PMO staff.
