## 3. THE GIC VULNERABILITY IN NETWORKING

Cisco networking hardware has two primary GIC ingress paths:
1. **Power Supply:** Standard electrolytic capacitors in PSUs fail under quasi-DC overvoltage. MXene replacement solves this.
2. **Ethernet Ports:** UTP (unshielded twisted pair) cables carry common-mode GIC into PHY magnetics. Nanocrystalline common-mode chokes block this.

**Regulatory Fit:** IEEE 1613 (substation networking environmental standard), IEC 61850-3 (utility communications), and MIL-STD-461G (EMI) provide an established compliance framework for GIC-hardened networking — the engineering is incremental, not revolutionary.
