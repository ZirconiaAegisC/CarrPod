# Dossier Assembly and Distribution Guide

## Fleet Bridge Protection & Retrofit Program
### Version 1.0 — July 2026

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document provides complete instructions for the physical assembly, binding, labeling, and distribution of the Fleet Bridge Protection & Retrofit Program dossier. Adherence to these specifications ensures consistency across all distributed copies and presents the work at the standard of quality befitting a document prepared for T.Y. Lin International's executive leadership.

---

## 2. Printing Specifications

### 2.1 Paper Stock

All text pages shall be printed on high-quality bond paper with the following minimum specifications:

- **Weight:** 28 lb bond (105 gsm) minimum; 32 lb bond (120 gsm) preferred for executive copies
- **Finish:** Matte or satin finish — avoid high-gloss stock that creates glare under office lighting
- **Color:** Bright white (92+ brightness rating) for text pages; no off-white or cream stock
- **Page size:** 8.5 × 11 inches (US Letter), portrait orientation

### 2.2 Color Printing

The following elements require full-color printing:

- All diagrams, charts, and graphical figures embedded in the technical sections
- Bridge location maps and condition assessment heat maps
- Coating system selection flowcharts and decision matrices
- Lifecycle cost comparison bar charts and net-present-value curves
- Cathodic protection system schematic diagrams and anode layout plans
- Executive Summary section (full color throughout)
- Section divider pages (color tabs with section titles)

### 2.3 Duplex Printing

All pages shall be printed double-sided (duplex) to reduce binder thickness and paper consumption. Single-sided printing is permitted only for section divider pages and pages containing full-bleed diagrams where show-through would impair legibility.

### 2.4 Print Quality

- Minimum resolution: 600 dpi for text; 1200 dpi for diagrams and figures
- Laser printing strongly preferred; inkjet acceptable only if archival-quality pigment inks are used
- No staple holes, punch debris, or toner smudging permitted in final assembly

## 3. Binding Options

### 3.1 Primary Option: Three-Hole Punched in Presentation Folders

For maximum flexibility and ease of page replacement during future revisions:

- Use heavy-duty three-hole punch; verify alignment across all pages
- Insert pages into a navy blue or charcoal gray pressboard presentation folder
- Folder must have a die-cut business card slot on the inside front cover
- Folder must have a clear document window on the front cover aligned to display the title page
- Use 1-inch capacity Acco-style metal fasteners (prong fasteners) for paper retention
- Do not use plastic comb, wire, or coil binding for primary copies — these impede page replacement

### 3.2 Secondary Option: Spiral/Coil Binding

Permitted for secondary and field copies:

- Use black plastic coil, 1-inch diameter maximum
- Front cover: Clear acetate sheet, 10 mil minimum thickness
- Back cover: Black vinyl or heavy card stock, minimum 80 lb cover weight
- Ensure coil does not crimp or catch when opening flat

### 3.3 Tab Dividers

Each major section shall be separated by a printed tab divider:

- Use 8-tab or 10-tab pre-printed divider sets, letter size
- Tab labels: Executive Summary, Technical Foundation, Coating Systems, Cathodic Protection, Fleet Assessment, Lifecycle Cost Models, Implementation Roadmap, Regulatory & Funding, Risk & Resilience, Research Archive, Appendices
- Tab text: 12 pt bold, sans-serif font
- Divider pages themselves may be printed on 65 lb card stock for durability

### 5.1 Exterior Folder

- **Color:** Navy blue (preferred) or charcoal gray (alternate)
- **Material:** Heavy pressboard or leatherette-grained cover stock, minimum 20 pt thickness
- **Logo:** T.Y. Lin International logo shall be visible through the front cover document window, printed on the title page beneath the dossier title

## 7. Table of Contents

A comprehensive table of contents shall be placed at the front of each bound volume, immediately following the title page and preceding the Executive Summary. The table of contents shall:

- List every major section, subsection, figure, and table with page numbers
- Use hierarchical indentation to reflect document structure
- Be updated to reflect final pagination before printing
- Cross-reference USB key contents with a section reference note

## 8. Digital Distribution

### 8.1 Primary Digital Repository

All digital files shall be uploaded to a secure, access-controlled location:

- **Platform:** TYLin SharePoint or Box (as determined by IT policy)
- **Folder name:** "Fleet Bridge Protection Program — July 2026"
- **Access:** Restricted to named individuals approved by the Bridge Sector Manager
- **File format:** PDF/A-2 for archival documents; native formats (XLSX, DWG, PPTX) for editable assets
- **Password protection:** All PDFs shall be encrypted with a document-open password; passwords shall be distributed separately via secure channel
- **Version control:** All files shall carry version numbers in filenames (e.g., "01-Executive-Summary-v1.0.pdf")

### 8.2 Backup

- One complete digital copy stored on TYLin's encrypted backup infrastructure
- One additional copy retained by the document author for reference and future revision
- USB keys serve as standalone offline distribution (see Section 9)

---

## 9. USB Key Media

### 9.1 Hardware Specifications

- **Quantity:** Minimum five (5) USB drives
- **Capacity:** 32 GB minimum; 64 GB recommended
- **Interface:** USB 3.0 or higher
- **Form factor:** Low-profile preferred to prevent damage when inserted in binders
- **Brand:** SanDisk, Kingston, or Samsung — no generic or promotional-grade drives

### 9.2 Loading Instructions

- Format each drive as exFAT (cross-platform compatibility)
- Create directory structure matching the printed dossier (see USB Master Manifest for details)
- Copy all PDF files plus supplementary data files
- Run SHA-256 checksums on each file and include the checksum manifest in the root directory
- Eject safely and verify content on a separate machine before sealing

### 9.3 Labeling

- Each USB drive shall be physically labeled with a durable adhesive label or laser-etched marking
- Label text: "TYLIN-FBP-2026-07 — Copy [N] of 5"
- Drives shall be sealed in individual anti-static bags before placement in binder pockets

## 10. Quality Control Checklist

Before any copy leaves the assembly area, verify the following:

- [ ] All pages present and in correct sequence
- [ ] Page numbers continuous and matching table of contents
- [ ] Color pages printed in color (no monochrome substitutions)
- [ ] No missing, torn, misaligned, or blank pages
- [ ] Binding secure; pages turn freely without binding
- [ ] Tab dividers placed at correct section breaks
- [ ] Cover label and spine label correctly oriented and affixed
- [ ] USB key content verified by checksum comparison
- [ ] USB key inserted in binder pocket or attached securely
- [ ] Digital copies uploaded to SharePoint/Box and access tested

## 11. Revisions and Updates

This dossier is a living document. Future revisions shall follow a controlled update process:

- Version numbering: Major.Minor format (1.0, 1.1, 2.0, etc.)
- Major revisions (new sections, methodology changes): version integer increment
- Minor revisions (errata, updated references, formatting): decimal increment
- All recipients shall receive notification of updates and shall replace superseded pages or volumes
- A revision history log shall be maintained as the final page of each volume
