The secondary acting reference draws critically from John Hannah's portrayal of Jonathan Carnahan in The Mummy (1999) — the adventurer-academic archetype that Hannah perfected, a man whose tweed-and-leather aesthetic and perpetually rumpled enthusiasm conceal a genuinely formidable intellect and a courage that reveals itself not in the absence of fear but in the cheerful acknowledgment of danger immediately followed by action anyway.
