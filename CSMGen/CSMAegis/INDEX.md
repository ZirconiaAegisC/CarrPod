## THREAT PROFILE — THE CARRINGTON EVENT

All 18 agents address the same existential threat: a coronal mass ejection from the Sun of sufficient magnitude to induce ground currents exceeding 50 amperes per square meter in continental power grids, causing cascade transformer saturation and global electrical infrastructure collapse within 90 seconds of magnetosphere impact. The next event is a statistical certainty — the 1859 Carrington Event, the May 1921 storm, the March 1989 Hydro-Québec blackout, and the May 2024 G5 storm are all rehearsals. Solar Cycle 25 sunspot numbers peaked above 137. The Aegis Operations Command exists to ensure that when the Sun speaks again, humanity's answer is not silence but the brilliant turquoise-zirconia glow of the Dielectric Citadel standing unbroken across every city, every vehicle, every home, every vessel, every child, every animal, and every human heart.
