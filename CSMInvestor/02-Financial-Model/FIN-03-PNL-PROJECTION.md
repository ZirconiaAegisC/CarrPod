## Executive Summary: The Path to Profitability

Carrington Storm Motors is projected to achieve EBITDA breakeven in Q3 of FY2029 (Year 3), approximately 30 months after Series A close. Net income turns positive in Q1 of FY2030 (Year 4). The company generates cumulative revenue of $1.94B over five years with cumulative net income of $233.3M. By Year 5, net margin reaches 14.1% ‚Äî competitive with mature defense and industrial technology companies.

**Williams Heuristic:** We lose money building the machine. The machine starts printing money in Year 3. By Year 5, it's a money cannon. This is the standard arc for capital-intensive industrial technology companies: SpaceX lost money for a decade; Tesla, for 17 years; Palantir, for 18 years. CSM reaches profitability in Year 3 because we're selling into an existing procurement pipeline (governments and utilities already buy grid equipment) with a product that demonstrably solves an acknowledged problem. We're not creating demand; we're fulfilling it.
