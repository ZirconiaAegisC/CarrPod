## 10.0 PROTONIC COMMUNICATION NETWORK (1 Product)

**Lead Agent: Agent Orin Hale**

A planet-scale mesh network using ionic current (H⁺, Li⁺, Na⁺) as the signal carrier in solid-state protonic conductors. Total immunity to electromagnetic disruption because the signal carrier — protons — is 1,836× heavier than electrons and responds to EM fields at fundamentally different frequencies.

**System architecture:**
- **Node hardware:** Ionic conductivity polymer transducer, microcontroller, LoRa radio for hybrid fallback, solar/battery power
- **Network topology:** Self-organizing mesh, up to 65,536 nodes per network segment
- **Data rate:** 1–100 kbps — sufficient for text messaging, telemetry, emergency alerts, and low-bitrate voice codec
- **Range:** 10–30 km per node (LoRa hybrid); pure protonic range scales with ionic conductor development
- **Deployment:** Rapid — nodes are self-configuring; no infrastructure required beyond the nodes themselves
