## 3.0 PILOT PROGRAM PROPOSALS

### Pilot 1: Substation Hardening (DOE/Utility Partnership)

**Scope:** Retrofit 3–5 high-voltage substations in high-GIC-risk regions with full Dielectric Citadel Protocol: MXene wall shielding (Aegis-C S3), CNT neutral-ground blocking, ceramic bearing isolation on bus supports, geopolymer foundation pads.

**Partners:** Department of Energy, 1–2 investor-owned utilities, NERC

**Timeline:** 18 months (6 months assessment + 12 months installation and monitoring)

**Budget:** $8–12M (DOE funded)

**Success metric:** >90% GIC reduction measured at transformer neutrals during geomagnetic storm events

### Pilot 2: Aegis Home New Construction (Builder Partnership)

**Scope:** Build 50–100 homes to full Aegis Home specification in a high-GIC-risk region (Minnesota/Wisconsin/Upper Peninsula Michigan). Partner with a national homebuilder for production integration, a regional insurer for premium reduction program, and a local utility for grid integration.

**Partners:** National homebuilder, regional P&C insurer, local electric utility

**Timeline:** 24 months (6 months design integration + 18 months construction and monitoring)

**Budget:** $3–5M (split between CSM, builder, and insurer)

**Success metric:** 96%+ GIC reduction measured; 15%+ insurance premium reduction achieved; resale value premium demonstrated

### Pilot 3: AMETHYST Prototype Vessel (Maritime)

**Scope:** Build one AMETHYST-Class amphibious vehicle to full CSM specification. Conduct water trials, amphibious transition testing, EM immunity validation, and sea-keeping assessment.

**Partners:** Shipyard, US Coast Guard (regulatory), potential defense customer

**Timeline:** 24 months (8 months fabrication + 16 months testing and certification)

**Budget:** $3–5M (CSM funded from Series A)

**Success metric:** All F2-xxx specification requirements met; amphibious transition in <60 seconds; >94% GIC reduction vs. steel-hull equivalent

### Pilot 4: Insurance Premium Reduction Pilot

**Scope:** Partner with a major P&C insurer to offer 15% premium reduction on Aegis Home-certified properties. Deploy Pocket-Watch-X monitors and FEATHER nodes to measure actual GIC exposure. Validate the premium reduction model with real data.

**Partners:** Major P&C insurer, reinsurance broker, catastrophe modeler

**Timeline:** 18 months

**Budget:** $2–3M (split between CSM and insurer)

**Success metric:** 500+ properties enrolled; 15% premium reduction demonstrated; loss ratio on enrolled properties ≤ unhardened properties
