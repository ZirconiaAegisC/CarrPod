## 2.0 THE PROBLEM (One Paragraph)

A Carrington-class coronal mass ejection — a solar event that has occurred approximately every 100–150 years throughout recorded history, most recently in 1859 (the Carrington Event), 1921 (the New York Railroad Storm), and 1989 (Hydro-Québec blackout, a much smaller precursor) — induces geomagnetic current in all long conductive structures on Earth's surface. This quasi-DC current saturates high-voltage transformer cores, causing rapid internal heating, insulation degradation, and catastrophic failure. A modern Carrington event would destroy 300+ large power transformers in the United States alone. These transformers have a 12–24 month manufacturing lead time and a total domestic production capacity of approximately 150 units per year. Replacement of 300+ simultaneously destroyed units would require 5–8 years. During that period: no reliable grid power. No water treatment. No food refrigeration. No hospital equipment beyond generator fuel duration. No telecommunications. No fuel pumping. No financial transactions. The cascading economic loss is modeled by Lloyd's of London at $2.0–$6.0 trillion, which is approximately 3× the total capital of the global reinsurance industry. The insurance sector would experience a cascading insolvency event. This is not speculation. It is actuarial math, confirmed by peer-reviewed physics, that the insurance industry has been explicitly warned about since at least 2013.

## 3.0 THE SOLUTION (One Paragraph)

The Dielectric Citadel Protocol replaces conductive materials with dielectric composites at every scale — from the atomic (MXene shielding films at 45μm, 92 dB attenuation) to the architectural (BFRP composite structural elements, geopolymer concrete foundations, CNT-doped polymer wiring, ceramic bearings, protonic communications). It does not build a Faraday cage around civilization. It makes civilization itself the shield. A residential structure built to Aegis Home specifications experiences 96.7% less geomagnetically induced current than a conventional structure. A BFRP-hulled vessel (Charlemagne-Class Fleet) experiences 94.0% less. A substation retrofitted with the full Dielectric Citadel Protocol becomes effectively transparent to geomagnetic fields — the current has no conductive path to follow. The materials are characterized, costed, and validated. The products are designed. The manufacturing specifications are written. The insurance integration framework is drafted. The diplomatic channels are open in 200 countries. **This is not a concept seeking funding to explore. This is a completed architecture seeking funding to manufacture.**
