This document maps all 50+ Carrington Storm Motors products from their current development state through prototype, testing, certification, pilot production, and volume manufacturing across a 5-year horizon. The timeline is organized in Gantt-style narrative format with quarterly milestones, critical path analysis, resource requirements, and dependency chains. Products are grouped into five parallel development streams: (I) Core Materials & Shielding, (II) Aegis Home Residential Protection, (III) Vehicle & Vessel Hardening, (IV) Robotics & Autonomous Systems, and (V) Communications & Software. Each stream has independent critical paths but shares common dependencies on MXene supply chain maturation, BFRP manufacturing scale-up, and testing facility availability.
