## PARTNERSHIP PHILOSOPHY

Carrington Storm Motors cannot build the Dielectric Citadel alone. The electromagnetic hardening of civilization requires a coalition: insurance carriers to price the risk reduction and fund the build-out; electric utilities to deploy infrastructure products; construction firms to install Aegis Homes at scale; maritime operators to retrofit the Charlemagne Fleet; defense contractors to integrate Phantom robots and hardened C4I; materials suppliers to provide MXene precursors, basalt fiber, and ceramic powders; and research universities to independently validate every claim. CSM's partnership strategy is tiered: Tier 1 (Founding Partners — equity/revenue share relationships), Tier 2 (Strategic Partners — long-term supply/purchase agreements), Tier 3 (Implementation Partners — project-specific MOUs). This document maps the engagement strategy for each partner category.
