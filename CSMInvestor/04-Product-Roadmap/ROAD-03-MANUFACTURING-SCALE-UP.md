## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Carrington Storm Motors currently operates at laboratory and prototype scale: 50 g/day MXene production, 100 m/day BFRP pultrusion (batch), and individual product assembly at the CSMFAB El Segundo facility (8,000 sq ft). This document defines the manufacturing scale-up pathway through three phases: (I) Pilot Production Facility (2027–2028), (II) Volume Manufacturing Plant (2029–2030), and (III) Global Supply Chain Integration (2031+). The manufacturing strategy is organized around five core material platforms — MXene, BFRP composites, ceramics (ZrB₂-SiC, Si₃N₄, ZrO₂), geopolymer concrete, and piezoelectric elements — that feed all 50+ CSM products through shared manufacturing cells and a common BOM-REGISTRY.
