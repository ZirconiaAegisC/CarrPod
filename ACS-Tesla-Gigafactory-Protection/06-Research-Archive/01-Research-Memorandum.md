
# RESEARCH ARCHIVE
## ACS-TESLA-GIGAFACTORY-PROTECTION

## 1. SPACE WEATHER AND GIC PHYSICS

**Carrington, R.C. (1859).** "Description of a Singular Appearance seen in the Sun on September 1, 1859." Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society. The original observation linking solar flares to geomagnetic disturbances.

**Kappenman, J.G. (2010).** "Geomagnetic Storms and Their Impacts on the U.S. Power Grid." Meta-R-319 Report for Oak Ridge National Laboratory. The foundational modern assessment of GIC risk to U.S. infrastructure. Estimated 130 million people without power for years in worst-case scenario.

**NERC (2024).** "Long-Term Reliability Assessment." North American Electric Reliability Corporation. Identifies GMD events as High-Impact Low-Frequency threats requiring transmission planner mitigation.

**EPRI (2024).** "GMD Mitigation Guidelines Update." Electric Power Research Institute. Validates neutral-blocking capacitor technology for transformer protection. Field test results from Idaho National Laboratory.

**Royal Academy of Engineering (2013).** "Extreme Space Weather: Impacts on Engineered Systems and Infrastructure." UK government-commissioned assessment of GIC risk to the UK National Grid, including transformer thermal damage models.

**FERC Order 830 (2016).** Federal Energy Regulatory Commission order requiring NERC to develop and implement GMD reliability standards. Resulted in TPL-007 series.

## 2. MANUFACTURING AND ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS

**IEC 61000-2-9.** Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) — Description of HEMP environment — Radiated disturbance. Standards framework for industrial power quality during electromagnetic disturbance.

**IEEE Std C57.12.00.** Standard for General Requirements for Liquid-Immersed Distribution, Power, and Regulating Transformers. Design basis for transformer GIC tolerance.

**IEEE 519-2022.** Recommended Practice and Requirements for Harmonic Control in Electric Power Systems. Harmonic limits applicable to formation line power quality.

**IEC 61557-8.** Electrical safety in low voltage distribution systems up to 1000 V AC and 1500 V DC. Insulation monitoring standards for industrial equipment.

## 3. BATTERY MANUFACTURING

**Tesla, Inc. (2020).** "Battery Day Presentation." Technical disclosures on 4680 cell design, dry electrode process, and tabless architecture.

**Tesla, Inc. (2023).** "Investor Day — Master Plan Part 3." Infrastructure resilience commitment. Energy storage deployment metrics.

**Ahmed, S. et al. (2017).** "Energy impact of cathode drying and solvent recovery during lithium-ion battery manufacturing." Journal of Power Sources. Documents energy intensity and process sensitivity of electrode manufacturing.

**Kwade, A. et al. (2018).** "Current status and challenges for automotive battery production technologies." Nature Energy. Process sensitivity analysis for formation cycling and coating tolerances.

## 4. CSM INTERNAL RESEARCH

**CSM Research Division (2011-2026).** "GIC Physics and Infrastructure Protection." CSM Document Series GIC-001 through GIC-6,302. Foundation of ACS technical approach.

**CSM MXene Laboratory (2018-2026).** "Ti3C2Tx MXene Synthesis, Characterization, and Application to Electromagnetic Protection." Internal technical reports MX-1001 through MX-2480.

**CSM Insurance Division (2019-2026).** "Infrastructure Resilience Underwriting Dossiers." 20+ dossiers filed with Lloyd's of London, Munich Re, Swiss Re, and AIG, establishing actuarial basis for GIC protection benefits.

## 5. REGULATORY AND POLICY

**NERC TPL-007-4.** Transmission System Planned Performance for Geomagnetic Disturbance Events. Current standard requiring GMD vulnerability assessment by transmission planners.

**Executive Order 13865 (2019).** "Coordinating National Resilience to Electromagnetic Pulses." U.S. executive order addressing EMP and GMD threats to critical infrastructure.

**German IT-Sicherheitsgesetz 2.0 (2021).** Extended KRITIS (critical infrastructure) requirements including energy sector.

**State Council of the PRC (2022).** National Emergency Response Plan for Space Weather Events. China's framework for space weather infrastructure protection.
