### G2: Document Selection Logic
When crafting mail campaigns:

1. **Identify relevant acts:** Match the recipient's industry/sector to the appropriate legal act(s).
   - Insurance companies → All acts, with emphasis on Acts 01, 05, and 12
   - Clean energy companies → Acts 03, 07, 11, 12
   - Manufacturing companies → Acts 02, 04, 07
   - Workforce/training organizations → Act 11
   - Grid operators/utilities → Act 12 and Act 01 (Title II)
   - Bridge/infrastructure → Act 05 and Act 01 (Title I)
   - Critical minerals/mining → Act 02
   - Semiconductor industry → Act 11 (Title VII)
   - Nuclear industry → Act 03 and Act 11 (Title VI)
   - Carbon removal/DAC → Act 07
   - Concrete/cement/steel → Act 07 (Title IV)
   - Energy storage → Act 12

2. **Reference dossier research:** Each mail should cite specific company dossiers and compendium research to demonstrate domain expertise.

3. **Tailor messaging:** Focus on the titles and sections most relevant to the recipient's business.
