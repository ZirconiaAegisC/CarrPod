## 1.0 Strategic Context

PPG's digital transformation represents a deliberate shift from a product-centric coatings supplier to an integrated service-and-solutions provider. The centerpiece of this strategy is the PPG LINQ and MOONWALK digital ecosystem, which links hardware, software, cloud-based analytics, and color-science algorithms into a unified platform across automotive refinish and industrial finishing markets. As of Q1 2026, PPG reported **3,300+ installations** globally.

## 2.0 PPG LINQ Ecosystem Architecture

### 2.1 System Components

| Component | Function | Deployment |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| PPG LINQ Color | Cloud-connected spectrophotometer and color database | Body shops, fleet refinishers |
| PPG LINQ Mix | Automated toner dispensing, formulation retrieval, and mixing module | Body shops, industrial finishers |
| PPG LINQ Inventory | RFID- and barcode-enabled stock management with auto-replenishment | Body shops, distributors |
| PPG LINQ Analytics | Dashboard and reporting suite tracking consumption, waste, cycle time | Multi-site operators |
| PPG MOONWALK | Automated mixing machine—robotic precision dispensing and tinting | High-throughput body shops |
| PPG LINQ End-to-End | Full-suite integration linking estimation, color retrieval, mixing, application, and invoicing | Enterprise body-shop networks |

### 2.2 Technical Workflow

The digital workflow operates as follows:

1. **Vehicle intake:** Damage assessment and repair estimate generated
2. **Color identification:** LINQ Color spectrophotometer captures vehicle surface reflectance data; algorithm matches against PPG's global color database of 2+ million formulations
3. **Formulation retrieval:** Cloud-based database returns precise toner weights for the matched variant (including alternates for variants caused by OEM assembly-plant paint-line variation)
4. **Automated dispensing:** MOONWALK or LINQ Mix module dispenses toners to 0.1-gram precision, eliminating manual weighing errors
5. **Application:** Painter applies accurately formulated coating with integrated process documentation
6. **Data capture:** Consumption, waste, cycle-time, and cost data uploaded for analytics and KPI tracking

### 2.3 MOONWALK Differentiation

MOONWALK is PPG's proprietary automated mixing machine, designed and manufactured in-house. Key specifications:

- Dispensing accuracy: ±0.1 g
- Mixing capacity: up to 90 formulations per day
- Footprint: Compact benchtop unit, approximately 0.5 m²
- Toner compatibility: PPG ENVIROBASE High Performance waterborne, Deltron, Nexa Autocolor, and commercial-vehicle product lines
- Connectivity: Real-time cloud synchronization with PPG color database

MOONWALK eliminates human error in the tinting process—historically the largest source of color mismatch and rework in automotive refinish. By converting tinting from an artisanal, technician-dependent process into a precision-automated operation, PPG reduces per-repair material consumption by **15–25%** and rework rates by measurable margins.

## 3.0 Commercial Model Transformation

The digital ecosystem shifts PPG's value proposition:

| Traditional Model | Digital Model |
|------------------|--------------|
| Transactional sale of toners, clears, ancillaries | Integrated solution including hardware, software, consumables, and data services |
| Price-per-liter competition | Total-cost-of-repair optimization, driving share-of-wallet |
| Fragmented ordering across distributors | Single-platform procurement with automated replenishment |
| No operational data transparency | Real-time KPI dashboards enabling multi-site benchmarking |
| Color-matching dependent on painter skill | Algorithmic matching with MOONWALK precision dispensing |

This model deepens customer switching costs—a body shop or MSO (multi-shop operator) that has integrated LINQ/MOONWALK into its estimating, color-matching, mixing, and inventory workflows faces significant operational disruption to change coating suppliers.

## 4.0 Industrial & Commercial Applications

Beyond automotive refinish, PPG is extending digital service models into:

### 4.1 Aerospace

The PPG AEROSPACE Managed Services program incorporates digital inventory management, consumption analytics, and process documentation at airline and MRO facilities. Integration with airline maintenance IT systems (TRAX, AMOS, SAP MRO) enables automated reorder triggers and shelf-life management for time-sensitive aerospace coatings and sealants.

### 4.2 Industrial Finishing

Digital color-management and formulation-retrieval tools are deployed in coil-coating lines, wood-finishing operations, and general industrial paint lines, enabling rapid product changeover and first-run color accuracy for OEM manufacturing.

### 4.3 Architectural

Pro-painter and contractor digital tools encompass color visualization (PPG PAINTS Visualizer), project estimation, and digital tinting at owned-store and independent dealer points of sale.

## 5.0 Competitive Landscape

Digital service-model competition in coatings is nascent but intensifying:

| Competitor | Digital Platform | Status |
|------------|-----------------|--------|

| BASF | Refinity digital platform | Body-shop process digitization |

PPG's first-mover advantage is material—with 3,300+ installations, the installed base creates data-network effects: each additional spectrophotometer reading enriches the color database, and each additional MOONWALK deployment generates operational benchmarks that strengthen the analytics proposition for multi-site enterprise customers.

## 6.0 Data Monetization Potential

The digital ecosystem generates structured data across the refinish value chain:

- **Formulation consumption data** by repair type, vehicle make/model, and geographic region
- **Cycle-time benchmarks** enabling predictive staffing and capacity planning
- **Waste analytics** supporting sustainability reporting and regulatory compliance
- **Color-trend analysis** feeding OEM color-styling studios and aftermarket demand forecasting

These data streams are not yet a discrete revenue line item but represent latent monetization potential through analytics subscriptions, benchmark reports, and integration with insurance-claims and fleet-management platforms.

## 7.0 Engagement Relevance

PPG's digital service model is a structural competitive differentiator that increases customer retention, raises switching costs, and captures operational data with latent monetization value. The installed base of 3,300+ units, combined with MOONWALK's precision-automation value proposition, positions PPG for sustained share gains in automotive refinish and adjacent industrial finishing markets.
