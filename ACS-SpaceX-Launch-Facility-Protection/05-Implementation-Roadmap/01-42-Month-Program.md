
# IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
## ACS-SPACEX-LAUNCH-FACILITY-PROTECTION
### 42-Month Launch Infrastructure Protection Program

**CLASSIFICATION: COMMERCIAL-IN-CONFIDENCE**

| Phase | Duration | Key Activities |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Phase 0 — 4-site survey | Months 1-3 | KSC, Vandenberg, Starbase, Hawthorne GIC survey. Launch pad cable routing analysis. Starlink ground station inventory and latitude prioritization. |
| Phase 1 — KSC/LC-39A pilot | Months 4-12 | Pad substation NBC (2 transformers). Outdoor cable tray shielding. FTS power hardening. Protonic link to Hawthorne. |
| Phase 2 — All pads | Months 12-24 | SLC-40 (CCSFS), SLC-4E (Vandenberg), Starbase pad hardening. Standardized pad protection kit developed during KSC pilot. |
| Phase 3 — Mission control | Months 12-18 | Hawthorne and KSC LCC substation NBCs. Flight computer power conditioning. |
| Phase 4 — Starlink 100+ ground stations | Months 18-42 | Priority: 40°N+ stations first. Kit manufacturing ramp-up. Remote installation teams. |
| Phase 5 — Protonic network | Months 12-18 | 4-node network (Hawthorne, KSC, Vandenberg, Starbase). |

**Range Safety Coordination:** All modifications to launch pad electrical infrastructure require USSF range safety review. ACS regulatory specialists coordinate with 45th Space Wing (Cape Canaveral) and 30th Space Wing (Vandenberg) for approval of power system modifications affecting FTS and range safety systems.

**Starbase Considerations:** Boca Chica launch site is under active development for Starship/Super Heavy. GIC protection should be integrated into the design before the orbital launch mount reaches full operational status — retrofitting after operational start is more expensive and requires launch campaign interruptions.

**Key Milestones:**
- Month 3: Site surveys complete. FTS modification approval pathway validated.
- Month 12: KSC/LC-39A fully protected — first GIC-hardened launch pad in history.
- Month 24: All four primary pads hardened.
- Month 42: Full program delivery including 100+ Starlink ground stations.
