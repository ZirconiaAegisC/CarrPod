# M&A STRATEGY: GUIDETECH ACQUISITION (NOVEMBER 2025)

## TRANSACTION OVERVIEW

On or about November 2025, Palladyne AI completed the acquisition of GuideTech LLC, a
privately held avionics and loitering munition systems developer. The transaction was valued
at approximately $25 million plus an earnout structure, details of which remain partially
undisclosed in public filings beyond references to performance-based consideration. The
acquisition was funded through a combination of cash reserves and equity consideration,
consistent with Palladyne's stated capital allocation strategy for defense vertical expansion.

## STRATEGIC RATIONALE

GuideTech brought three critical capabilities into Palladyne that the company lacked
organically:

1. **Loitering Munition IP** — The Gremlin-X loitering munition platform represents a
   mature, flight-tested system in the man-portable category. Unlike startups entering
   the loitering munition space from a cold start, GuideTech arrived with existing
   hardware, flight heritage, and manufacturing tooling.

2. **Avionics Competency** — GuideTech's miniaturized avionics packages, including flight
   control computers, guidance systems, and communication links, form the technological
   core of the Gremlin-X and are applicable across Palladyne's broader autonomous systems
   portfolio. This avionics IP is dual-use: it serves both GuideTech's own munitions and
   can be integrated into third-party UAS platforms.

3. **Engineering Workforce** — GuideTech's technical team provided Palladyne with a cadre
   of aerospace engineers with direct experience in weapons system integration, flight
   testing, and MIL-STD qualification. This talent pool is scarce and difficult to
   assemble organically, particularly for a company transitioning from industrial
   robotics.

## THE GREMLIN-X PLATFORM

The Gremlin-X is positioned in the tactical loitering munition segment, competing with
AeroVironment's Switchblade series, UVision's Hero family, and other man-portable systems.
Key characteristics include:

- Man-portable form factor with tube or rail launch
- Electro-optical/infrared (EO/IR) seeker for terminal guidance
- Loitering endurance suitable for tactical reconnaissance and precision strike
- Modular warhead options for anti-personnel and light anti-materiel effects
- Integrated with Palladyne's SwarmOS for multi-unit autonomous coordination

Commercial traction for the Gremlin-X is expected to emerge through both direct U.S. DoD
procurement channels and foreign military sales (FMS) pathways, particularly to customers
operating under the IAI partnership umbrella.

## INTEGRATION STATUS

As of mid-2026, the GuideTech integration appears substantially complete. The GuideTech
brand has been subsumed under Palladyne Defense, with the Gremlin-X serving as the
flagship internally-developed loitering munition alongside the IAI-licensed HARPY/HAROP
family. The avionics group continues to operate as a horizontal capability serving both
the munitions product line and the broader SwarmOS/Palladyne Pilot ecosystem.

## FINANCIAL IMPACT

GuideTech contributed an undisclosed portion of Palladyne's FY2026 guided revenue of
$24-27 million. The earnout structure suggests a meaningful portion of GuideTech's
valuation was contingent on achieving specific revenue or program milestones, aligning
seller incentives with Palladyne's execution.
