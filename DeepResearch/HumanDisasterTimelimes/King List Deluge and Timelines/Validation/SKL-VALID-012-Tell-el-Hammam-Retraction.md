## 2. Reasons for the Tell el-Hammam Retraction

The 2025 retraction of the Tell el-Hammam airburst paper by *Scientific Reports*
(Nature Publishing Group) was based on:

1. **Data manipulation:** Evidence that excavation photographs had been digitally
   altered to exaggerate impact features
2. **Conflict of interest:** Undisclosed commercial relationships between authors and
   organizations promoting the site's biblical identification
3. **Methodological concerns:** Independent reviewers could not replicate
   stratigraphic claims using the published data
