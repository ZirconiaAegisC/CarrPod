# Astroscale — Intellectual Property Portfolio

## Patent Strategy Overview

Astroscale has built an IP portfolio concentrated on the core technologies
that differentiate its on-orbit servicing platform. As of Q1 2026, the
company holds or has filed for **over 80 patent families** across Japan,
the United States, Europe, and key spacefaring jurisdictions.

## IP Portfolio Value

Astroscale's IP portfolio creates a **defensive moat** in on-orbit
servicing. Competitors seeking to offer similar services must either license
Astroscale's technology, develop workaround solutions (at significant time
and cost), or accept higher operational risk. The portfolio is also a
potential **licensing revenue stream** as the market matures and new
entrants seek to shortcut development.

## Trade Secret Protection

In addition to patents, Astroscale protects critical know-how as trade
secrets:
- Proprietary electric thruster control algorithms.
- Internal EMI/EMC design practices for proximity operations.
- Supplier qualification processes and acceptance test procedures.
- Capture success prediction models built from ADRAS-J flight data.
