## Capital Raising

In May 2026, Astroscale executed a combined **¥30.6 billion** capital raise
consisting of:

- **Convertible Bonds (CB):** ¥18.0B, zero coupon, 5-year maturity, conversion
  premium set at 125% of reference share price.
- **Equity Placement:** ¥12.6B through a third-party allotment to institutional
  investors including Japanese banks, European space-focused funds, and a
  strategic allocation to Mitsubishi Electric.

Proceeds are earmarked for:
1. ADRAS-J2 mission execution and launch costs (¥8.0B)
2. ELSA-M manufacturing and Isar Aerospace Spectrum launch (¥6.0B)
3. New cleanroom and testing facility at Tsukuba Space Center (¥7.0B)
4. APS-R prototype development for US Space Force (¥4.0B)
5. Working capital and debt service reserve (¥5.6B)

## Facilities Investment

¥7.0 billion was allocated in FY2026 for capital expenditures, primarily for a
new satellite integration facility at the Tsukuba Space Center in Ibaraki
Prefecture. The facility will house twin ISO Class 7 cleanrooms capable of
simultaneous assembly of two full-size servicing spacecraft, plus a
hardware-in-the-loop RPO simulation laboratory.
