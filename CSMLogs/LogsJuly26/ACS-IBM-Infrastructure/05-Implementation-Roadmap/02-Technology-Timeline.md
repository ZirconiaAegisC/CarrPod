# IBM Technology Timeline: 2026–2035

**Document Classification:** ACS Internal — Strategic Planning
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** July 2026

---

## Overview

This timeline synthesizes IBM's published and projected technology milestones across five platform domains: Quantum Computing, System z Mainframe, AI & Data Platforms, Red Hat OpenShift, and Semiconductor & Packaging. Dates represent IBM's publicly communicated targets from investor presentations, product announcements, and published research roadmaps as of Q2 2026.
