### 10.3.2 Starlink Standalone Valuation

Starlink alone, at a $1.2 trillion implied enterprise value, represents nearly 52% of total SpaceX valuation. Key DCF drivers:

- **Revenue CAGR FY2025–FY2033:** 18%
- **Terminal growth rate:** 3.5%
- **Terminal EBITDA margin:** 35%
- **Discount rate (WACC):** 11% (elevated vs. terrestrial telcos to reflect LEO competition and spectrum risk)

Sensitivity: Each 10M subscriber above 50M at terminal steady state adds approximately $180B to Starlink standalone valuation at current ARPU. A 10% ARPU decline would reduce valuation by approximately $160B.

### 10.3.3 Terafab / AI Compute Option Value

The $500B valuation attribution for AI Compute assumes a 30% probability-weighted option scenario:

| Scenario | Probability | Value ($B) | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full success (1M satellites, dominant compute platform) | 30% | $1,500 | Terafab capex on budget; orbital compute captures 20% of global AI inference market |
| Moderate success (limited deployment) | 40% | $200 | 100K satellites; niche enterprise + Grok internal demand |
| Failure / abandonment | 30% | $0 | Terafab cost overrun; orbital compute uncompetitive vs. terrestrial |

Probability-weighted value: (0.3 × $1,500) + (0.4 × $200) + (0.3 × $0) = $530B, rounded to $500B.
