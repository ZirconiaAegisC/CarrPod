===============================================================================
  DOSSIER ARCHITECTURE
===============================================================================

The dossier is organized across five strategic framework pillars:

  PILLAR I    Global Gigafactory Network & Vehicle Manufacturing
              - 5 operational vehicle assembly facilities
              - 2.3M+ combined nominal annual capacity
              - Cybercab, Tesla Semi: H2 2026 volume production

  PILLAR II   Energy Storage, Solar & Grid Infrastructure
              - 80 GWh Megapack annual capacity
              - >6 GWh Powerwall cumulative deployments
              - >1,000,000 Powerwalls in service; 89K+ VPP events

  PILLAR III  AI, FSD & Autonomous Platform
              - 1.28M active FSD subscriptions
              - Cortex 2 AI training supercomputer
              - End-to-end neural network architecture (FSD v14)

  PILLAR IV   Optimus Robotics & Future Manufacturing
              - Gen 3 hardware; 1M/yr line (Fremont), 10M/yr (Texas)
              - 28 custom-designed actuators
              - Shared AI stack with FSD

  PILLAR V    Supercharger & Service Fleet Infrastructure
              - 8,463 stations; 79,918 connectors (+19% YoY)
              - V4 cabinets: 3x power density
              - NACS adopted by >15 major automakers
