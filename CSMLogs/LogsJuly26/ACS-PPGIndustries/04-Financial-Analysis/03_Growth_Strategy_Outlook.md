## 2.0 Growth Algorithm Decomposition

PPG's long-term organic growth framework comprises:

### 2.1 Volume Growth (+1–3% secular baseline)

PPG targets volume growth modestly above industrial-production indices through:

- **Technology-driven share capture** in protective & marine (12 consecutive quarters of volume growth), aerospace, and packaging
- **Emerging-market penetration** — particularly China, India, Southeast Asia, and Mexico
- **Channel expansion** — professional contractor, owned-store, and digital-direct models in architectural

### 2.2 Price/Mix Enhancement (+0.5–1.5%)

- Formula-based pricing in automotive OEM
- Value-based pricing in aerospace, protective & marine, and refinish
- Mix shift toward higher-value technology products (chromate-free aerospace, non-BPA packaging, polysiloxane protective topcoats, powder coatings, UV-curable systems)

### 2.3 Margin Expansion (+20–30 bps annually)

- Raw-material cycle normalization
- Manufacturing productivity (automation, yield improvement, energy efficiency)
- Restructuring and footprint optimization
- Favorable segment mix (Performance Coatings outgrowing Industrial and Architectural)

### 2.4 Capital Deployment (+1–3% EPS from buybacks)

- Ongoing share repurchases under $1.9 billion authorization
- Bolt-on M&A in technology-intensive sub-segments
- Dividend growth in line with earnings

## 3.0 Secular Growth Vectors

### 3.1 Aerospace Supercycle

The combination of narrowbody production-rate acceleration (Airbus A320neo toward rate 75/month, 737 MAX recovery toward rate 50+/month), widebody recovery (A350, 787), military modernization, and deferred MRO demand creates a multi-year growth trajectory. PPG's $315 million aerospace backlog and chromate-free technology position the company to capture disproportionate value as qualification cycles mature.

**Estimated Revenue CAGR (2025–2028):** 8–12%

### 3.2 Non-BPA Packaging Conversion

The regulatory-driven conversion of global metal-packaging coating capacity from BPA-based epoxy to next-generation non-BPA systems (PPG INNOVEL, PPG HOBA) represents a decade-scale, ~$2+ billion global opportunity. PPG's first-mover position with major can-makers and brand-owner approvals provides competitive insulation. The EU regulatory timeline (2025–2028 enforcement) creates near-term urgency.

**Estimated Revenue CAGR (2025–2028):** 10–15%

### 3.3 Protective & Marine Infrastructure

Global energy, infrastructure, and marine-investment cycles support sustained protective-coatings demand. PPG's 12-quarter volume-growth streak and Asia Pacific marine share gains suggest structural market-share capture, not merely cyclical recovery. Intumescent fire protection (STEELGUARD) and fouling-release (SIGMAGLIDE) technologies address secular regulatory drivers (building codes, IMO CII/EEXI).

**Estimated Revenue CAGR (2025–2028):** 4–6%

### 3.4 Digital Service Model

PPG LINQ and MOONWALK (3,300+ installations) deepen customer integration and raise switching costs while generating operational data with latent monetization value. The installed base creates a data-network effect—each new installation enriches the color database and strengthens the analytics proposition.

**Estimated Revenue Impact (2025–2028):** Primarily share-of-wallet and retention; direct revenue monetization emerging

PPG trades at a modest discount to its specialty-chemicals and industrial-technology peer group, reflecting (a) exposure to cyclical automotive and construction end-markets, (b) concerns about architectural DIY secular decline, and (c) the conglomerate discount inherent in a multi-segment coatings enterprise. The valuation does not appear to fully impute the secular growth vectors in aerospace, packaging, and protective coatings.

## 8.0 Critical Observations

1. **Guidance conservatism:** PPG's FY2026 organic growth guidance of "flat to low single-digit" may understate the compounding effect of aerospace, packaging, and protective & marine secular growth vectors
2. **EPS quality:** FY2026 EPS growth is primarily driven by margin expansion and buybacks rather than heroic volume assumptions
3. **Multi-year visibility:** Aerospace backlog ($315M) and packaging conversion cycle provide unusual multi-year revenue visibility for a coatings business
4. **Optionality:** Digital ecosystem (LINQ/MOONWALK) and sustainability portfolio (INNOVEL, SIGMAGLIDE, chromate-free) embed longer-term optionality not fully reflected in consensus estimates
