## 4.0 Strategic Growth Vectors

**Aerospace Coatings.** A $315 million backlog underpins double-digit organic growth. Proprietary chromate-free pretreatment, lightweight primers, and advanced topcoats constitute a durable competitive moat tied to global narrowbody and widebody build rates.

**Protective & Marine Coatings.** Twelve consecutive quarters of volume growth signal structural market-share capture, particularly in Asia Pacific marine. PPG PSX polysiloxane, SIGMAGLIDE biocide-free fouling release, and PPG STEELGUARD intumescent fire protection technologies anchor the portfolio.

**Packaging Coatings.** Double-digit growth with a cumulative 20%+ two-year stacked trajectory reflects the ongoing conversion from bisphenol A (BPA)-based epoxy linings to next-generation PPG INNOVEL and PPG HOBA non-BPA platforms. Regulatory tailwinds in the European Union and momentum in North America sustain the adoption curve.

**Automotive OEM & Industrial.** Geopolitical and tariff-related overhangs on global light-vehicle production are partially offset by share gains in China, Mexico, and India. Electrification transitions drive reformulation requirements for battery enclosure coatings, dielectric protection, and thermal management.

**Digital Transformation.** PPG LINQ and MOONWALK represent an end-to-end digital ecosystem with 3,300+ installations globally. The platforms enable automated mixing, color-matching, inventory management, and waste reduction in body shop and industrial finishing environments, shifting the value proposition from consumable supply to integrated service delivery.
