## He-3 Supply Chain
- **Primary Source (Short Term):** D-D side reactions within the FRC generate He-3 and tritium as fusion products.
- **Secondary Source (Long Term):** Potential lunar mining — Helion has acknowledged this but treats it as a scaling option, not a near-term requirement.
- **Tritium Decay Pathway:** Tritium (half-life 12.3 years) decays into He-3, providing a secondary production pathway if tritium is stockpiled.
- **Stockpile Status:** No publicly disclosed He-3 inventory; believed to be small quantities for R&D.
