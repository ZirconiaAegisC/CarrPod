## Impact Categories Assessed

Beyond global warming potential (GWP100), the LCA evaluates:

- **Acidification potential (AP):** SO2 and NOx from fuel combustion; lower than OPC
  due to reduced thermal energy
- **Eutrophication potential (EP):** Comparable to OPC
- **Photochemical ozone creation (POCP):** Slightly elevated from increased mining activity
- **Abiotic depletion (ADP elements):** Lower ‚Äî no limestone depletion
- **Primary energy demand (PED):** 2.5‚Äì2.9 GJ/t vs. 3.0‚Äì3.4 GJ/t for conventional OPC

## Sensitivity Analysis

Key sensitivities modeled:

1. **Grid carbon intensity:** If site uses 100% renewable electricity, net CO2 improves
   by an additional ‚àí40 to ‚àí60 kg/t
2. **Alternative fuels (biomass, hydrogen):** Combustion CO2 can approach zero
3. **MgO carbonation conversion rate:** 60% ‚Üí 95% range shifts net balance by ¬±200 kg/t
4. **Ore grade:** Lower CaO basalt requires more rock mass and higher mining emissions
