## WHY ENGAGE NOW
The IUA's London company market members write over GBP 30 billion in annual premium, much of it in specialty lines where novel risk transfer structures are conceived and placed. Their Clauses and Wordings Committee controls the contractual language that governs international insurance placements — the mechanism through which CME exclusions or affirmative cover grants would enter global commercial insurance contracts. The IUA's Digital Transformation Programme is actively exploring how new data sources can improve underwriting outcomes for complex risks.
