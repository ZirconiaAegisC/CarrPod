## WHY ENGAGE NOW
The Triple-I is the insurance industry's primary public-facing research and communications arm — its white papers, data analyses, and media outreach shape public and policymaker understanding of insurance markets. Triple-I's catastrophe reporting infrastructure (annual facts and statistics, webinar series, state-level profiles) provides a dissemination channel to educate the broader public about CME risk. The Insurance Research Council (IRC), a Triple-I division, conducts rigorous empirical insurance research that could commission the first systematic study of electromagnetic exposure in US property portfolios.
