## WHY ENGAGE NOW
The Geneva Association's membership comprises the CEOs of the world's 80 largest insurers and reinsurers — offering a convening platform unmatched in the industry for C-Suite-level engagement on emerging perils. Their "Extreme Events and Climate Risk" research program has produced seminal work on flood, wildfire, and cyber accumulation risk; electromagnetic catastrophe is the logical next frontier. The Association's "New Technologies and Data" workstream is actively exploring how new data sources can unlock previously uninsurable risks.
