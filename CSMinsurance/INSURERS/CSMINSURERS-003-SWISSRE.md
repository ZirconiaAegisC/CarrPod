## WHY SWISS RE MUST ENGAGE NOW

Swiss Re's sigma research series is the insurance industry's most respected publication on emerging risks and macroeconomic trends. Their 2025 sigma report identified "infrastructure interdependence risk" as a top-tier concern — the cascading failure chain from a single large event propagating through power, telecommunications, water, and transport networks.

A Carrington-class CME is the quintessential infrastructure-interdependence risk. The May 2024 Gannon Storm (G5/Kp9) demonstrated this: GPS disruption cascaded into agricultural equipment failures, which cascaded into planting delays, which cascaded into crop insurance exposure — a risk chain no one had fully modeled.
