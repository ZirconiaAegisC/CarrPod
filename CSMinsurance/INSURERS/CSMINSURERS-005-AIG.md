## WHY AIG MUST ENGAGE NOW

AIG is one of the largest commercial property insurers in the world and the largest U.S.-based commercial insurer. Their portfolio covers virtually every asset class that a Carrington-class CME would damage: commercial buildings (electronics/transformer fire risk), energy infrastructure (grid exposure), marine (shipping navigation failure), aviation (satellite and aircraft electronics), and cyber (cascading IT failures during grid-down events).

AIG also has a dedicated **Client Risk Solutions** consulting practice that advises large corporate clients on loss prevention — a perfect distribution channel for AEGIS-C mitigation recommendations.
