## WHY ENGAGE NOW
Mapfre's dual insurer-reinsurer structure and heavy Ibero-American footprint (Spain, Portugal, Brazil, Mexico, across Latin America) provide geographic diversification for CME risk placement across multiple regulatory regimes. Their Mapfre Re division is a top-20 global reinsurer with treaty relationships throughout the developing world — markets where grid resilience is lowest and CME protection is most needed. Mapfre's Fundación Mapfre research arm has a track record of funding emerging risk and climate adaptation research.
