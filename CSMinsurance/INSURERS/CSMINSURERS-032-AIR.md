## WHY ENGAGE NOW
AIR's Touchstone platform runs the financial models that determine how much capacity the global insurance industry allocates to catastrophe risk — for every peril except electromagnetic pulse. AIR's parent Verisk provides the data infrastructure (ISO, Xactware, PCS) to bind modeled CME loss estimates to real-world claims triggers and settlement benchmarks. AIR's supply chain risk module (ARI) could be extended to model CME-induced multi-nodal cascading failures — precisely the type of systemic event that renders traditional geographic diversification ineffective.
