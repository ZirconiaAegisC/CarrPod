## WHY ENGAGE NOW
The NAIC's Climate & Resiliency Task Force is actively developing climate risk disclosure requirements and scenario analysis frameworks for US insurers — electromagnetic catastrophe exposure is entirely absent from these frameworks. The NAIC's Catastrophe Modeling Center of Excellence evaluates and validates CAT models used by carriers for rate-making and solvency purposes; CME model endorsement would trigger mandatory insurer attention. The NAIC's Financial Condition (E) Committee oversees the Risk-Based Capital framework that could mandate CME exposure disclosure and capital charges.
