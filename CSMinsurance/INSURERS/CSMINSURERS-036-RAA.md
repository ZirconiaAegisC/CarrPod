## WHY ENGAGE NOW
The RAA serves as the unified voice of the US reinsurance industry before Congress, federal agencies, and state regulators — precisely the institutional channel needed to establish CME risk as a legislative and regulatory priority. Their annual Reinsurance Under Review and industry data reports shape the narrative that insurers, rating agencies, and policymakers use to assess market conditions. The RAA's Catastrophe Risk Education initiative provides a platform to socialize electromagnetic catastrophe within the reinsurance community through established educational programming.
