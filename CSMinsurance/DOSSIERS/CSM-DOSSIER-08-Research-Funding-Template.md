## SECTION 2: CORE DELIVERABLE — RESEARCH FUNDING REQUEST AND PROGRAM PLAN

### 1. RESEARCH PROGRAM OVERVIEW — AEGIS-C COMPOSITE SHIELDING SYSTEM

#### 1.1. Material System Description
- **Substrate**: ZrB₂-SiC (Zirconium Diboride — Silicon Carbide) ceramic matrix composite.
  - ZrB₂: Ultra-high-temperature ceramic (UHTC), melting point 3,246°C, excellent thermal shock resistance, oxidation resistance to 1,800°C.
  - SiC reinforcement: enhances fracture toughness, oxidation protection via SiO₂ passivation layer.
  - Composite architecture: multi-layer laminate with gradient composition for impedance matching and mechanical integrity.
- **EMI layer**: MXene (Ti₃C₂Tₓ) two-dimensional transition metal carbide.
  - Deposition thickness: 45 μm (±2 μm tolerance).
  - Electrical conductivity: up to 15,000 S/cm (comparable to multi-walled carbon nanotubes).
  - EMI shielding mechanism: primarily absorption (SEA > 120 dB), secondary reflection (SER ~30-45 dB) at conductor-dielectric interfaces.
- **Shielding effectiveness (SE)**: 148-165 dB across 1 kHz to 10 GHz.
  - SE = 10 log₁₀(P_incident / P_transmitted).
  - SE = 148 dB means P_transmitted / P_incident = 10^(-14.8)
  - SE = 165 dB means P_transmitted / P_incident = 10^(-16.5)

#### 1.2. Frequency Coverage
| Frequency Range | Shielding Mechanism | SE (dB) | Target Application |
|---|---|---|---|
| DC - 0.1 Hz (GIC) | Conductivity + magnetic permeability (multi-layer) | >140 dB | Transformer neutral blocking, GIC pathway interruption |
| 1 kHz - 100 MHz (E1 EMP) | MXene absorption (dominant) + reflection | 148-155 dB | Electronic equipment enclosures, control systems |
| 100 MHz - 1 GHz (E2 EMP) | MXene absorption + multi-layer reflection | 155-162 dB | Data center shielding, communications equipment |
| 1 GHz - 10 GHz (E3/HPM) | Gradient impedance matching + absorption | 160-165 dB | Radar/communications antenna protection, avionics |
