### 4. THE DIVERSIFICATION ILLUSION — WHY TRADITIONAL REINSURANCE FAILS FOR SPACE WEATHER

#### 4.1. Standard Reinsurance Diversification Assumptions
1. Perils are geographically independent (hurricane in Florida ≠ earthquake in Japan ≠ flood in Bangladesh).
2. Perils are temporally independent (they don't occur simultaneously).
3. Insurance lines are partially independent (property claims ≠ cyber claims ≠ marine claims).

#### 4.2. Carrington Event Violation of All Three Assumptions
1. **Geographic independence**: Violated. CME affects all geographies simultaneously. Correlation coefficient = 1.0.
2. **Temporal independence**: Violated. Single event triggers all perils (GIC, EMP, GPS loss, comms loss) within a 24-72 hour window.
3. **Line independence**: Violated. Property, BI, CBI, Marine, Aviation, Energy, Cyber, Crop, Workers' Comp, D&O, Life/Health all trigger from the same root cause.

#### 4.3. Consequence for Reinsurance Treaties
- **Excess of loss treaties**: Multiple layers triggered simultaneously. Ultimate net loss (UNL) exceeds modeled PML by 300-800%.
- **Catastrophe bonds**: NATCAT trigger (G-scale, Kp, Dst) triggers all outstanding cat bonds with any NATCAT exposure simultaneously. ILS market experiences first-ever "correlated catastrophe" across all outstanding bonds.
- **Retrocession**: No one to retrocede to — all counterparties are similarly affected.
- **Sidecar and collateralized structures**: Collateral is released to pay claims. No new capital available to replenish — capital markets are frozen (trading infrastructure affected by power/communications loss).
