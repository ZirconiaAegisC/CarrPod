## PHASE 3 — VERIFICATION OF RECEIPT (Day 14-28)

### Electronic Verification
1. Email read receipts and link clicks tracked in CRM
2. Cloud folder access logs (who viewed, downloaded what)
3. Automated follow-up email at Day 3 if no open detected

### Physical Verification
1. FedEx/UPS delivery confirmation with signature
2. Physical mail return receipt cards
3. Phone call follow-up at Day 5: "I'm calling to confirm the packet arrived safely"

### CRM Status Tracking
Every contact must have one of these statuses:
- SENT: Materials dispatched, awaiting confirmation
- DELIVERED: Confirmed receipt (electronic or physical)
- OPENED: Materials opened/viewed (tracked)
- ENGAGED: Response received
- MEETING SCHEDULED: Call or meeting confirmed
- FUNDING DISCUSSING: Active conversation about funding
- PARTNER: Formal partnership or funding agreement
- DECLINED: Explicit refusal
- NO RESPONSE: Three contact attempts, no response
