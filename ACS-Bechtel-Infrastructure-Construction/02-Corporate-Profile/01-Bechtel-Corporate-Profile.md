IIJA authorized $1.2T in federal infrastructure spending, with ~$25B specifically allocated to grid resilience and critical infrastructure hardening. Bechtel is the premier U.S. infrastructure contractor for major civil projects. Embedding AEGIS standards positions Bechtel to capture resilience-funded projects and offer premium services competitors cannot match.

## 4. PROPOSED STANDARDS

**BFRP Rebar:** Dielectric alternative to steel. CSM-MX-2400 MXene coating for enhanced bonding and EMI shielding. 8-15% cost premium, >100-year service life.

**Geopolymer Concrete:** Fly-ash/slag-based with MXene admixture. 100-1,000x higher electrical resistivity than Portland cement. Eliminates concrete as GIC conduction path.

**Dielectric Bearing Pads:** MXene-infused elastomeric bearings at all structural interfaces. Prevents GIC traversal between bridge deck and substructure.

**AEGIS Certification:** Silver (electrical), Gold (structural + electrical), Platinum (full-site immunity).
