## OPERATING PRINCIPLE
Daytime: PCM absorbs heat, melts at 4°C, keeps interior cool. Nighttime: PCM radiates stored heat to night sky via vent, re-freezes. Geothermal ground coupling provides baseline 10-13°C. Combined: steady 4-8°C interior with zero electricity. System is entirely passive — no moving parts, no maintenance, infinite service life.
