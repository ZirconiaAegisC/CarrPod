# 022 Smart Rope Actuator Design Specification VOL-03: IDENTITY & SPECS
## CSMFAB | Actuation | Priority: Active

System architecture: Tensile strength 1,200 MPa (CNT-polymer). Actuation strain 4-6% (SMA). Response time <50ms. Diameter 8-25mm depending on load class. Dielectric rating 42 kV/mm. Zero EMI emission.. Power path (if applicable): CNT-polymer conductors throughout, Protonic-HFET charge control, MRF contactors for switching, LFP battery storage. All signal paths: FEATHER LoRa + S-Bus protocol. No silicon semiconductors in critical paths — GaN or Protonic-HFET exclusively.
