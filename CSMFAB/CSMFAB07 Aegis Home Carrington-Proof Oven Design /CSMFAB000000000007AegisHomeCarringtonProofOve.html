  • Stone: The chassis is soaked in water. The water-soluble refractory binder dissolves.9 The soapstone slabs separate cleanly. They are re-surfaced (sanded down 1mm) and re-issued as "Heritage Stone" slabs for new ovens.
  • Ceramics: The SiC elements and Zirconia bearings are chemically cleaned in an acid bath to remove carbon deposits. They are tested for resistance and runout, then re-certified.
  • Zero Waste: The only waste product is the degraded ceramic fiber gaskets, which are inert and non-toxic (silica sand).

14. Conclusion: A New Industrial Epoch

The "Geothermal" Stone-Bake Air Fryer Oven is the flagship of the Aegis Home. It is a device built for a timeline where the sun is a threat, not just a source of light. By rejecting the conductive fragility of the Iron Age and embracing the dielectric permanence of the Ceramic Age, we provide a product that is "hyphy" in its ambition and "ancient" in its stability.

We are not just selling an oven. We are selling the assurance that when the sky turns red with the aurora of a superstorm, and the transformers explode, and the "smart" homes go dark, the owner of an Aegis oven can still bake bread. They can still feed their family. The hearth remains warm.
