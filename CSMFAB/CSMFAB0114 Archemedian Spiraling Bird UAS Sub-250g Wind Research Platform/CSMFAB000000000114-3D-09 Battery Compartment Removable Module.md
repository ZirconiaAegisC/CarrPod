# 3D Generation Prompt: Battery Compartment — Removable Module
**Carrington Storm Motors / Safe Pod Engineering Company | Version 1.0 | June 2026**

## Component Identity
**Name:** LiFePO₄ Battery Compartment
**Function:** Removable battery housing within the electronics bay. Holds the 2S LiFePO₄ 300mAh battery securely during high-G rotation while allowing quick swap in the field. The battery compartment slides into the inner wall of the electronics bay and clicks into a retention rail.
**Mass target:** 3g (housing only) | **Material:** PETG

## Precise Dimensions
- Cavity: 50mm × 32mm × 14mm (matches GNB 2S 300mAh LiFePO₄ footprint + 1mm clearance)
- Wall thickness: 1.5mm
- Lid: snap-lid with cantilever clip on one edge, piano hinge on other
- Retention rails: 2× rail ribs on exterior sides, 1mm proud, 20mm long — engage matching grooves in electronics bay
- JST-PH 2.0 cutout: 7mm × 4mm through-slot at one end-face, centered
- Velcro strap slot: 3mm × 50mm through-slot over top of lid for 10mm Velcro strap retention
- Foam pad dimples: 4× 6mm diameter × 1mm deep dimple pockets on inner floor (for anti-vibration foam dots)
- Charge port access notch: 5mm × 4mm notch on end adjacent to JST cutout (for balance lead access)

## 3D Prompt (All-in-One)
Design a small removable battery housing for a LiPo/LiFePO4 style battery (50mm × 32mm × 14mm internal cavity). The housing is a slim rectangular box with 1.5mm PETG walls. The lid is a snap-top with a hinge on one long side and a snap-clip on the other. A JST-PH connector slot (7mm × 4mm) is cut through one end. A 3mm wide slot runs across the top of the lid for a Velcro strap to pass through. Two thin retention rail ribs on the exterior long sides allow the module to slide into a slot in a larger housing. Four small circular dimples on the interior floor accept anti-vibration foam dot inserts. A small notch beside the JST port gives access to the battery balance lead. Material: translucent yellow PETG (so you can see the battery charge indicator light through the wall). Render: slightly open lid showing battery silhouette inside.
