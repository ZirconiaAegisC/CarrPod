## Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3D print failure (warping, delamination) | Medium | Medium | Use brim; PETG not PLA; print at 90°C bed temp |
| Procurement delay (AliExpress shipping) | High | Medium | Order critical parts in Week 2; local fallback at Mouser |
| Weight over 249g after final assembly | Low | High | Track mass at every phase; 72g margin is generous |
| FAA Part 107 test failure | Low | High | Study FAA study guide; practice tests at cram.com; 70% pass score needed |
| SMA wire activation failure | Low | Low | Order 2m of wire; test each wire before installation |
| Governor set point drift | Low | Medium | Calibrate on test stand; re-check after any disassembly |
| Weather delay for first flight | High | Low | 10+ m/s coastal wind is common in Southern California; many windows available |
