# 3D Generation Prompt: Wind Sensor Mast and Probe Housing
**Carrington Storm Motors / Safe Pod Engineering Company | Version 1.0 | June 2026**

## Component Identity
**Name:** 4-Hole Pressure Probe Mast and Tip
**Function:** The wind measurement probe at the nose of the ASB-1. A slender 40mm mast topped with a precision 4-hole differential pressure probe tip. Measures 3D wind velocity via differential pressure at four hole positions.
**Mass target:** 1g (mast tube 0.3g + tip housing 0.7g) | **Material:** 3mm OD carbon fiber tube (mast) + 3D-printed BFRP tip

## Precise Dimensions
- Mast tube: 3mm OD × 2mm ID × 40mm long carbon fiber tube
- Probe tip housing: 5mm OD × 10mm long cylinder, mounted on mast end
- 4 pressure holes: 0.5mm diameter, drilled at 90° spacing around circumference, 3mm from tip
- 1 total pressure hole: 0.5mm diameter, centered at tip apex
- Chamfer: 30° chamfer on leading edge of each hole
- Internal manifolds: 5× 1mm ID bores running from each hole down through the mast to the base connector
- Base connector ring: 7mm OD × 5mm long adaptor ring at mast base, 2mm ID PTFE tube ports (×5) exiting the base for connection to pressure sensors
- PEEK sleeve: 6mm OD × 4mm long sleeve molded at the mast base for electrical isolation from body
- Mast setscrew: M2 × 4mm setscrew radially into the 6mm sleeve to lock the mast to the nose cone

## 3D Prompt (All-in-One)
Design an atmospheric wind sensing probe for a small drone. The probe consists of a slender carbon fiber rod, 3mm in diameter and 40mm long. At the top end, a slightly enlarged (5mm diameter) smooth cylindrical tip is precision-machined. Five tiny 0.5mm holes are drilled in this tip: one centered at the very apex facing forward, and four holes equally spaced at 90-degree intervals around the cylindrical circumference 3mm from the top. Each hole has a slight 30-degree chamfer. Inside the probe tip and running down the length of the 3mm rod, five separate 1mm micro-channels carry the pressure signals to the base. At the base, a small adaptor ring (7mm OD) connects five 2mm silicone tube ports for connection to pressure sensors. A short non-conductive sleeve (PEEK material, grey) fits around the base to electrically insulate the carbon fiber tube from the drone body, with a small radial setscrew for locking.
