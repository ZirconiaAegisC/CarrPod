# 3D Generation Prompt: Smart Rope Actuator Housing
**Carrington Storm Motors / Safe Pod Engineering Company | Version 1.0 | June 2026**

## Component Identity
**Name:** SMA Wire Actuator Housing — Smart Rope Module
**Function:** Houses the 100mm nitinol SMA wire that forms the Smart Rope actuator. Four of these are installed at 90° spacing in the spiral body arm slots. When the SMA wire contracts (on electrical activation), it tilts the spiral body's spin axis to steer the device.
**Mass target:** 1.5g each (×4 = 6g total) | **Material:** BFRP or PETG printed

## Precise Dimensions
- Overall shape: cylindrical tube with rectangular mounting base
- Tube: 8mm OD × 30mm long, 3mm ID inner bore for SMA wire
- Mounting base: 10mm × 35mm × 3mm flat rectangular plate, integrated to bottom of tube
- Two crimp sleeve exits: each end of the 3mm bore terminates in a 2mm diameter crimp sleeve boss (1mm tall), one at each end
- Wire exit slots: two 0.5mm × 5mm slots on opposite ends of the tube for SMA wire lead routing
- Snap-fit tabs: two 1mm × 3mm flexible snap tabs on the long edges of the mounting base (engage the actuator slot in the spiral body)
- Pin indexing features: two 1.5mm diameter × 1.5mm pins on mounting base underside for location in spiral body slot
