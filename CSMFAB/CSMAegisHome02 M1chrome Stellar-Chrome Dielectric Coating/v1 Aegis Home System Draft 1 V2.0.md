- Added QD-YInMn Blue +31% UV enhancement to all exterior surfaces

1. **ZrB₂/Si₃N₄ Stove Burner (CSMFAB000000000002)** — ancient hearth burner, Voronoi tessellation top, gel-cast fabrication, piezoelectric ignition, thermochromic Cobalt Flare indicators
2. **Aerogel Thermal Break (CSMFAB000000000003)** — λ=0.010 W/m·K polyimide-silica hybrid, decouples burner heat from chassis
3. **PEX-a Plumbing System (CSMFAB000000000012)** — ρ > 10¹² Ω·m, ASTM F876-25 compliant, eliminates copper GIC pathway
4. **BFRP Rebar (CSMFAB000000000009)** — structural reinforcement, zero conductivity, 1000 MPa tensile per ACI 440.11-25
5. **YInMn Blue exterior coating** — SRI=115, reflects 85% NIR, primary spectral defense

## Version 1 Limitation
Covers kitchen and plumbing only. No refrigeration, no microwave protection, no building envelope shielding, no wiring hardening. V2 onwards adds these layers.

**V1 GIC threat reduction vs. standard home: ~35%** (kitchen appliances + plumbing isolated)
