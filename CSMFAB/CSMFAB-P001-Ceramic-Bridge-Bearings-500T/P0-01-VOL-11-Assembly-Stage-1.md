## CRITICAL ALIGNMENT
The ceramic disc must sit perfectly level in the housing (±0.05° tilt). A tilted disc creates edge-loading at the PTFE interface, reducing translation capacity and concentrating stress at the ceramic rim. Use a 0.05mm feeler gauge around the full circumference — must not enter at any point.
