# P0-01-VOL-12: Assembly Sequence — Stage 2: Anchor Bolt Torque to Final QC
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## ASSEMBLY STAGE 2: Anchoring and Final Quality Check

| Step | Operation | Tooling | Time (min) | QC Gate |
|------|-----------|---------|------------|---------|
| 2.1 | Position bearing on assembly fixture | Overhead crane | 5 | Aligned to fixture marks |
| 2.2 | Insert 4× BFRP anchor bolts with PTFE sleeves | Manual | 3 | Sleeves fully seated |
| 2.3 | Apply BFRP insulation washers | Manual | 1 | Washer flat against housing |
| 2.4 | Hand-tighten nuts | Manual wrench | 2 | Snug, no cross-threading |
| 2.5 | Cross-pattern torque to 225 N·m (50%) | Torque wrench | 4 | — |
| 2.6 | Cross-pattern torque to 450 N·m (100%) | Torque wrench + multiplier | 6 | Torque ±5% |
| 2.7 | Verify torque after 30 min relaxation | Torque wrench | 2 | No relaxation >5% |
| 2.8 | Apply weather seal silicone (Vol-10 procedure) | Pneumatic gun | 15 | Continuous bead, tooled |
| 2.9 | Laser-etch serial no., date code, load rating | Laser etcher | 2 | Legible, correct |
| 2.10 | Final dielectric test: 50 kV DC, bolt-to-housing | Hipot tester | 3 | <1μA leakage |
| 2.11 | Final dimensional: overall envelope | CMM | 5 | ±0.5mm all axes |
| 2.12 | Package in custom foam crate | Manual + crane | 10 | Secured, desiccant pack |
| 2.13 | Attach CoC + inspection reports | Manual | 2 | All docs present |
| 2.14 | Final QC hold — ship/no-ship decision | Inspector | — | All gates passed |

**Stage 2 Total Time:** 60 minutes (1.0 hour)
**Total Assembly Time (Stages 1+2):** 194 minutes (3.2 hours per bearing)

## REJECTION TRIGGERS DURING ASSEMBLY
- Anchor bolt torque exceeds 500 N·m (BFRP thread stripping risk)
- Dielectric test failure at any voltage under 42 kV
- Weather seal gap >1mm at any point
- Laser etch misalignment rendering serial unreadable
