# P0-01-VOL-13: Assembly Sequence — Stage 3: Field Installation
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## FIELD INSTALLATION PROCEDURE

### Prerequisites
- Bridge lifted on hydraulic jacks, existing bearing removed
- Bearing pedestal cleaned and inspected for cracks
- Concrete surface flatness: ±1mm over 350mm × 350mm area
- Grout pad (geopolymer CSM-MAT-GP-001) cured 7 days minimum
- Ambient temperature: 0°C to 40°C
- No precipitation during installation

### Procedure

| Step | Operation | Equipment | Time (hr) |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| 3.1 | Position bearing on grout pad | Mobile crane (5T) | 0.5 |
| 3.2 | Align to survey marks (±2mm) | Total station | 0.3 |
| 3.3 | Mark anchor bolt hole locations | Transfer punch | 0.2 |
| 3.4 | Remove bearing, drill 4× 30mm dia × 140mm deep holes | Rotary hammer drill | 1.0 |
| 3.5 | Clean holes with compressed air + brush | Compressor | 0.2 |
| 3.6 | Inject HIT-RE 500 V3 epoxy to 2/3 hole depth | Dispensing gun | 0.3 |
| 3.7 | Re-position bearing, insert BFRP bolts into epoxy | Crane + manual | 0.5 |
| 3.8 | Allow epoxy cure (min 4hr at 20°C) | — | 4.0 |
| 3.9 | Torque nuts per Stage 2 procedure | Torque wrench | 0.5 |
| 3.10 | Install weather seal (Vol-10) | Pneumatic gun | 0.3 |
| 3.11 | Lower bridge onto bearing | Hydraulic jacks | 0.5 |
| 3.12 | Verify bearing compression: 1-2mm measured settlement | Dial indicator | 0.2 |
| 3.13 | Final survey: bearing position ±3mm design | Total station | 0.3 |
| 3.14 | Document with photos + as-built survey | Tablet | 0.2 |
| **Total Field Time (excl. epoxy cure):** | | | **5.0 hr** |

## TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT
- Lane closure: bearing lane + 1 adjacent lane
- Minimum closure duration: 12 hours (includes 4hr epoxy cure + safety margin)
- Traffic control per MUTCD Chapter 6F
