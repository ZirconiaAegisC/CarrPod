## SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE

| Interval | Task | Method | Time/Bearing |
|----------|------|--------|-------------|
| **After major GMD event (Kp>7)** | Dielectric test | Megger 1 kV | 15 min |
| | Visual inspection | Crack, PTFE wear, seal | 10 min |
| | Anchor bolt torque check | Torque wrench | 10 min |
| **Annual** | Visual inspection | Binoculars from deck | 5 min |
| | FEATHER node data download | LoRa mesh | Automated |
| **5-year** | Full Level 2 inspection | All electrical + mechanical tests | 45 min |
| | PTFE thickness measurement | Ultrasonic gauge | 5 min |
| **25-year** | PTFE sliding surface replacement | Vol-08 procedure | 2 hr |
| **50-year** | Complete bearing replacement | Full retrofit procedure | 12 hr |

## UNPLANNED MAINTENANCE TRIGGERS
- FEATHER node alerts: GIC >5A, temperature excursion >85°C, displacement anomaly
- Visual: crack >0.5mm in ceramic disc, weather seal gap >2mm, BFRP housing delamination
- Post-seismic: any earthquake >M4.0 within 50km — full Level 2 inspection
