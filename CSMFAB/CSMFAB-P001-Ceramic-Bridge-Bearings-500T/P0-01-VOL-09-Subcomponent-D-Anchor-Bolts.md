# P0-01-VOL-09: Subcomponent D — Anchor Bolt Set
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## SUBCOMPONENT ID: SC-D-001
**Name:** Dielectric Anchor Bolt Set, M24 × 200mm, 4 per bearing

## BOLT SPECIFICATION

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Bolt material | BFRP threaded rod (BF-002 Marine Grade) |
| Thread | M24 × 3.0 pitch |
| Length | 200mm |
| Tensile strength | 780 MPa |
| Shear strength | 220 MPa |
| Insulation sleeve | PTFE, 2mm wall thickness |
| Insulation washer | BFRP, 5mm thick, 50mm OD |
| Torque specification | 450 N·m (dry) |
| Dielectric withstand | 50 kV DC bolt-to-housing |

## FUNCTION
Secures bearing assembly to bridge superstructure and substructure. The BFRP bolt eliminates the steel anchor bolt conductive path that would bypass the ceramic dielectric break. The PTFE isolation sleeve prevents any incidental contact between the bolt body and the bridge steel, even after decades of vibration and thermal cycling.

## THREAD ENGAGEMENT
- Minimum thread engagement: 36mm (1.5× bolt diameter)
- Insert depth into concrete: 120mm minimum
- Epoxy anchor: Hilti HIT-RE 500 V3 (or approved equivalent for BFRP-in-concrete bonding)

## CORROSION
BFRP is immune to chloride corrosion (no steel to oxidize). No galvanic couple with bridge steel. Suitable for marine environments and de-icing salt exposure without protective coating.
