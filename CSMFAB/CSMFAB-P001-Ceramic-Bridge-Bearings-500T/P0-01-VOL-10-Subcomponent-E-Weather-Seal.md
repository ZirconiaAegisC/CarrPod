# P0-01-VOL-10: Subcomponent E — Weather Seal System
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## SUBCOMPONENT ID: SC-E-001
**Name:** Silicone Weather Seal, Continuous Perimeter, 3mm × 3mm

## MATERIAL: Dow Corning 795 Silicone Building Sealant

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Durometer (Shore A) | 35 |
| Tensile Strength | 2.4 MPa |
| Elongation at Break | 1,200% |
| Movement Capability | ±50% |
| Service Temperature | -55 to +200°C |
| UV Resistance | 50-year rated |
| Application Temperature | -20 to +50°C |
| Tack-Free Time | 15-30 min |
| Full Cure | 7 days (3mm bead) |

## SEAL GROOVE GEOMETRY
- Width: 3mm
- Depth: 3mm
- Path: Continuous around all 4 sides of the BFRP housing, between housing and bridge structure
- Bond breaker: polyethylene backer rod, 3mm dia, at bottom of groove

## FUNCTION
Prevents water, de-icing salts, and debris ingress into the bearing assembly. Maintains dielectric isolation by preventing conductive moisture bridges between the ceramic disc and the bridge steel. Must survive 75 years of UV exposure, thermal cycling (-40 to +85°C), and ±50mm translation without debonding or cracking.

## INSTALLATION
1. Clean groove surfaces with isopropyl alcohol, dry 5 min
2. Install polyethylene backer rod
3. Mask adjacent surfaces with painter's tape
4. Apply silicone bead with pneumatic gun, 6mm nozzle
5. Tool to flat profile within 10 min of application
6. Remove masking tape immediately
7. Protect from rain/dew for 4 hours minimum
8. Full cure before bearing load application: 7 days
