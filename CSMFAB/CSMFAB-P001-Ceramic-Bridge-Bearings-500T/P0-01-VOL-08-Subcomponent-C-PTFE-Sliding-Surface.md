# P0-01-VOL-08: Subcomponent C — PTFE Sliding Surface
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## SUBCOMPONENT ID: SC-C-001
**Name:** PTFE Sliding Surface Sheet, 250mm dia × 4mm
**Qty per bearing:** 2 (upper and lower interfaces)

## MATERIAL: FLUOROGOLD® PTFE (Saint-Gobain)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Thickness | 4mm ±0.1 |
| Sheet diameter | 250mm |
| Coefficient of Friction (steel) | 0.04 static, 0.03 dynamic |
| Compressive Strength | 35 MPa |
| Operating Temperature | -200 to +260°C |
| UV Resistance | Excellent — no degradation after 5,000 hr QUV |
| Service Life | 75 years (no maintenance) |
| Weight per sheet | 0.48 kg |

## FUNCTION
Provides low-friction sliding interface between the ZTA ceramic disc and the bridge superstructure/substructure. Allows ±50mm translation and ±0.02 rad rotation under load without metal-to-ceramic contact. Eliminates steel slider plates entirely, breaking the last remaining conductive path.

## BONDING TO ZTA DISC
1. Ceramic surface cleaned with acetone, dried 60°C/30min
2. Apply silane coupling agent (3M Scotch-Weld AC-130)
3. Compression bond PTFE sheet at 4 MPa, 180°C, 45 min
4. Cool under pressure to 50°C
5. QC: shear test at 4 MPa — must exceed PTFE compressive yield (35 MPa) before bond failure

## REPLACEMENT
PTFE sliding surfaces are field-replaceable. Remove bearing from housing, peel worn sheet, solvent-clean ceramic surface, apply new sheet per bonding procedure. Estimated labor: 2 hours per bearing. Recommended replacement interval: 25 years (proactive) or upon inspection finding >1mm wear.
