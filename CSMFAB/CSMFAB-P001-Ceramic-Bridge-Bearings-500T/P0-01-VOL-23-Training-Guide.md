# P0-01-VOL-23: Training Guide — Inspector & Installer Certification
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## CERTIFICATION PATH

### Level 1: Bearing Installer (16 hr course)
- Module 1: GIC physics for bridge engineers (4hr) — why steel bearings conduct, why ceramic doesn't
- Module 2: Ceramic bearing anatomy (4hr) — ZTA disc, BFRP housing, PTFE surfaces
- Module 3: Installation procedure (4hr classroom + 4hr field practicum)
- Exam: Install one bearing under supervision, pass QC gate checklist

### Level 2: Bearing Inspector (8 hr course, prereq: Level 1)
- Module 4: Dielectric testing (megger, hipot)
- Module 5: Wear measurement (PTFE thickness via ultrasonic, ceramic crack detection via dye penetrant)
- Module 6: FEATHER node data interpretation
- Exam: Inspect 3 bearings, document findings, pass/fail determination

### Level 3: Bearing Designer (40 hr course, prereq: Level 2 + PE license)
- Custom bearing sizing for non-standard bridge geometries
- FEM analysis of ZTA disc under eccentric loading
- Integration with bridge structural health monitoring systems

## RECERTIFICATION
- Level 1 & 2: Every 3 years
- Level 3: Every 5 years + 20 continuing education hours in bridge bearing technology
