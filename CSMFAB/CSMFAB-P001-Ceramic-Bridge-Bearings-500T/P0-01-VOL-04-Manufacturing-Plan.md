# P0-01-VOL-04: Manufacturing Plan
## CSMFAB-P001 | Ceramic Bridge Bearings 500T

---

## PROCESS FLOW

### Phase 1: Powder Preparation
1. Weigh ZrO₂ (18.2 kg) + Al₂O₃ (6.8 kg) + CNT (0.12 kg) + sintering aids (0.4 kg)
2. Ball mill in deionized water with 5mm YSZ media, 24hr, 60 RPM
3. Spray dry to granulate (inlet 220°C, outlet 110°C)
4. Sieve to 100-200μm granule size
5. QC: particle size distribution via laser diffraction (D50 = 150μm ± 20)

### Phase 2: Green Body Forming
6. Cold isostatic press (CIP) at 200 MPa, 3 min dwell
7. Green machining: CNC lathe to near-net shape (+1.5mm allowance)
8. Green density check: >55% theoretical via Archimedes method
9. QC: dimensional check via CMM, all axes ±0.05mm

### Phase 3: Flash Sintering
10. Load into flash sintering furnace (custom CSM-FAB-001)
11. Ramp to 700°C at 10°C/min in air
12. Apply 120V/cm DC field, current limit 80 mA/mm²
13. Flash event: <5 sec at 850°C, power density 1200 mW/mm³
14. Hold at 850°C for 10 min post-flash for grain stabilization
15. Controlled cool at 5°C/min to 200°C, then natural

### Phase 4: Finishing
16. Diamond grind to final dimensions (±0.02mm)
17. Polish bearing surface to Ra <0.2μm
18. Apply PTFE sliding surface with compression bonding
19. QC: full dimensional inspection, surface roughness profilometry

### Phase 5: Assembly & Packaging
20. Mount bearing into BFRP housing
21. Torque anchor bolts to 450 N·m
22. Apply silicone weather seal
23. Laser-etch serial number, date code, load rating
24. Package in custom foam crate with desiccant
25. Final QC: dielectric test at 50 kV DC

## PRODUCTION LINE LAYOUT
- Floor space: 450 m²
- Equipment: 2 ball mills, 1 spray dryer, 1 CIP press, 2 CNC lathes, 4 flash sintering furnaces, 2 diamond grinders, 1 CMM station
- Throughput: 3 bearings/day (single shift), 6 bearings/day (2 shifts)
- Annual capacity: 1,100 units (single shift, 250 days)
