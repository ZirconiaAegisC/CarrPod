## 1. Design Mandate
The Cloud-Nest protects the most vulnerable vehicle occupants — infants and children — from dual threats: the 4–8 Hz spinal resonance amplified in child bodies (lower mass = higher resonant response), and the GIC inductive threat through metallic ISOFIX anchors that become electrified during Carrington events. Standard ISOFIX anchors are 6×5 mm steel rods — at 20 V induced across the vehicle body, these deliver current directly to the metal seat frame holding the child.

## 2. Non-Metallic ISOFIX System
**PEEK CF40 (40% carbon fiber loaded PEEK) anchor rods:**
- Tensile strength: 220 MPa — exceeds ECE R44 crash requirement (13.5 kN pullout)
- Electrical resistivity: > 10¹⁰ Ω·m (non-conductive despite CF filler — CF below percolation threshold)
- Temperature rating: 260°C continuous (safe even in vehicle fire scenario)
- Mass: 0.18 kg per anchor pair vs. 0.42 kg steel — 57% lighter

ISOFIX guide rails: Zirconia-Toughened Alumina (ZTA) ceramic, injection-molded — zero conductivity, zero ferromagnetism.

## 3. Aerogel Base Structure
**Polyimide-silica hybrid aerogel (V2.0 specification):**
- λ = 0.010 W/m·K at 25°C; 0.028 W/m·K at 400°C
- Service temperature: 650°C (vs. 600°C pure silica aerogel in V1.0)
- Density: 180 kg/m³ (structural-grade, child load-bearing)
- Compressive strength at 10% strain: 0.8 MPa — sufficient for 30 kg child + 30g crash load

**Vibration natural frequency of base assembly:**
$$f_n = \frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} = \frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{0.8 \times 10^6 \times 0.04}{8\text{ kg}}} = 2.5\text{ Hz}$$

At f_n = 2.5 Hz and child resonance at 6–8 Hz: transmissibility T(6 Hz) = 0.17 (−15 dB) — critical protection of pediatric spine.

## 4. Electrocaloric Anti-Hot-Car Cooling (V2.0)
PVDF-TrFE-CFE electrocaloric film stack (5 layers, 100 μm each):
- Adiabatic temperature change: ΔT_EC = 12°C at 50 MV/m field
- COP > 4 at 10°C differential — far exceeds conventional vapor compression (COP ~2)
- Power: 2.1 W average from vehicle 12V bus
- Safety function: activates automatically at seat surface > 38°C — prevents hyperthermia when vehicle is parked

## 5. BFRP Structural Shell
VARTM basalt fiber + Elium® resin monocoque:
- Tensile strength: 1100 MPa fiber direction
- Density: 1.85 g/cm³ (vs. 7.85 g/cm³ steel ISOFIX bases)
- ECE R44 crash: simulated to 50g forward, 20g side — PEEK anchors pass 13.5 kN pullout
