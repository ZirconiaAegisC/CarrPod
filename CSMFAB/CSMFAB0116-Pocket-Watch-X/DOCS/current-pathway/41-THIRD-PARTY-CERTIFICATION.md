# CSMFAB0116-41 — THIRD-PARTY FIN CERTIFICATION PROGRAM
## 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
The 'Works with Pocket Watch X' certification ensures third-party fins meet quality, safety, and compatibility standards.

## 2. CERTIFICATION TIERS
| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Bronze** | Mechanical fit, basic electrical, self-certified | Listed in compatible directory |
| **Silver** | Bronze + full compliance testing at authorized lab | Logo usage, marketing support |
| **Gold** | Silver + performance benchmarks, reliability testing | Priority listing, co-marketing, retail placement |
