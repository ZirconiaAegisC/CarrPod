## 2. OPTICAL BONDING PROCESS
1. Plasma clean glass and touch sensor surfaces (Ar/O₂, 200W, 60s).
2. Laminate OCA film onto touch sensor at 60°C, 0.3 MPa.
3. Align cover glass to sensor (±25 µm).
4. Vacuum laminate at 0.1 Pa, 65°C, 0.5 MPa, 120s.
5. Autoclave: 50°C, 0.5 MPa, 20 min (bubble removal).
6. UV edge cure: 365 nm, 2 J/cm².
