# CSMFAB0116-F13 — FLEXIBLE/STRETCHABLE ELECTRONICS PATHWAY: Liquid metal traces (Galinstan: Ga-In-Sn) in elastomer substrate for fin connectors. Conformable to non-planar surfaces. Stretchability: >50% strain without failure. Durability: 100K+ stretch cycles. Applications: conformal fin contact, wearable sensors. Timeline: 2028 flexible fin PCBs. Key research: Rogers Group (Northwestern), Someya Lab (U Tokyo).
