# CSMFAB0116-F16 — QUANTUM COMMUNICATION FIN PATHWAY (2030-2040): QKD (Quantum Key Distribution) miniaturization. BB84 or E91 protocol over free-space optical (line-of-sight) or fiber. Entangled photon source: SPDC (spontaneous parametric down-conversion). Single-photon detector: SNSPD (superconducting nanowire, needs cooling) or SPAD array. Satellite QKD: integrate with Micius-style satellite network. Timeline: 2035 first compact QKD transmitter, 2040 fin integration. Challenge: cooling, alignment, key rate.
