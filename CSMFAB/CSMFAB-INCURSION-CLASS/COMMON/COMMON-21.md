# INCURSION COMMON-21: Thermal Management — Passive Cooling Architecture
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

No active cooling. All heat-generating components (Core-1, motors, battery) mounted to aluminum heat spreader plate bonded to inner hull surface. Hull acts as heat exchanger to water. Heat flux: 0.5 W/cm² at full power. Hull-water ΔT <8°C. Advantage: BFRP thermal conductivity 0.4 W/m·K — insulates interior from cold water while conducting electronics heat outward through dedicated thermal vias (CNT-doped BFRP inserts, 12 W/m·K).

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
