# INCURSION COMMON-18: Anti-Fouling and Marine Growth Prevention
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

Stellar-Chrome coating on all wetted surfaces: ZrB₂-SiC with 0.5% Cu₂O biocide (EPA-registered). Smoothness: Ra <0.1μm — barnacle cyprids cannot attach. MXene underlayer: UV-activated photocatalytic — produces trace H₂O₂ at surface in sunlight, inhibiting biofilm. Recoat interval: 2 years in tropical waters. No TBT, no copper paint — environmentally compliant.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
