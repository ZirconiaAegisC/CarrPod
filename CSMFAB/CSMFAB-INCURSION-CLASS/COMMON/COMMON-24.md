# INCURSION COMMON-24: Executive Summary — Quintuple-Class Incursion Drone System
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

Five autonomous surface/sub-surface vessels sharing a common biomimetic architecture. Archimedean screw propulsion for silence and efficiency. Retractable hydrofoils for speed and rough-weather submersion. Tardigrade-inspired hull for impact resilience and cryptobiotic survival. Wave-powered inductive charging for extended endurance. BFRP-MXene construction for EMI resilience and corrosion immunity. Unit costs from $4,800 (Scout) to $32,000 (Deep). Target markets: consumer recreation ($2.4B TAM), maritime industry ($8.7B), scientific research ($1.2B), disaster response ($3.8B). Production ramp: 500 units Year 1 to 50,000 units Year 5. The Incursion Class: water drones that go where propellers can't, survive what boats can't, and charge themselves from the ocean they explore.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
