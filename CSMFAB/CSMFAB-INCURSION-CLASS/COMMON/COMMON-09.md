# INCURSION COMMON-09: Submersible Ballast and Sealing System
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

Active ballast: 2× 500mL piston tanks (Scout) to 4× 1L (Deep). BFRP piston, PTFE seal, MRF valve. Flood time: 15 sec (Scout), 45 sec (Deep). Hydrogel gaskets at all hull penetrations — swell on water contact, self-sealing. Tested to 2× rated depth. Tardigrade cryptobiotic seal mimics dehydration curl.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
