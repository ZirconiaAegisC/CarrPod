# INCURSION COMMON-22: Modular Construction and Field Repairability
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

All 5 vessels share 70% parts commonality. Hydrofoil modules: identical across Scout/Courier/Surveyor. Screw propulsion: identical pitch control mechanism. Core-1: identical PCB. Battery: modular LFP pouches (50Wh each) — swap failed cells in field. Hull repair: BFRP patch kit (epoxy + fabric) included. Field-replaceable: hydrofoil blades, screw elements, gaskets, antenna, camera housing. Mean time to repair: <30 min for 80% of failure modes.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
