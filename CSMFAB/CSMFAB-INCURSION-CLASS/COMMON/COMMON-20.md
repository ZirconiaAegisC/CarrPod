# INCURSION COMMON-20: Depth Rating and Pressure Vessel Design
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

Scout: 15m (1.5 bar) — standard BFRP hull, no special reinforcement. Courier: 20m (2 bar) — 6mm hull, sealed cargo bay. Guardian: 30m (3 bar) — 7mm hull, armored sensor bay. Surveyor: 50m (5 bar) — 8mm hull, 2mm ZTA ceramic reinforcement ring at equator. Deep: 200m (20 bar) — 10mm hull, 4mm ZTA reinforcement, hemispherical end caps, Ti-6Al-4V insert rings at all penetrations. All depth ratings include 2× safety factor.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
