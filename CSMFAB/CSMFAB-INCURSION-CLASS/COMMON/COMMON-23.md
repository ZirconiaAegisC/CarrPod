# INCURSION COMMON-23: Manufacturing Production Line Layout
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

Shared line for all 5 classes. Station 1: Hull layup (VARTM, 8hr cure). Station 2: CNC trim and drill. Station 3: MXene spray coating. Station 4: Hydrofoil assembly and test. Station 5: Screw propulsion assembly. Station 6: Electronics tray (Core-1, battery, sensors). Station 7: Final integration and pressure test. Station 8: Water tank mission profile test. Throughput: 4 vessels/day (single shift). Floor space: 350 m². Workforce: 12 technicians + 2 engineers.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
