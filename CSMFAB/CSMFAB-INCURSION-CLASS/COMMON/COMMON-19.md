# INCURSION COMMON-19: Fleet Operations and Swarm Tactics
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

5-20 vessel swarm. Roles: 1 Lead (Guardian, forward-scan), 3-5 Surveyors (grid pattern), 2 Couriers (relay), 2 Scouts (perimeter), 1 Deep (subsurface). S-Bus coordination: Lead assigns survey grid, vessels self-organize. Loss compensation: if Lead lost, Guardian with next-highest battery SOC assumes Lead. Swarm endurance: 8-16 hours continuous coverage. Area coverage: 2.5 km²/hr (5 vessel) to 40 km²/hr (20 vessel).

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
