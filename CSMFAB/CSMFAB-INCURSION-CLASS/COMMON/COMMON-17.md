# INCURSION COMMON-17: Hydrofoil Actuator Specification — MRF Rotary
## CSMFAB-INCURSION | Common Architecture | All 5 Vessel Classes

CSM-MRF-001 rotary actuator. 120° travel. 25 N·m torque. MRF: carbonyl iron 40% in silicone oil. Coil: 800 turns 32 AWG on BFRP bobbin. Response: <200ms full travel. Holding: MRF solidifies in <5ms when current removed — hydrofoil locked in position without power. Fail-safe: spring-return to retracted. Rated 50,000 cycles. BFRP housing. Depth-rated to 200m.

Applies to: I-SCOUT, I-COURIER, I-SURVEYOR, I-GUARDIAN, I-DEEP. Commonality ensures 70% parts sharing across fleet.
