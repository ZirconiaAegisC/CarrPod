## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Incursion Class is a quintuple family of autonomous water drones — five vessels
spanning consumer recreation through deep-water scientific exploration — unified by a
shared biomimetic architecture. Every vessel uses Archimedean screw propulsion for
silent, efficient, entanglement-free thrust. Every vessel deploys retractable hydrofoils
for high-speed surface operation and folds them flush into the hull for submersion or
rough-weather survival. Every vessel's hull draws from the tardigrade — the most
resilient microscopic creature known to science — incorporating segmented armor,
cryptobiotic survival protocols, and hydrogel self-sealing gaskets. Every vessel
harvests energy from the ocean itself through a wave-motion inductive Message-to-Charge
system that extends endurance 40% in typical sea states.

## CORE TECHNOLOGIES

**Archimedean Screw Propulsion:** Variable-pitch spiral blades on water-lubricated ZTA
ceramic bearings with CNT-polymer shafts. 85% propulsive efficiency (vs. 60% for
propellers). Silent — no cavitation below 25 knots. No entanglement hazard — no
exposed propeller, safe for marine mammals and kelp beds.

**Retractable Hydrofoil System:** 2 forward canards + 2 aft main foils. ZTA sections
on BFRP spars. MRF rotary actuators deploy in <2 seconds, retract flush into hull
channels. Hydrogel gaskets (tardigrade-derived swell-on-contact material) seal foil
channels when retracted. 50,000 cycle rated. In rough weather or during submersion,
foils retract and the vessel operates as a displacement hull or submersible.

**Tardigrade Biomimetic Hull:** Segmented BFRP-MXene armor plates with overlapping
flexible joints. Cryptobiotic "tun state" survival mode: vessel dehydrates internal
cavities (purges with dry nitrogen), curls hydrofoils, seals all orifices with
hydrogel, powers down to <1mW, and drifts passively for up to 90 days. Reactivation
via FEATHER mesh wake command. Impact-tolerant: segmented armor distributes strike
force across plates rather than concentrating at point of impact.

**Message-to-Charge Wave Induction:** Linear induction generator in central hull cavity.
NdFeB N52 magnet array on sprung mass oscillates with wave heave at tunable 0.5-2 Hz
resonance (via MRF damper). CNT-polymer stator coil. Protonic-HFET rectifier.
5-25W continuous in Sea State 2-4, extending endurance 40%. No external moving parts.
Survival lock: magnet array mechanically locks at >5g acceleration.

**70% Parts Commonality:** Hydrofoil modules, screw propulsion pitch mechanisms,
Core-1 avionics, battery pouches, gaskets, antennas, and hull repair kits are identical
across all five classes. Manufacturing on a single shared production line.
