## V1.0 vs V2.0 — Specific Parameter Changes

| Parameter | V1.0 | V2.0 | Reason | Primary Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fence post material** | Hot-dip galvanized steel 75×75×4 mm SHS | BFRP/Elium® 80×80×5 mm box pultrusion (ρ>10¹⁰ Ω·m, 1100 MPa) | 14 km perimeter induces 280 V GIC; steel posts conduct and corrode 10 kg/A·yr | Johnson et al. 2024; Faraday corrosion law |
| **Post surface coating** | Polyester powder coat (SE ~10 dB) | Ti₃C₂Tₓ MXene spray + YInMn Blue (SE 92 dB, SRI 130) | HPM/DEW protection for critical infrastructure perimeter required at 92 dB; V1.0 coating inadequate | ACS Appl. Mater. Interfaces 2025; Solar Energy Mater. Sol. Cells 258 (2025) |
| **YInMn Blue coating** | YInMn Blue + Ta₂O₅ (SRI 115, UV standard) | QD-enhanced: CsPbBr₃ QD overlay (SRI 130, UV +31%) | V1.0 UV band gap leaves polymer substrate UV-degradation unmitigated | Solar Energy Mater. Sol. Cells 258 (2025) 112456 |
| **Connection hardware** | Galvanized steel bolts M10 (plasma arc risk) | PEEK CF40 clamps + ZrO₂ M12 ceramic bolts (18 kV/mm breakdown) | Steel bolted connections are plasma arc initiation points during GIC events | ZrO₂ ceramic fastener data 2025; PEEK CF40 spec |
| **Ground anchor** | Concrete embedded steel | Flash-sintered 3Y-TZP ZrO₂ ceramic socket anchor (85 kN) | Steel anchor connects fence post to soil GIC return path — breaks isolation | Johnson et al. 2024; ISO 3828 anchor testing |
| **Solar Cycle 25** | SSN ~115 basis | SSN ~137; fence GIC voltage +20% (from 233 V to 280 V at 14 km perimeter) | Direct quantitative update required for all GIC-dependent design values | NOAA SWPC Q1 2026 |
