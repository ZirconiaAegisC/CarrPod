# 040 Smart Rope Actuator Design Specification VOL-05: IDENTITY & SPECS
## CSMFAB | Actuation | Priority: Active

Critical material specifications: Tensile strength 1,200 MPa (CNT-polymer). Actuation strain 4-6% (SMA). Response time <50ms. Diameter 8-25mm depending on load class. Dielectric rating 42 kV/mm. Zero EMI emission.. Testing per CSM-TEST protocol suite. Every material lot tested for: composition (XRF/XRD), particle size (laser diffraction for powders, SEM for composites), dielectric strength (ASTM D149), DC resistivity (ASTM D257), mechanical properties (ASTM per material class).
