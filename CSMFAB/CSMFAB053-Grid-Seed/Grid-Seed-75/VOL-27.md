# Grid-Seed VOL-27: Battery Capacity Test
## CSMFAB053-75 | Home Microgrid | 4kWh LFP | 3kW Inverter

Charge: 0.2C (40A) to 28.8V (3.6V/cell), CC-CV, terminate at C/20 (10A). Rest 30 min. Discharge: 0.2C to 20V (2.5V/cell). Capacity = discharge Ah. Pass: >192Ah (96% of rated 200Ah). Equipment: Arbin BT2000 battery tester. 100% of units.
