# Grid-Seed VOL-03: Battery Pack Specification
## CSMFAB053-75 | 2P4S LiFePO₄ | 24V/200Ah/4kWh

8× EVE LF100LA prismatic cells, 100Ah/3.2V each. Configuration: 2 parallel × 4 series. Nominal 24V, 200Ah, 4.0kWh (3.5kWh usable at 90% DoD). Cell dimensions: 160×50×118mm, 1.95kg each. Compression fixture: BFRP end plates with 4× M6 threaded rods, 300kgf (validated for 6,000 cycle life). BMS: CSM-BMS-8S, ±5mV cell accuracy, 4× NTC thermistors, 100mA passive balancing. Over-voltage protection 3.65V/cell, under-voltage 2.5V/cell. Over-current 200A pulse/100A continuous. Cycle life: 6,000 to 80% capacity. Calendar life: 15 years at 25°C ambient. Chemistry: intrinsically safe — no thermal runaway below 250°C, no liquid electrolyte, UN38.3 certified transport.
