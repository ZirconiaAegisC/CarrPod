# Grid-Seed VOL-01: System Architecture Overview
## CSMFAB053-75 | Home Microgrid | 4kWh LFP | 3kW Inverter

Wall-mounted 4kWh lithium iron phosphate battery with integrated 3kW pure sine wave inverter, automatic 50ms transfer switch, and Protonic-HFET solar MPPT. BFRP-Elium enclosure (450×300×180mm, 42kg) provides inherent EMI shielding. Islands home critical circuits during grid failure. No silicon semiconductors in charge path — immune to GIC-induced single-event upset. Communicates via FEATHER LoRa mesh with S-Bus protocol. Powers refrigerator, furnace blower, well pump, internet router, and LED lighting for 8-12 hours. The plug-and-play Carrington-hardened microgrid.
