# Grid-Seed VOL-04: Inverter Power Stage
## CSMFAB053-75 | 3kW GaN | 93% Efficiency | 100kHz Switching

4× GaN Systems GS66516T 650V HEMTs in full-bridge topology. Gate drivers: Silicon Labs Si8271 isolated. DC link: 6× 470μF electrolytic + 4× 10μF MLCC ceramic (CNT-polymer bus bars). Output filter: 2× 10μH ferrite-core inductors (hand-wound, 10 turns CNT-001 wire) + 4× 4.7μF film capacitors. Switching: 100kHz PWM, unipolar modulation. Output: 120V/240V split-phase, 60Hz ±0.1Hz. Surge: 6kW for 3 seconds. Peak efficiency: 93% at 50% load. Idle consumption: <5W no-load. GaN bandgap 3.4 eV — immune to single-event burnout from GIC-induced voltage transients. Heatsink: aluminum extrusion 150×100×40mm, natural convection, max 75°C at full load.
