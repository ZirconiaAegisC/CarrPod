# Grid-Seed VOL-26: HI-POT Electrical Safety Test
## CSMFAB053-75 | Home Microgrid | 4kWh LFP | 3kW Inverter

1,500V AC, 60 sec: input-to-output, input-to-enclosure, output-to-enclosure. Pass: <1mA leakage all cases. Test equipment: Chroma 19052 hipot tester. Procedure: connect all phases together, apply voltage, ramp 5 sec, hold 60 sec, measure. Fail at >1mA → root cause inspection → rework → retest. 100% of units.
