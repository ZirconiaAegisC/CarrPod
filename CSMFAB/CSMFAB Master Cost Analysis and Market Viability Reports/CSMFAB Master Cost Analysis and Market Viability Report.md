## Executive Summary

The CSMFAB portfolio spans 43 distinct products across 9 commercial categories. Per-unit production
costs range from **$32** (BFRP rebar, per 6m bar) to **$108,000** (Knott's Berry Farm per 100m² module).
The full portfolio cannot be launched simultaneously. A strategic staged approach — beginning with
**12 immediately marketable consumer products** — can generate seed revenue of an estimated
**$8.5M–$22M in Year 1** at projected initial volumes, funding tooling and material supply chains for
the full product line.

**Critical cost finding:** MXene Ti₃C₂Tₓ at $2,000/kg (2026) is the single highest per-unit cost
driver for 18 products. Price projections of $400-600/kg by 2028-2029 as production scales will
dramatically improve margins across the entire portfolio.

## Part V — Recommended 5-Product Seed Money Bundle

Based on lowest NRE, fastest time-to-market, best margins, and clearest distribution path:

### 🏆 Product 1: BFRP Rebar
**Why:** Zero exotic equipment; pull-winding is standard. Existing steel rebar market = $26B/yr.
Immediate structural contractor channel. Mark: **$32 COGS → $65 MSRP = 51% margin.**

### 🏆 Product 2: Passive EMF Detector
**Why:** No moving parts, no exotic processes except chromatic glaze. First-mover in $0 existing market.
Consumer + industrial safety segment. Privacy + space weather monitoring trend. Mark: **$235 COGS → $420 MSRP = 44% margin.**

### 🏆 Product 3: Stellar-Tint EV Window Film
**Why:** Roll-to-roll manufacturing; existing window film infrastructure can be contracted.
7M+ new EVs/year in US alone. NIR + circadian + EMI in one film = unique value prop.
Mark: **$295 COGS → $520 MSRP = 43% margin.**

### 🏆 Product 4: Dielectric Rope & Rigging Kit
**Why:** UHMWPE rope is standard; ZrO2/PEEK hardware is precision machined but small volumes.
Immediate industrial electrical safety + marine markets. Mark: **$265 COGS → $480 MSRP = 45% margin.**

### 🏆 Product 5: Thorax-Calm STF Seatbelt Set
**Why:** TPU and STF fluid are low-cost; PEEK buckle covers are standard machined parts.
Automotive aftermarket + fleet safety segment. Clear liability reduction story.
Mark: **$200 COGS → $380 MSRP = 47% margin.**
