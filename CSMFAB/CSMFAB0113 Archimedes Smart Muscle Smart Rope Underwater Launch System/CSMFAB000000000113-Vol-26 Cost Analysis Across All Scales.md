## 2. Reuse and Amortization

Smart Rope lifetime: 3-10 launches (limited by UHMWPE UV degradation for surface ropes)
Spear lifetime: 1 launch (ZrB2-SiC — expendable for high-velocity flights; recoverable at nano/micro scale)
Vessels: chartered per launch or owned (1-3 year payback for frequent use)

At 10 launches/year, Scale 3:
- Fixed costs (vessels, equipment): $30,000/yr
- Variable per launch: $8,000
- Total annualized: $110,000/yr = $11,000 per launch

**$11,000 per suborbital research launch** — competitive with sounding rockets ($40,000-$150,000).
