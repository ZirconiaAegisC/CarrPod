# CSMFAB0116-F18 — PLASMONIC SENSOR FIN PATHWAY: Surface plasmon resonance (SPR) for molecular detection. Gold nanostructures on fin sensor surface. Detects: specific proteins (antibodies), DNA hybridization, small molecules. Sensitivity: refractive index change 10⁻⁷ RIU enabling pg/mL detection. Applications: medical diagnostics (cancer biomarkers, infectious disease), environmental monitoring (heavy metals, pesticides), food safety. Lab-on-a-fin concept. Research: plasmonic biosensing field active since 2000s; miniaturization to fin scale needs integrated optics (LED + spectrometer). Timeline: 2030-2035.
