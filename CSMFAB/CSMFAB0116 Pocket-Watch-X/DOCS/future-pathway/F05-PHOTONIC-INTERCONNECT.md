# CSMFAB0116-F05 — PHOTONIC INTERCONNECT PATHWAY (2030-2035): Replace copper PCIe traces with silicon photonic waveguides in fin bus. Benefits: >100 Gbps per lane, zero EMI, lower power per bit. Technologies: micro-ring modulators, Ge photodetectors on SOI. Challenges: optical coupling through fin connector (grating couplers, microlenses). CMOS compatibility: GlobalFoundries 45CLO or TSMC COUPE. Roadmap: 2028 first silicon photonic interposers, 2032 miniaturization for wearable, 2035 Pocket Watch X Gen3 fin bus.
