# TESTA-R-07: AegisC Ceramic Composite — Material Specification
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

The foundational material of all Testa containers. ZrB₂-SiC (Zirconium Diboride — Silicon Carbide) ceramic with 0.8% CNT dopant and Ti₃C₂Tₓ MXene spray (45μm). Density 4.2 g/cm³. Compressive strength 850 MPa. Flexural 1,100 MPa. Fracture toughness 12 MPa·m½. Dielectric 42 kV/mm. EMI shielding 92 dB SE. Thermal conductivity 3.2 W/m·K. Flash-sintered at 850°C in <5 sec (0.08 kWh/piece vs. 4.2 kWh conventional). Recycled shipping steel used as raw iron source for ZTA sintering aids — 3.2 tonnes steel recycled per Tier 1 container.
