# TESTA-R-22: Container School — Classroom in a Box
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

20ft container transforming into 25-student classroom. Contents: 25 Carr-Library tablets (mesh-networked), teacher station with 65" e-ink display, BFRP desks and chairs, Stellar-Tint windows (glare control), solar-powered ventilation. Deploy: 20 min by 2 people. Stack: 2 containers = 2 classrooms + shared bathroom container. Cost: $28,000 per classroom. Designed for: refugee camps, remote villages, disaster-recovery education, tsunami-zone schools that can be moved inland on 48-hour storm warning.
