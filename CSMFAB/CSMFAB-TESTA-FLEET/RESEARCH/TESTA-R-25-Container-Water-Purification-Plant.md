# TESTA-R-25: Container Water Purification Plant
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

20ft container producing 10,000 L/day potable water. Process: pre-filter (100μm) → ultrafiltration (0.01μm) → reverse osmosis → UV sterilization → remineralization. Source: seawater, brackish groundwater, or contaminated surface water. Power: 15kW solar + 50kWh LFP. Brine: zero liquid discharge — crystallizer produces salt blocks for Cold-Root PCM. Monitoring: S-Bus water quality sensors (turbidity, conductivity, pH, chlorine residual). Cost: $72,000. Serves: 2,500 people (WHO minimum 4L/person/day).
