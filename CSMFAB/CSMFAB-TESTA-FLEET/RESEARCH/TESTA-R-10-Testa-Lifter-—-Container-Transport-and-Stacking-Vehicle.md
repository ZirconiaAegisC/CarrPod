# TESTA-R-10: Testa-Lifter — Container Transport and Stacking Vehicle
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

Purpose-built all-terrain vehicle for container delivery, lifting, and stacking. Dimensions: 8.5m × 3.2m × 4.2m (stowed). Lifting capacity: 12,000 kg at 8m reach. Power: diesel-electric hybrid (compliant with existing infrastructure) or full-electric LFP (240 kWh, 8hr operation). Tires: BFRP run-flat, 1.5m diameter, 0.8 bar ground pressure. Modes: road (65 km/h), off-road (25 km/h), crawl (5 km/h — zero ground pressure increase over human footstep). Crane: 4-axis hydraulic with ZTA ceramic bearings and CNT-polymer hoses. Self-loading: loads container from dock, transports to site, lifts and stacks to 4 high. FEATHER mesh + S-Bus: autonomous waypoint delivery. Cost: $285,000.
