# TESTA-R-17: Recycling Existing Steel Containers into AegisC
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

Process for transforming the global fleet of 17 million steel shipping containers into AegisC Testa containers. Step 1: Container arrives at TestaForge facility. Step 2: Steel walls, roof, floor removed and sorted. Step 3: Steel sent to electric arc furnace — melted, alloyed with Zr and B to produce ZrB₂ precursor ingots (3.2 tonnes steel → 1.8 tonnes ZrB₂). Step 4: Corner castings retained — BFRP-MXene walls attached to existing ISO corner blocks (preserves intermodal compatibility). Step 5: New container emerges — same ISO footprint, 200 kg lighter, 50-year life, tsunami-rated. Cost: $5,800 per conversion (vs. $8,400 new). Carbon: 2.1 tonnes CO₂e per conversion (vs. 8.4 tonnes new steel container).
