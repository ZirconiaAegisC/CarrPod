# TESTA-R-49: Container Aquaponics — Fish and Plant Symbiotic System
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

40ft container with 5,000L fish tank (tilapia, 200 fish), 6-tier hydroponic grow beds, biofilter. Cycle: fish waste → bacteria → plant nutrients → clean water returns to fish. Production: 40 kg fish/month + 80 kg vegetables/month. Closed loop — 95% water recycled. Power: 8kW solar + 20kWh LFP. Cost: $52,000. Designed for: remote community protein production, educational demonstration, food sovereignty. The fish are happy. The plants are happy. The system is silent — Archimedean screw water pump.
