# TESTA-R-15: Container Floatation and Self-Righting Dynamics
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

Computational fluid dynamics analysis of AegisC container in wave impact scenarios. Container sealed: 27 tonnes displacement (20ft), 54 tonnes (40ft). Self-righting: active ballast tanks (4,000L total) with MRF valves. Gyroscopic sensor detects inversion >90° — floods low-side tanks, buoyancy moment arm = 1.2m × 265 kN = 318 kN·m. Righting time: 12 seconds from full inversion. Tested at University of Hawaii wave tank: 1:10 scale model survived 8× design wave height. Full-scale test: scheduled Q2 2027 at Oregon State O.H. Hinsdale Wave Research Laboratory.
