# TESTA-R-01: The Testa Fleet — Executive Vision
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

The complete shipping container transformation ecosystem. Named for the seed coat (testa) that protects the pea — the thin outer skin that shields the living embryo within from environmental damage, desiccation, and predation. The Testa Fleet applies this botanical principle to maritime logistics: a thin, ultra-strong AegisC ceramic outer skin protecting human life and cargo within. Five tiers of shipping container specification, from basic logistics through tsunami-rated survival pods to legendary-wall-of-water rated structures. Built on recycled shipping industry steel, transformed into BFRP-MXene-AegisC composite containers. 75 fabrication documents per tier. Complete logistics tool chain from port crane to customer backyard.
