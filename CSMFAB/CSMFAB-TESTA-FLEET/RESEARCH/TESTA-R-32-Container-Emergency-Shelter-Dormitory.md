# TESTA-R-32: Container Emergency Shelter Dormitory
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

40ft container with 20 bunks (stackable 3-high). Privacy curtains. Personal storage lockers (BFRP). Climate control: passive ventilation + Peltier heating/cooling. Lighting: LED, FEATHER mesh controlled. Sanitation: connects to Sanitation Container (R-23) via enclosed BFRP walkway. Cost: $42,000. Deploy: 30 min. Sleeps: 20 people. Stack: 2 containers = 40-person dormitory block. Designed for: refugee camps, disaster evacuation centers, remote worker housing.
