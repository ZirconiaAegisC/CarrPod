# TESTA-R-13: Container Wall Panel Quick-Release System
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

ZTA ceramic pin-and-wedge quick-release mechanism. Each wall panel: 4 pins (25mm dia, 60mm long). Panel removal: pull wedge tabs simultaneously — all 4 pins release. Panel insert: push into position — spring-loaded wedges auto-engage. No tools. 5 seconds per panel. Panel weight: 42 kg (2-person lift or Testa-Lifter assist). 24 panels per 20ft container = full disassembly in 2 minutes. Reassembly: 3 minutes. Cycle life: 10,000 insertions. Weather seal: hydrogel gasket (tardigrade-derived) — swells on water contact, self-seals. Tested to 200m depth.
