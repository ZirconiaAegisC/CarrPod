# TESTA-R-23: Container Sanitation System
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

20ft container with 8 toilets, 4 showers, 4 sinks, laundry. Water: 2,000L tank + Dew-Catcher replenishment + greywater recycling (90% recovery via membrane bioreactor). Waste: composting toilets (no water use) or vacuum flush (1L/flush). Power: solar thermal for hot water + 2kW PV for pumps and lighting. Odor control: activated carbon + UV photocatalytic oxidation. Cost: $32,000. Designed for: N-Node community hubs, Haven Clusters, refugee camps. One unit serves 80 people.
