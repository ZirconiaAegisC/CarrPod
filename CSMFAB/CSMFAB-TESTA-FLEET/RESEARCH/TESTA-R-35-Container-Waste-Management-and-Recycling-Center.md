# TESTA-R-35: Container Waste Management and Recycling Center
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | Deep Research | July 2026

40ft container processing 2 tonnes/day municipal waste. Systems: sorting conveyor, organic composter (2-week cycle → fertilizer), plastics shredder → BFRP filler feedstock, metals sorter → electric arc furnace feedstock, glass crusher → geopolymer aggregate. Residue: <5% to landfill. Power: self-powered from waste-to-energy gasifier (50kW). Cost: $185,000. Designed for: N-Node hubs, disaster camps, remote communities. Closes the loop — waste becomes resource.
