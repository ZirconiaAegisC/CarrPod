# SeedCoat Standard Container VOL-15: Hyper-Detailed Fabrication Specification
## CSMFAB-TESTA-FLEET | TIER-1-SEEDCOAT | Tardigrade-Structural-Pivot Architecture

Manufacturing process for SeedCoat Standard Container. Panel fabrication: VARTM (Vacuum-Assisted Resin Transfer Molding). Panel layup: 24-ply VARTM ([(0/90)₄ / (±45)₄]₂ + MXene glass scrim). Cure: room temp 24hr + post-cure 80°C/4hr. CNC trim ±0.5mm. Corner casting assembly: ZTA ball pressed into BFRP socket with 25kN preload. Door installation: ZTA hinge pins inserted, hydrogel gasket applied to continuous 3×3mm groove. QC: CMM full dimensional (±0.5mm all axes), hipot 50kV DC (<1μA leakage), IR >100MΩ at 1kV, load test 500T vertical + 200T lateral. Serial number laser-etched, linked to CSMTest database.. Quality control: 100% dimensional inspection via CMM (±0.5mm). Dielectric hipot test at 50kV DC (<1μA leakage). Insulation resistance >100MΩ at 1kV DC. Load test: 500T vertical per ISO 1496-1 + 200T lateral. Thermal cycle: -40°C/4hr → +85°C/4hr, 10 cycles. Salt spray: ASTM B117, 2,000 hours — no corrosion (ceramic/BFRP immune). Water spray: 50kPa for 1 hour, no interior ingress. UV exposure: ASTM G154, 5,000 hours — no degradation. Production line: Station 1 (panel VARTM, 8hr cure), Station 2 (CNC trim, 1hr), Station 3 (MXene spray, 30min), Station 4 (corner assembly, 2hr), Station 5 (door/gasket install, 1hr), Station 6 (QC + serialization, 30min). Total: 13 hours per container. Throughput: 4 containers/day (single shift), 8/day (double). Annual: 1,000 (single), 2,000 (double). Workforce: 24 technicians + 4 engineers + 2 QC inspectors. Floor space: 2,400 m². Tardigrade curl integration: MRF dampers installed at corner posts during Station 4. Preload verification: torque wrench check at 450 N·m ±5%, recorded in CSMTest database with serial number link. Each damper individually characterized (force-velocity curve, 0-800 mm/s range, 0.5% accuracy).
