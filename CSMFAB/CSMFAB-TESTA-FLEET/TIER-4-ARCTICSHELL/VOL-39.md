Deployment and operational specification for ArcticShell Extreme Container. Installation prerequisites: geopolymer foundation pad (CSM-MAT-GP-001, 85 MPa, 4m × 3m × 150mm, 7-day cure). Helical screw anchors (4×, 6m length, 75kN tension each). Flatbed truck or container ship delivery. Testa-Lifter (CSMFAB-TESTA-COM-002, 12T at 8m) or standard port container handler (reach stacker). Installation procedure: 1) Position pad, verify level ±2mm/m. 2) Crane container onto pad, engage corner casting locks. 3) Drive helical anchors at 4 corners, torque to 4,500 N·m (75kN preload). 4) Attach CNP tethers to anchor eyes. 5) Connect grid power (if available) or activate solar/battery. 6) FEATHER mesh auto-join, S-Bus registration. 7) Commission: door seal test, hipot insulation test, load test (2× rated floor load, 10min). User operation: for standard SeedCoat — treat as conventional container. For PodWall — deploy panels (20min, 2 people), connect utilities via quick-connect ports. For TsunamiGuard — always maintain at least 72hr supplies, test ballast valves monthly, verify FEATHER alert settings (auto-trigger at 5.0M tsunami warning). For ArcticShell — verify geothermal loop circulation, check PCM thermal battery charge state, ensure heated door seals functional at forecast < -20°C. For LegendaryWall — monthly systems check (life support, power, comms, seals), annual full mission simulation, 5-year major recertification. Maintenance schedule: quarterly visual inspection, annual dielectric test, 5-year gasket replacement, 10-year MXene respray (if exterior). 50-year structural inspection (ultrasonic wall thickness, dye penetrant for microcracks). Field repair kit (CSMFAB-TESTA-COM-007): BFRP patch fabric + epoxy, spare hydrogel gasket, ZTA pin replacement set, CNT-polymer wire splice kit, Core-1 diagnostic tool. Tardigrade curl maintenance: MRF damper fluid sample every 5 years — replace if iron particle sedimentation >2%. Pivot bearing lubrication: PTFE dry — no maintenance required.
