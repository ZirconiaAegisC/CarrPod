
# APPENDIX — GLOSSARY
## ACS-AMAZON-AWS-DATACENTER-RESILIENCE

**Availability Zone (AZ):** Physically separate data center cluster within an AWS region, with independent power, cooling, and networking.

**Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS):** Device that automatically transfers electrical load from primary (grid) to backup (generator) power source upon detecting an outage. Standard ATS controllers are vulnerable to GIC-induced power quality degradation.

**Immersion Cooling:** Data center cooling method where servers are submerged in dielectric (non-conductive) coolant liquid, which is circulated by electrically-driven pumps.

**Neutral-Blocking Capacitor (NBC):** Capacitor installed in transformer neutral-to-ground connection to block quasi-DC GIC while passing AC neutral current.

**Protonic Communications:** Backup communication system using proton-conducting ceramic transceivers that are immune to electromagnetic interference by physical principle — signals propagate via proton conduction, not electromagnetic radiation.

**Soil Resistivity:** Measure of how strongly soil resists electrical current flow, expressed in ohm-meters. High-resistivity geology (igneous/metamorphic rock) amplifies GIC coupling by 5-10x compared to low-resistivity (sedimentary) geology.

**Williams Heuristic:** CSM editorial standard requiring every technical claim to be paired with a plain-language explanation.
