## Business Model

Semtech designs, develops, and markets high-performance analog and mixed-signal semiconductors, advanced algorithms, and wireless connectivity solutions. The company operates through three reportable segments, each serving distinct end markets with proprietary technology moats.

## Segment Overview

### Signal Integrity (30.8% of FY2026 Revenue)
High-speed optical and copper interconnect solutions for data centers, enterprise networking, and telecom infrastructure. Products include FiberEdge transimpedance amplifiers, laser drivers, clock and data recovery ICs, and CopperEdge re-drivers and retimers. Segment margin of 65.2% reflects strong pricing power and technology leadership in 800G and emerging 1.6T deployments.

### Analog Mixed Signal & Wireless (35.5% of FY2026 Revenue)
LoRa-enabled radio transceivers and chipsets for long-range low-power IoT connectivity, protection diodes and TVS devices for high-speed interfaces, wireless charging solutions, and video transport products. Segment margin of 58.9% demonstrates the value of proprietary LoRa IP and broad protection product portfolio.

### IoT Systems & Connectivity (33.7% of FY2026 Revenue)
Complete IoT solutions including LoRa modules, gateways, routers, and the AirLink cellular router product line. Also includes cloud-based connectivity management and device provisioning platforms. Segment margin of 35.5% reflects the hardware-centric nature of this business.

## Technology Leadership

Semtech's LoRa technology represents the industry-leading long-range, low-power wide-area network (LPWAN) standard. With millions of devices deployed globally, LoRa enables smart city infrastructure, precision agriculture, asset tracking, building automation, and environmental monitoring. The LoRa Alliance, comprising over 500 member companies, ensures a robust, interoperable ecosystem for continued adoption.

## Financial Trajectory

Semtech delivered record revenue of $291 million in Q1 FY2027, representing 16% year-over-year growth. Adjusted EPS of $0.51 grew 34% year-over-year, and adjusted gross margin reached 53%. Free cash flow generation improved dramatically, with FY2026 FCF of $171.4 million representing a 3x increase over the prior year. Adjusted EBITDA of $232 million yielded a 22.1% margin in FY2026.

## Strategic Positioning

The company is strategically positioned at the intersection of two major secular growth trends: the exponential expansion of data center interconnect bandwidth driven by hyperscale AI infrastructure buildout, and the proliferation of connected IoT devices enabled by LoRa technology. Semtech's diversified portfolio provides multiple paths to sustained double-digit revenue growth and margin expansion.
