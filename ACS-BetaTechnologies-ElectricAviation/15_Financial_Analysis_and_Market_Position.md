### VALUATION CONTEXT

At ~$18/share, Beta trades at approximately 50% below its $36 IPO price. In the context of a $3.9 billion backlog, a fully funded certification pathway, established charging infrastructure, and a first-mover regulatory position, the current valuation reflects market skepticism about electric aviation commercialization timelines rather than company-specific execution risk. Key catalysts for value realization include:

1. H500A Type Certificate issuance (H1 2026)
2. CX300 Type Certificate issuance (H2 2027)
3. First commercial delivery and revenue recognition
4. MV250 Program of Record transition
5. Eve Air Mobility motor delivery commencement
6. Additional international flag-carrier customer announcements
