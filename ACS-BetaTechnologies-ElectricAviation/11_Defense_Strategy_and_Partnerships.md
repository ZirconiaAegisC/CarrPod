### USAF AGILITY PRIME

Agility Prime is the U.S. Air Force's flagship program for accelerating commercial electric VTOL technology for military applications. Beta's participation provides:

- **Non-dilutive funding** — Government funding that does not require equity issuance, preserving shareholder value
- **Military airworthiness** — Air Force airworthiness assessment parallel to FAA certification, providing a secondary validation pathway
- **Test infrastructure** — Access to military test ranges, instrumentation, and subject-matter experts
- **Procurement pathway** — Agility Prime serves as a bridge to formal acquisition programs of record

### NAVY UNDERSEA PROPULSION (GENERAL DYNAMICS)

The General Dynamics undersea propulsion partnership (detailed in File 06) provides Beta entry into the U.S. Navy submarine and UUV propulsion market, which is undergoing a generational transition from mechanically geared turbine propulsion to integrated electric drive (IED). The Navy's Columbia-class submarine program alone represents a $100B+ acquisition program, with electric drive components constituting a significant subsystem opportunity.

### STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES IN DEFENSE

**Acoustic Signature.** Electric aircraft offer an inherently lower acoustic signature than turbine-powered alternatives, a critical advantage for special operations and contested logistics scenarios where detection avoidance is paramount. Beta's 135,000+ nautical miles of flight data includes acoustic characterization that informs military mission planning.

**Thermal Signature.** Electric propulsion generates substantially lower infrared signatures than gas turbines, reducing vulnerability to IR-guided threats. For the MV250 operating in contested environments, this survivability advantage is a primary differentiator versus conventional logistics UAVs.

**Logistics Footprint Reduction.** The DoD's Operational Energy Strategy identifies fuel logistics as the single largest vulnerability in expeditionary operations. An electric aircraft that recharges from distributed generation (tactical microgrids, mobile nuclear, or solar) eliminates the fuel supply chain entirely, reducing the logistics burden by an estimated 70%.

**Dual-Use Technology.** Beta's commercial certification effort under Part 23 generates safety data, reliability statistics, and operational procedures directly applicable to military airworthiness. This dual-use approach reduces military qualification costs and accelerates fielding timelines relative to defense-unique development programs.

### RISK CONSIDERATIONS

- Defense acquisition timelines are inherently unpredictable and subject to congressional appropriations cycles
- Transition from OTA/prototype to Program of Record is not guaranteed; many Agility Prime participants have not secured POR status
- Competing logistics UAVs (Elroy Air, Silent Arrow, Malloy Aeronautics) address overlapping mission requirements
- ITAR compliance imposes operational overhead and constrains international commercial partnerships
- DoD budget prioritization may shift with changes in administration and strategic environment
