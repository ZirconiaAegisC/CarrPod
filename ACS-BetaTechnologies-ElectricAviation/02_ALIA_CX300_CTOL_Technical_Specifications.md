# ALIA CX300 CTOL — TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
## Beta Technologies Corporation — ACS Dossier File 02

---

### CONFIGURATION

The ALIA CX300 is a single-pilot, all-electric, fixed-wing aircraft designed for Conventional Takeoff and Landing (CTOL) operations. The aircraft employs a high-aspect-ratio composite wing structure with wingtip-mounted vertical stabilizers, a T-tail empennage, and a semi-monocoque composite fuselage optimized for low-speed aerodynamic efficiency with a laminar-flow wing profile.

### CERTIFICATION BASIS

The CX300 is being certified under 14 CFR Part 23, Amendment 64 (Normal Category), leveraging FAA Advisory Circular AC 21.17-4 for type certification of powered-lift and electric aircraft. Beta Technologies was the first eVTOL developer selected for the FAA's tailored certification pathway under AC 21.17-4. The certification strategy employs a phased approach: airframe structures and mechanical systems certification, followed by propulsion and energy storage systems, culminating in full aircraft type certification.

### MISSION PROFILES
