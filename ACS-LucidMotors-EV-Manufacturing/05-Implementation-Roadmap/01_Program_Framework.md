# 05.01 — Implementation Roadmap & Program Framework

## 5.1 Strategic Objective

Achieve free cash flow breakeven at Lucid Group through coordinated execution across manufacturing scale, product portfolio expansion, autonomous mobility commercialization, and capital structure optimization. Target: positive FCF by FY2029 (130,000–150,000 annual unit production).

---

## 5.3 Critical Path Dependencies

The program's critical path runs through the following dependencies:

```
Gravity Production Ramp
    └─→ FY2026 Revenue Bridge
            └─→ 2026 Convert Refinancing
                    └─→ Capital to Fund Midsize Tooling (2027)
                            └─→ Midsize Volume Production (2027–2028)
                                    └─→ Volume Absorption → Gross Margin Breakeven
                                            └─→ FCF Breakeven (2029)
```

**Single-Point Failures:**

1. **Gravity demand sustainability:** If Gravity order rates decline post-initial reservation fulfillment, the entire FY2026–2027 revenue bridge collapses, and the Midsize investment cannot be funded from operations.
2. **2026 Convert refinancing failure:** If Lucid cannot refinance the $1.75B December 2026 maturity, a liquidity crisis ensues. PIF backstop is probable but not guaranteed in form or timing.
3. **Midsize platform delay beyond H2 2027:** Every quarter of delay defers the volume absorption thesis by an equivalent period, extending the cash burn runway and the date of FCF breakeven.
4. **AMP-2 Phase 2 construction/equipment delay:** If CBU production slips from 2028 to 2029, the 2029 FCF breakeven target is likely unreachable, as AMP-1 alone at 90K units generates approximately $(0.5)B FCF.
5. **Battery cell cost trajectory:** If pack costs remain above $100/kWh through 2028, the Midsize platform's sub-$50K entry price becomes economically infeasible without negative unit margins that undermine the volume thesis.
