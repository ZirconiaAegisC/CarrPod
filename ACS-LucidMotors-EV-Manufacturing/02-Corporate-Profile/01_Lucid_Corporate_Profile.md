### 2.3 Business Model

Lucid operates a hybrid distribution model:

- **North America (United States, Canada):** Direct-to-consumer (D2C) through company-owned Studios and service centers. Forty-five locations operational across North America as of Q1 2026. Online configurator with home delivery logistics.
- **Europe, Middle East, Africa (EMEA):** Importer/dealer/agent model through regional partners. Twelve European locations (Germany, Netherlands, Switzerland, Norway, others) and five Middle East locations (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain) operational.
- **Asia-Pacific:** Exploratory. No material presence as of mid-2026.

**Revenue Streams:**

1. Vehicle sales (primary): Lucid Air sedan, Lucid Gravity SUV.
2. Atlas electric drive unit supply to external OEMs.
3. Autonomous mobility services (robotaxi revenue share with Uber, projected late 2026).
4. Regulatory credit sales (ZEV, CAFE, GHG).
5. Aftermarket parts, service, and accessories.

### 2.4 Competitive Landscape

Lucid competes in the premium/luxury EV segment (ASP $80,000–$250,000). Primary competitors:

| Competitor | Overlap | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Tesla (Model S, Model X) | Full-size sedan/SUV luxury | Incumbent leader; scale and charging network advantages |
| Mercedes-Benz (EQS, EQE SUV) | Full-size luxury EV | Brand equity; global dealer network |
| BMW (i7, iX) | Full-size luxury EV | Established EV platform; strong European presence |
| Porsche (Taycan, Macan EV) | Performance luxury EV | Brand prestige; driving dynamics focus |
| Rivian (R1S) | Premium SUV | Adventure/lifestyle positioning |
| Cadillac (Celestiq, Lyriq) | Luxury EV | GM scale; Ultium platform |

Lucid's competitive differentiation rests on: (1) industry-leading efficiency (Air Pure: 5.0 mi/kWh, 420-mile EPA range, best-in-class), (2) proprietary 900V+ electrical architecture, (3) vertically integrated electric drive unit manufacturing, (4) dual-manufacturing-continent footprint with sovereign backing.

### 2.6 Regulatory & Legal

- **SEC Filings:** 10-K filed February 2026; 10-Q for Q1 2026 filed May 2026.
- **CA DMV Autonomous Vehicle Testing Permit:** Granted Q2 2026 (driverless testing with safety driver).
- **NHTSA:** Ongoing compliance with FMVSS standards for Air and Gravity platforms.
- **EPA/CARB:** ZEV credit generation from vehicle sales; credits sold to other OEMs.
- **Export Controls:** Technology transfer from AMP-1 to AMP-2 subject to U.S. Department of Commerce export licensing for advanced EV powertrain components. Lucid has secured required licenses for SKD operations.
- **Litigation:** Standard securities class actions related to SPAC merger (2021–2022) substantially resolved. No material pending litigation as of Q1 2026.
