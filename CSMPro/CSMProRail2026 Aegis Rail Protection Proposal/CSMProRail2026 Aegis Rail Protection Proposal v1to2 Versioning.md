# CSMProRail2026 — Aegis Rail Protection Proposal
## V1.0 to V2.0 Versioning Delta
**Type:** Scientific Change Log | **Date:** June 2026 | **Applies to:** `CSMProRail2026 — Aegis Rail Protection Proposal V2.0`

---

## Revision Necessity — Three Drivers

1. **Empirical data superseded** — 2024-2025 peer-reviewed publications and measured data invalidate V1.0 specifications
2. **Heliophysical risk recalibrated** — Solar Cycle 25 SSN ~137 (observed) exceeds ~115 (V1.0 forecast); design basis updated with revised geoelectric field values
3. **Standards/regulations updated** — One or more referenced codes superseded since V1.0

---

## V1.0 vs V2.0 — Specific Parameter Changes

| Parameter | V1.0 | V2.0 | Reason | Primary Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Rail GIC quantification** | General GIC threat described | LA-SF rail (615 km) at LA Basin amplified field: 26,445 V DC, 1,524 A, 40 MW total | Amplified LA Basin field (SC25 adjusted) increases threat 4.3× vs national average assumed in V1.0 | USGS 2024; NOAA SWPC Q1 2026 |
| **BFRP catenary mast matrix** | Thermoset epoxy (no recyclability pathway) | Elium® thermoplastic (95% fiber + 100% monomer recovery via solvolysis 350°C) | Phoenix Protocol mandate; thermoset masts have no viable EOL recovery | Arkema Elium® TDS 2025 |
| **Caltrain substation hardening** | DC blocking capacitor concept described | 500 μF, 12 kV ZrO₂ ceramic capacitor bank at each Caltrain neutral; $2.04M total cost | Specific implementation path, component spec, and cost now documented for 11 substations | Caltrain 2×25 kV topology 2025 data |
| **Signal equipment shielding** | Steel cabinet SE ~45 dB | Ti₃C₂Tₓ MXene interior spray SE 92 dB ($460/cabinet) | 47 dB deficit eliminates compliance with MIL-STD-461; MXene resolves at confirmed cost | ACS Appl. Mater. Interfaces 2025 |
| **Track circuit replacement** | Concept referenced | LoRa axle counter mesh: $8,400/km vs $12,000/km conventional track circuit, GPS-free | Conventional track circuits use DC through rail — exactly the GIC path being eliminated | LoRa Alliance v1.1 |
| **Solar Cycle 25** | Generic solar maximum framing | SSN ~137; Caltrain catenary GIC voltage +20% vs V1.0 (26,445 V vs 22,038 V) | Risk quantification requires updated SSN basis for all design voltages | NOAA SWPC Q1 2026 |
