## V1.0 vs V2.0 Parameter Changes

| Parameter | V1.0 | V2.0 | Consequence if Not Updated |
|---|---|---|---|

| **Thames amplification** | 3.2x estimate | BGS 2024 survey confirmed 3.2x London Clay amplification | V1.0 estimate confirmed; V2.0 provides hard data for insurance claims |
| **Tower Bridge bascule** | General steel failure mode | Hydraulic seal failure from 78,000 A GIC -> bridge drops open | V1.0 missed critical public safety failure mode |
| **Hammersmith repair** | General CFRP concept | 2024 reopening: first London bridge with structural CFRP confirmed | V1.0 was aspirational; V2.0 cites confirmed precedent in London |
| **NESO recommendation** | Not in V1.0 | UK NESO (2024): formally recommends GIC bridge protection | UK government endorsement post V1.0 |
