**Corrected Indirect Costs Section:**

### 7.0 INDIRECT COSTS — $20,000

Indirect costs are requested at a rate of 20% of Modified Total Direct Costs (MTDC), consistent with the de minimis rate for small businesses without a negotiated indirect cost rate agreement (NICRA) under 2 CFR 200.414(f).

**MTDC Calculation:**
- Direct Labor: $74,600
- Fringe Benefits: $18,650
- Materials & Supplies: $26,950
- Travel: $3,500
- Other Direct Costs: $6,300
- Less Excluded Items (Equipment, Capital Expenditures): $0 (no equipment purchased)
- **MTDC Base: $130,000**

At the 20% de minimis rate, the calculated indirect cost amount is $26,000 (20% × $130,000 MTDC), which would produce a total budget request of $156,000. To remain within the $150,000 Phase I funding limit, CSM is voluntarily absorbing **$6,000 of indirect costs as cost share**. The amount requested for indirect costs is therefore **$20,000**, reflecting an effective indirect cost recovery rate of approximately 15.4%.

Indirect costs cover organizational expenses not directly allocable to the project, including: general administrative support, accounting and financial management, IT infrastructure (servers, cloud storage for the 6,302-document research corpus), facility utilities (electricity for laboratory equipment, internet connectivity), insurance (general liability, professional liability), and general office supplies.

CSM will maintain time and effort reporting for all personnel and will track indirect cost pools in accordance with 2 CFR 200, Subpart E, should the agency request supporting documentation.
