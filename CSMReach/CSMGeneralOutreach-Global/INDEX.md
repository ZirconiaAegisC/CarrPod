## CSMGeneralOutreach-Global — Complete Country Index (200 Nations)

This directory contains 200 individually tailored outreach letters to the
governments and electrical authorities of every nation and select observer
territories, ranked and prioritized by CME vulnerability based on grid
exposure to geomagnetic induced currents (GIC), metallurgical infrastructure
density, population concentration, and healthcare-system fragility.

Each letter is authored under the Williams Heuristic Communication Standard
(CSMSOPP000001, MODULE-V2) and follows the bifurcated Williams Register /
Core Deliverable structure employed throughout the CSM outreach corpus.
