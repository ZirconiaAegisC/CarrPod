SUPERPARTICULATE ENGINE — Physics Attribution

The particle catalog and worldline evolution model draws from:
  CODATA 2022 recommended values
  KATRIN 2024 — neutrino mass bound < 0.45 eV
  HAYSTAC 2023 — axion dark matter search
  LHC Run 3 2024 — neutralino mass exclusion > 900 GeV
  FNAL Muon g-2 final result 2023
  CMS 2024 — W boson mass 80.360 GeV
  sPHENIX 2024 — gluon orbital angular momentum
  Husain et al. Science 380 (2023) — Pines Demon confirmation
  LIGO O4 2024 — graviton mass bound < 1.27×10⁻²³ eV
  JWST 2023-2024 — fuzzy dark matter constraints
  FLAG 2024 — lattice QCD precision (m_u, m_d, F_π)
  NA62 2024 — K→πνν̄ branching ratio
  NIF 2024 — fusion ignition Q > 1 (3.15 MJ)

All physics data values current as of 2026-07-07.
