
# APPENDIX A — GLOSSARY OF TERMS
## ACS-TESLA-GIGAFACTORY-PROTECTION

**AEGIS Protocol:** The operational framework under which Carrington Storm Motors and its subsidiary ACS coordinate infrastructure protection activities. Named for the mythological shield of Zeus.

**Carrington Event:** The September 1859 geomagnetic storm — the most intense on record. Caused telegraph system failures globally, aurorae visible at equatorial latitudes, and would devastate modern electrical infrastructure if repeated.

**Coronal Mass Ejection (CME):** A massive release of plasma and magnetic field from the Sun's corona. When Earth-directed, CMEs can cause geomagnetic storms upon impact with Earth's magnetosphere.

**Deep Space Climate Observatory (DSCOVR):** NOAA satellite stationed at L1 Lagrange point (~1.5 million km sunward of Earth), providing 15-60 minutes warning of CME arrival.

**Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA):** Diagnostic technique measuring gases dissolved in transformer oil to detect thermal faults, partial discharge, and insulation degradation.

**Geomagnetically Induced Current (GIC):** Quasi-DC current induced in long conductors (transmission lines, pipelines, railways) during geomagnetic disturbances. Frequencies from DC to ~0.1 Hz.

**Half-Cycle Saturation:** Transformer operating condition during GIC events where the core saturates on one half of each AC cycle, producing harmonics, reactive power consumption, and heating.

**Harmonic Distortion:** Deviation of voltage or current waveform from pure sine wave. Expressed as Total Harmonic Distortion percentage. Formation lines require <1% THD.

**MXene (Ti3C2Tx):** Two-dimensional transition metal carbide developed by CSM's materials science division. Provides exceptional capacitance density, electromagnetic shielding, and electrical conductivity.

**Neutral-Blocking Capacitor (NBC):** Capacitor installed in transformer neutral-to-ground connection, blocking quasi-DC GIC while passing AC neutral current.

**Phantom Robot (CSM-PR-900):** Autonomous inspection and monitoring platform for electrical infrastructure.

**Protonic Communications (CSM-PC-5000):** Backup communication system using proton-conducting ceramic transceivers, immune to electromagnetic interference.

**Total Harmonic Distortion (THD):** Ratio of harmonic content to fundamental frequency, expressed as percentage. IEC 61000-3-2 Class A limits industrial THD to 5-8%, but formation lines require <1%.

**Williams Heuristic:** CSM's editorial standard requiring every technical claim to be accompanied by a plain-language explanation accessible to non-experts.

**El Segundo Calm:** CSM's professional communication standard — steady, measured, non-alarmist presentation of technical risks.

**Accountant Heuristic:** CSM's financial analysis standard requiring every protection measure to carry an explicit cost, expected loss prevention, and ROI calculation.
