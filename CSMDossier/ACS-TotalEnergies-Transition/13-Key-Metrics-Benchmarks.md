## Key Monitoring Indicators for Investors

1. **Brent Crude Oil Price** — Primary driver of E&P and trading earnings.
   Sensitivities: ~$2.7 billion in annual CFFO per $10/bbl change.

2. **TTF / Henry Hub / JKM Gas Prices** — Drive Integrated LNG earnings.
   TTF-JKM spread is a key indicator of trading profit opportunity.

3. **European Refining Margins** — Indicator of Refining & Chemicals
   segment profitability. Total benchmark margin (TBM).

4. **Quarterly Renewable Capacity Additions** — Progress toward 35 GW by
   2025 and 100 GW by 2030 targets.

5. **Methane Emissions Data** — Key ESG metric monitored by investors.
   -50% by 2025 and -80% by 2030 targets.

6. **Mozambique LNG Security Situation** — Binary catalyst for restart
   of a major growth project.

7. **EU Carbon Price (EUA)** — Impacts refining margins and CCS economics.
