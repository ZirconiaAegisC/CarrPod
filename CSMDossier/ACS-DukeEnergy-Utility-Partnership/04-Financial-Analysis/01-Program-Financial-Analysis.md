## 1. PROGRAM COST

| Phase | Cost |
|-------|------|
| Phase 0 — 12-site assessment | $2.1M |
| Phase 1 — 12 pilot substation NBCs | $18.4M |

## 2. RATE IMPACT

Amortized over 25 years at Duke's weighted cost of capital (~5.5%): ~$14.5M/year. Across 8.2M customers: ~$0.09/month per residential customer.

Estimated annual transmission insurance premium reduction: $4.2-8.5M. Over 25 years: $105-213M. Covers 49-99% of the $214.5M program cost before any loss prevention is counted.
