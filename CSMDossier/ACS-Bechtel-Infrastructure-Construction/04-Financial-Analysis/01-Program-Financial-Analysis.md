## 1. PARTNERSHIP ECONOMICS

**Revenue Model for ACS:**
- MXene material supply to Bechtel projects (per-ton pricing)
- AEGIS certification auditing fees (per-project)
- Engineering services and technical support
- Joint IP from construction material innovations

**Revenue Model for Bechtel:**
- 8-15% premium on BFRP rebar scopes
- 5-10% premium on geopolymer concrete scopes
- AEGIS certification as bid differentiator
- IIJA resilience funding capture (Bechtel projects with AEGIS certification qualify for resilience grants)
- Lifecycle maintenance contracts (Bechtel + ACS joint service)
