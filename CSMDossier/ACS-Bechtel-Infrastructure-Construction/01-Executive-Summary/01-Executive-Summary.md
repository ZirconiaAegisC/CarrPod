
# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
## ACS-BECHTEL-INFRASTRUCTURE-CONSTRUCTION
### AEGIS Dielectric Standards for Global Infrastructure

**CLASSIFICATION: COMMERCIAL-IN-CONFIDENCE**

## 1. THE STRATEGIC OPPORTUNITY

Bechtel Corporation — $30B+ annual revenue, 25+ active major projects in 40+ countries — is the world's premier infrastructure construction company. ACS proposes a partnership to embed AEGIS dielectric protection standards into all new Bechtel infrastructure projects. This transforms Bechtel from a builder of infrastructure into the delivery mechanism for GIC-resilient infrastructure worldwide.

**The IIJA Convergence:** $46B+ in authorized federal infrastructure funding is eligible for AEGIS-certified hardening under the 2021 Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act. Bechtel is the natural vehicle for delivering these resilience-embedded projects. No other contractor offers GIC-hardened construction standards.

## 2. THE ACS-BECHTEL VALUE PROPOSITION

1. **BFRP Rebar Standard:** Replace steel rebar with dielectric basalt fiber reinforced polymer in all new bridge decks, tunnel linings, and marine structures. 8-15% cost premium, >100-year service life vs. 40-75 for steel. MXene-coated for enhanced bonding.

2. **Geopolymer Concrete Standard:** Replace Portland cement with MXene-enhanced geopolymer concrete. 100-1,000x higher electrical resistivity, eliminates concrete as GIC conduction path. 70% lower carbon footprint — aligns with Bechtel's sustainability commitments.

3. **Dielectric Bearing Pads:** MXene-infused elastomeric bearings at all structural interfaces. Prevents GIC traversal across structural boundaries.

4. **AEGIS Certification:** Tiered (Silver/Gold/Platinum) certification creating premium project differentiation and IIJA funding eligibility.
