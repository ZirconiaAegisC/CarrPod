# 03 — Financial Overview: FY2026 (Year Ended January 2026)

## Top-Line Performance

Semtech reported total revenue of $1.05 billion for fiscal year 2026, representing year-over-year growth of 15%. This milestone marked the first time the company exceeded the billion-dollar annual revenue threshold, reflecting broad-based growth across all three business segments and successful execution in high-demand end markets.

## Revenue by Segment

| Segment | FY2026 Revenue | Revenue Share | Segment Margin |
|---------|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| Signal Integrity | $323 million | 30.8% | 65.2% |
| Analog Mixed Signal & Wireless | $373 million | 35.5% | 58.9% |
| IoT Systems & Connectivity | $354 million | 33.7% | 35.5% |
| **Total** | **$1,050 million** | **100%** | — |

The Signal Integrity segment achieved the highest contribution margin at 65.2%, driven by strong demand from hyperscale data center customers deploying 800G optical interconnects and the initial ramp of 1.6T solutions. The Analog Mixed Signal & Wireless segment contributed the largest absolute revenue share at $373 million, benefiting from robust LoRa radio transceiver shipments and steady protection product demand. IoT Systems & Connectivity generated $354 million with a 35.5% segment margin, reflecting the hardware-heavy nature of module and gateway products.

## Profitability Metrics

| Metric | FY2026 | FY2025 | YoY Change |
|--------|--------|--------|------------|
| Adjusted Gross Margin | 52.3% | 51.1% | +120 bps |
| Adjusted Operating Margin | 17.4% | 15.8% | +160 bps |
| Adjusted EBITDA | $232 million | $189 million | +22.8% |
| Adjusted EBITDA Margin | 22.1% | 20.4% | +170 bps |
| Adjusted EPS | $1.71 | $1.28 | +33.6% |

Adjusted gross margin expanded 120 basis points year-over-year to 52.3%, driven by favorable product mix shifting toward higher-margin Signal Integrity products and operating leverage on increased revenue scale. Adjusted EBITDA of $232 million yielded a 22.1% margin, reflecting disciplined operating expense management and the inherent scalability of the fabless semiconductor model.

## Cash Flow

| Metric | FY2026 | FY2025 | YoY Change |
|--------|--------|--------|------------|
| Operating Cash Flow | $213 million | $122 million | +74.6% |
| Capital Expenditures | $41.6 million | $38.2 million | +8.9% |
| Free Cash Flow | $171.4 million | $83.8 million | +104.5% |
| FCF Margin | 16.3% | 9.1% | +720 bps |

Free cash flow generation was a standout metric in FY2026, more than tripling year-over-year to $171.4 million. The 16.3% FCF margin represented a substantial improvement over the prior year and provided significant optionality for capital allocation including debt reduction, share repurchases, and strategic investments.

## Capital Allocation Strategy

Semtech's management articulated a balanced capital allocation framework for FY2026 and beyond:

1. **Organic Investment** — Continued R&D funding across all three segments, with particular emphasis on 1.6T optical interconnect development and LoRa ecosystem expansion.
2. **Debt Management** — Prioritization of gross debt reduction using excess free cash flow, targeting a net leverage ratio below 2.0x adjusted EBITDA.
3. **Strategic M&A** — Opportunistic evaluation of bolt-on acquisitions in adjacent analog, mixed-signal, and connectivity markets that strengthen technology portfolios or customer access.
4. **Shareholder Returns** — Potential initiation of share repurchase programs as balance sheet metrics achieve target thresholds.

## Balance Sheet Highlights

Semtech ended FY2026 with a strengthened balance sheet, reflecting the significant free cash flow improvement and disciplined working capital management. Inventory levels were stabilized following the post-pandemic normalization cycle, and accounts receivable days improved sequentially. The company maintained ample liquidity through cash reserves and an undrawn revolving credit facility.

## Revenue Mix Analysis

Semtech's revenue base is balanced across product categories, with no single product or customer representing more than 10% of total revenue. The mix of semiconductor components (Signal Integrity and Analog segments, combined ~66% of revenue) and systems hardware (IoT Systems, ~34% of revenue) provides diversification benefits. Semiconductor component revenue carries substantially higher gross margins, while systems hardware revenue provides deeper customer relationships and a recurring revenue dimension through cloud subscription services.
