# 02 — Corporate Profile

## Company Overview

Semtech Corporation is a leading global supplier of high-performance analog and mixed-signal semiconductors and advanced algorithms. The company's products are deployed across infrastructure, high-end consumer, and industrial equipment markets. Semtech's technology enables the performance, connectivity, and protection of electronic systems in data centers, enterprise networks, smart cities, industrial IoT, and mobile devices.

## Headquarters and Operations

Semtech is headquartered in Camarillo, California, with design centers, sales offices, and manufacturing partnerships spanning North America, Europe, and Asia. The company operates a fabless semiconductor model, contracting wafer fabrication to leading third-party foundries while retaining proprietary design, testing, and qualification capabilities.

## Leadership

### Hong Hou — Chief Executive Officer
Hong Hou joined Semtech as President and CEO, bringing decades of semiconductor industry leadership experience. Under his direction, the company has sharpened its strategic focus on high-growth markets including data center interconnects and IoT connectivity, while driving operational efficiencies that have expanded gross and operating margins.

### Mark Lin — Chief Financial Officer
Mark Lin serves as Executive Vice President and CFO, overseeing all financial operations, investor relations, and strategic planning. During his tenure, Semtech has significantly improved free cash flow generation and strengthened its balance sheet.

## Corporate History

Founded in 1960, Semtech has evolved from a niche analog component supplier into a diversified technology leader. Key milestones include the development of proprietary protection diode platforms, the acquisition of LoRa intellectual property and wireless product lines, the expansion into optical interconnect technologies, and the strategic buildout of an end-to-end IoT systems business spanning hardware, software, and cloud services.

## Public Listing

Semtech trades on the Nasdaq Global Select Market under the ticker SMTC. With a market capitalization of approximately $4 billion, the company is classified as a mid-cap technology stock. The stock is held by institutional investors, sector-focused growth funds, and thematic ETFs targeting semiconductor and IoT exposure.

The significant concentration of revenue from China and Hong Kong — while reflective of the region's dominance in electronics manufacturing and IoT deployment — represents a key risk factor subject to geopolitical tensions, trade restrictions, and economic cyclicality. Semtech management has acknowledged this concentration and is pursuing geographic diversification strategies.

## Employee Base

Semtech employs a global workforce of engineers, sales professionals, and operations staff. The company maintains a culture of innovation, with a substantial portion of employees dedicated to research and development across its three business segments.

## Fabless Manufacturing Model

Semtech follows a fabless semiconductor business model, outsourcing wafer fabrication to leading foundry partners including TSMC and other advanced process technology providers. This capital-light approach allows the company to focus resources on design, intellectual property development, applications engineering, and customer support while avoiding the multibillion-dollar costs of owning and operating wafer fabrication facilities.

## Intellectual Property Portfolio

Semtech holds an extensive portfolio of patents covering:
- Optical and copper interconnect circuit design
- LoRa modulation and protocol technology
- Protection diode structures and manufacturing processes
- Wireless charging power management
- Video transport and signal processing algorithms

The patent portfolio creates competitive moats across all three business segments and generates licensing revenue streams, particularly in the LoRa ecosystem where Semtech licenses core IP to third-party chip designers through the LoRa Alliance framework.

## Corporate Governance

Semtech maintains a Board of Directors composed of independent directors with expertise spanning semiconductors, enterprise technology, finance, and global operations. The board oversees executive compensation, audit functions, risk management, and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) initiatives. The company has publicly committed to decarbonization applications enabled by its LoRa technology portfolio.

## R&D and Innovation Investment

Research and development is central to Semtech's competitive strategy. The company invests approximately 18-20% of revenue in R&D, spanning advanced CMOS and SiGe process technologies for optical interconnects, LoRa modulation algorithm development, protection device miniaturization, and cloud platform software engineering. R&D centers are distributed globally to access specialized engineering talent, with major design facilities in North America, Switzerland, and Asia.
