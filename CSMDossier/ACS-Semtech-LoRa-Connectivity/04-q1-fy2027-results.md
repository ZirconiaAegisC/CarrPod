# 04 — Q1 FY2027 Results & Q2 FY2027 Guidance

## Q1 FY2027: Record Quarter

Semtech reported record quarterly revenue of $291 million for the first quarter of fiscal year 2027 (period ending April 2026), representing year-over-year growth of 16%. The results exceeded the midpoint of management's guidance range, driven by sustained demand strength in the Signal Integrity segment and steady performance of the IoT and analog wireless businesses.

## Revenue Growth Decomposition

| Component | Q1 FY2027 | Q1 FY2026 | YoY Change |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Total Revenue | $291 million | $251 million | +16% |
| Signal Integrity | ~$95 million | ~$78 million | +22% |
| Analog Mixed Signal & Wireless | ~$104 million | ~$94 million | +11% |
| IoT Systems & Connectivity | ~$92 million | ~$79 million | +16% |

Signal Integrity posted the strongest year-over-year growth at approximately 22%, reflecting the ramp of 1.6T optical interconnect products and continued 800G volume demand from hyperscale data center customers. IoT Systems & Connectivity grew approximately 16%, benefiting from LoRa gateway deployments and AirLink cellular router demand. Analog Mixed Signal & Wireless delivered approximately 11% growth, supported by steady LoRa transceiver volumes and protection product shipments.

## Profitability

| Metric | Q1 FY2027 | Q1 FY2026 | YoY Change |
|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Adjusted Gross Margin | 53.0% | 51.5% | +150 bps |
| Adjusted Operating Margin | 18.8% | 16.9% | +190 bps |
| Adjusted EPS | $0.51 | $0.38 | +34.2% |

Adjusted gross margin reached 53.0% in Q1 FY2027, a 150-basis-point improvement year-over-year and a sequential increase from the FY2026 average of 52.3%. The gross margin expansion was primarily attributable to a favorable product mix shift toward higher-margin Signal Integrity products and improved manufacturing efficiencies. Adjusted EPS of $0.51 grew 34% year-over-year, comfortably exceeding consensus analyst estimates.

## Operating Expense Leverage

Semtech demonstrated meaningful operating leverage during Q1 FY2027. While R&D spending increased year-over-year in absolute dollars to support next-generation optical interconnect and LoRa product development, R&D as a percentage of revenue declined slightly. SG&A expenses grew at a rate below revenue growth, reflecting the scalability of the company's go-to-market organization and tight cost controls.

## Balance Sheet and Cash Flow in Q1

Free cash flow generation remained strong in Q1 FY2027, continuing the trajectory established in FY2026. The company used excess cash to further reduce gross debt, with the net leverage ratio trending toward the stated target of below 2.0x adjusted EBITDA. Inventory days remained within management's target range, and the company reported no supply chain disruptions affecting production or delivery schedules.

## Q2 FY2027 Guidance

Semtech provided the following financial guidance for Q2 FY2027 (quarter ending July 2026):

| Metric | Q2 FY2027 Guidance | Q2 FY2026 Actual | Implied YoY Growth |
|--------|--------------------|------------------|--------------------|
| Revenue | $328 million | $282 million | +16.3% |
| Adjusted EPS | $0.61 | $0.48 | +27.1% |
| Adjusted Gross Margin | ~53.5% | 52.0% | +150 bps |

The Q2 FY2027 guidance implies a sequential revenue increase of approximately 13% from Q1 FY2027, reflecting the continued ramp of 1.6T optical products, seasonal strength in IoT deployments, and steady demand across the analog product portfolio. The guided adjusted EPS of $0.61 represents a sequential increase of approximately 20% from Q1 FY2027's $0.51, driven by revenue growth, gross margin expansion, and operating leverage.

## Key Management Commentary

During the Q1 FY2027 earnings call, CEO Hong Hou emphasized three strategic priorities:

1. **1.6T Optical Ramp** — Customer qualification activity for 1.6T interconnect products is accelerating, with multiple hyperscale customers expected to begin volume deployments in the second half of FY2027. Semtech believes it is well-positioned to capture a leading share of this transition.

2. **LoRa Ecosystem Momentum** — LoRa device deployments continue to grow globally, with particular strength in smart metering, asset tracking, and building management applications across Europe and Asia-Pacific.

3. **3.2T Development Pipeline** — Semtech is investing in next-generation optical interconnect technologies targeting 3.2T data rates, positioning the company for continued technology leadership as data center bandwidth requirements compound at 30-40% annually.

CFO Mark Lin highlighted the company's improving cash flow profile and reiterated the balanced capital allocation strategy prioritizing debt reduction, organic R&D investment, and opportunistic shareholder returns.

## FY2027 Full-Year Outlook

While Semtech provides guidance only one quarter forward, management offered qualitative color on the full fiscal year 2027 during the Q1 earnings call. The company expects FY2027 revenue to exceed $1.2 billion, representing approximately 15% growth over FY2026. Adjusted gross margin is expected to average 53-54% for the full year, with the second half benefiting from a richer mix of 1.6T optical products. Adjusted EPS for FY2027 is anticipated to exceed $2.20, driven by revenue growth, gross margin expansion, and continued operating expense leverage. Free cash flow is expected to exceed $200 million for the full year, further strengthening the balance sheet and providing capital allocation flexibility.
