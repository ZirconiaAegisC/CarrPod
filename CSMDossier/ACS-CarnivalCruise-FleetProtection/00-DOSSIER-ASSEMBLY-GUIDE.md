## Purpose

This document provides instructions for the professional printing, binding, and distribution of the Fleet Hull Excellence Program Dossier. Adherence to these specifications ensures consistent presentation quality for all recipients, appropriate security treatment of confidential materials, and traceability of all distributed copies.

---

## Required Materials

### Paper
- **Stock:** 28 lb (105 gsm) bright white, acid-free, 8.5 × 11 inch (Letter size, US) for all documents.
- **Tab dividers:** Pre-printed 5-tab set, Mylar-reinforced, for the five main dossier sections.
- **Cover stock:** 80 lb (216 gsm) card stock, navy blue, for front and back covers.

### Binding
- **Primary method:** Wire-O binding (double-loop wire), 7/8 inch (22 mm) diameter, black finish. Accommodates up to 240 sheets. For copies exceeding this page count, use 1 inch (25 mm) diameter binding.
- **Alternative (executive copies):** Cerlox comb binding, 1 inch (25 mm), black, for copies intended for copying or scanning.
- **Cover lamination:** Clear matte lamination (5 mil) on front cover only for C-suite copies (Copies 1–4). Standard unlaminated cover for all other copies.

### Printing Specifications
- **Color printing:** Required throughout. Charts, tables, the cover letter, and the signature page contain color elements including the Carnival Corporation brand colors (navy blue, #003A70; red, #E31837; gold accent, #C69214). Black-and-white reproduction is unacceptable for distribution copies.
- **Duplex:** All pages printed double-sided (duplex) except for the Signature Page (single-sided) and section divider pages (single-sided).
- **Resolution:** 600 dpi minimum. 1200 dpi recommended for pages containing fine detail (implementation framework tables, prioritization matrices).
- **Alignment:** Centered on page (equal margins left and right after binding compensation).

### USB Drive
- **Type:** USB 3.0 flash drive, 32 GB minimum, USB-A connector.
- **Branding:** White label with black text: "ACS-CCL-FP | Carnival Corporation | CONFIDENTIAL | July 2026"
- **Quantity:** 12 units, one per bound copy.
- **Content:** Per USB Master Manifest (Document ACS-CCL-FP-USB-001, see Section 08-USB-Content).

## Distribution Protocol

### Pre-Distribution
1. All copies are quality-checked for completeness, pagination, and binding integrity before distribution.
2. Each copy is logged in the Copy Tracking Log with copy number, recipient, date issued, and initials of the issuing party.
3. An electronic distribution list is maintained, mapping each recipient to their assigned copy number.

### Distribution
1. Copies 1–4 are hand-delivered to recipients at Carnival Corporation headquarters (3655 NW 87th Avenue, Miami, FL 33178) by courier or in person by the Program Director.
2. Copies 5–10 are shipped via FedEx Priority Overnight (or equivalent secure courier) with signature-required delivery to each brand president's office. Hamburg and European copies may use DHL Express.
3. Copies 11–12 are retained by the program team at the designated program office.
4. Each recipient signs the Acknowledgement of Receipt form (last page of the Signature Page document, 00-SIGNATURE-PAGE.md). Signed forms are returned to, and retained by, the Advanced Coatings Strategy Group administrator.

### Post-Distribution
1. Signed acknowledgement forms are scanned and filed digitally with the program records.
2. The Copy Tracking Log is updated to reflect confirmed receipt.
3. Lost or damaged copies are replaced ONLY after the recipient confirms in writing (email accepted) that the original copy has been destroyed or is otherwise accounted for. The replacement copy receives a new copy number noted with a suffix (e.g., Copy 5-R1).

## USB Drive Loading

Each USB drive must contain:
1. The complete dossier in PDF format (single file, bookmarked, 600 dpi).
2. The complete dossier in native editable format (Microsoft Word .docx or equivalent).
3. Supplementary data files per the USB Master Manifest (Document ACS-CCL-FP-USB-001, Section 08-USB-Content).
4. A plain text README file (02-README.txt, Section 08-USB-Content).

USB drives are loaded, virus-scanned, and verified (all files open successfully) before insertion into the Tyvek sleeve.

---

## Archival and Retention

1. The master electronic files are stored on Carnival Corporation's secure document management system (Microsoft SharePoint, Technical Operations document library).
2. The master print files (PDF and native format) are archived with a retention period of 15 years from the date of dossier issue.
3. Spare bound copies (minimum 3) are produced during the initial print run and stored flat at the Program Office for future distribution to new executives, board members, or audit personnel.

## Security Classification Handling

This dossier is classified **Confidential — Carnival Corporation Internal**.

- Do not distribute outside Carnival Corporation & plc, its subsidiaries, and authorized contractors without written authorization from the Program Director or above.
- Do not photocopy, scan, or reproduce without authorization.
- Damaged or superseded copies must be shredded (cross-cut, DIN Level P-4 or higher) or returned to the Program Office for controlled destruction.
- Electronic copies must not be transmitted via unencrypted email. Use Carnival Corporation's approved secure file transfer system (e.g., SharePoint with restricted access permissions or secure FTP).
