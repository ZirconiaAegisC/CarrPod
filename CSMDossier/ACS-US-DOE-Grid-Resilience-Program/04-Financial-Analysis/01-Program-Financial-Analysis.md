- Direct transformer damage: $8-25B
- GDP impact (multi-year restoration): $1.2-2.6T
- Societal costs (health, safety, cascading failures): $200-500B

**Program ROI to U.S. Economy:**
- Investment: $5B (10 years)
- Loss prevented (1 event): $1.2-2.6T
- ROI: 240x to 520x
- Annual federal investment: ~$340M (0.005% of federal budget)
