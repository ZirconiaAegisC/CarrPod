## 1. AGENCY OVERVIEW

**Relevant Offices:** Office of Electricity (OE), Grid Deployment Office (GDO), Office of Cybersecurity, Energy Security, and Emergency Response (CESER)

## 2. KEY LEADERSHIP

**Jennifer M. Granholm — Secretary of Energy.** Former Governor of Michigan (2003-2011). Led Michigan's economic transformation after the 2008 auto crisis — relevant experience for managing large-scale infrastructure transformation. Oversees DOE's $52B budget and 14,000+ employees.

**David Turk — Deputy Secretary.** Former Deputy Executive Director of the International Energy Agency (IEA). Deep understanding of global energy infrastructure policy.

**Maria D. Robinson — Director, Grid Deployment Office.** Oversees IIJA-funded grid resilience programs including the $10B Grid Resilience State and Tribal Formula Grants. Primary programmatic stakeholder for a national GIC hardening program.

**Gene Rodrigues — Assistant Secretary for Electricity.** Oversees DOE's electricity delivery and energy reliability research, including the GMD research portfolio. Former VP at ICF with 30+ years in energy efficiency and grid modernization.

## 3. EXISTING DOE GMD PROGRAMS

**DOE GMD Research and Development Strategy (2024):** Identifies transformer GIC protection as a priority research area. Validates NBC technology as a promising mitigation approach.

**Grid Resilience State and Tribal Formula Grants (IIJA Section 40101):** $10B over 5 years for grid resilience projects. GIC hardening is explicitly eligible but no state has yet submitted a GIC-focused grant application — representing a first-mover opportunity.

**DOE OE-417 Emergency Reporting:** Electric emergency incident reporting system. Currently captures weather-related outages but has limited GMD-specific incident categorization. This program would enhance OE-417 with GIC monitoring data integration.

**Loan Programs Office (LPO):** Title XVII Innovative Clean Energy loan guarantee program. $40B+ in remaining loan authority. GIC hardening could qualify under "grid modernization" category.
