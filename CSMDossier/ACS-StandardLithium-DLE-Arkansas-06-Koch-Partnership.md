# Standard Lithium — Koch Industries Partnership

## Partnership Overview

In 2025, Standard Lithium announced a strategic partnership with Koch Industries
through its subsidiary Koch Technology Solutions (KTS). The partnership represents
a significant milestone in Standard Lithium's development, bringing the engineering
capability, project execution expertise, and financial credibility of one of the
largest privately held companies in the United States to the lithium extraction
program.

## Koch Industries Profile

Koch Industries is one of the largest privately held companies in the world, with
annual revenue exceeding $125 billion across diverse business segments including
energy, chemicals, fertilizers, forest products, electronics, and commodities
trading. Koch's chemicals and process technology division (Koch Technology Solutions,
incorporating Koch-Glitsch, Koch Modular, and other businesses) specializes in mass
transfer, separation, and chemical processing technologies with decades of experience
in brine processing and industrial chemical operations.

Koch's involvement in the lithium industry reflects the company's broader strategic
interest in energy transition materials and technologies. The company has been
selectively investing in and partnering with companies across the battery materials
supply chain.

## Partnership Structure

The partnership encompasses several elements:

**Technology Collaboration:** KTS brings complementary DLE technology and process
engineering expertise to the project. The partnership may involve integration of
KTS's lithium-selective sorbent or extraction technology with Standard Lithium's
LiSTR process, or selection of the optimal technology approach based on comparative
performance data.

**Engineering and Project Execution:** KTS provides detailed engineering design,
procurement support, and project management for the Phase 1A commercial facility.
Koch's engineering scale and experience with large industrial projects reduces
execution risk.

**Strategic Investment:** Koch has made (or is expected to make) a strategic equity
investment in Standard Lithium, aligning interests and providing capital for
development. The investment structure and amount are governed by the partnership
agreement.

**Offtake and Commercialization:** Koch's extensive industrial relationships may
facilitate offtake agreements with battery manufacturers and cathode producers,
leveraging Koch's commercial network.

## Strategic Significance

The Koch partnership addresses several key challenges for Standard Lithium:

**Engineering Credibility:** As a relatively small development-stage company,
Standard Lithium benefits from association with Koch's established engineering
reputation. This enhances credibility with project finance lenders, off-takers,
and government stakeholders.

**Execution Capability:** Koch's project management and engineering resources
significantly augment Standard Lithium's in-house team, which numbers approximately
80-100 employees. Large-scale chemical plant construction requires capabilities
that are difficult for a junior company to maintain internally.

**Financial Flexibility:** Koch's balance sheet strength ($125B+ annual revenue,
strong credit profile) provides financial flexibility that Standard Lithium cannot
access independently. This may include direct investment, project-level guarantees,
or facilitation of third-party financing.

**Strategic Validation:** Koch's involvement represents third-party validation of
Standard Lithium's technology and project economics from one of the most
sophisticated industrial companies in the world. This validation is valuable for
attracting additional partners, lenders, and offtakers.

## Risk Considerations

While overwhelmingly positive, the Koch partnership does introduce considerations
including dilution to existing shareholders from strategic equity investment,
potential influence over strategic decisions (technology selection, project scope,
partnership structure), commercial terms of the partnership relative to market
norms, and execution dependency on Koch's continued engagement and resource
allocation to the project.

## Koch's Broader Lithium Strategy

Koch's involvement with Standard Lithium fits within a broader lithium and battery
materials strategy. Koch Engineered Solutions, Koch Minerals & Trading, and other
Koch entities are active across the lithium value chain including DLE technology
development and licensing, engineering and equipment supply for lithium projects,
potential lithium offtake and trading, and broader energy transition investments.
The Standard Lithium partnership provides Koch with a commercial-scale deployment
platform for its lithium processing technology and a pathway to participation in
the growing North American lithium supply chain.
