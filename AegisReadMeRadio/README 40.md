THE QUICK PITCH

Every communication system humanity depends on — radio, cellular, satellite — operates on electron-based physics. During a Carrington event, the electromagnetic environment becomes chaos, and every electron-based channel degrades simultaneously. CSMProtonics explores an alternative: proton-based communication using hydrogen field-effect transistors that modulate proton current through a palladium-gated channel. Protons are 1,836 times heavier than electrons. They do not respond to geomagnetic frequencies. A protonic transceiver cannot be jammed by a Carrington event because the physics of the charge carrier is mismatched to the physics of the threat. Agent Solara Vance embodies this technology. In SiblingFrequency Episode 1, when she switches the communication grid to protonic backup, the dialogue was fact-checked against this directory. Step into the protonics lab.
