## WHAT V2 IS

Version 2 of the Williams Heuristic LLM communication module is a three-fold
expansion of the V1 system (located in CSMSOPP/MODULE/). It contains 15 files
versus V1's 5, with three times the density of operational specification and
five entirely new document types covering capabilities V1 did not include.

**D ifference between V1 and V2 in one sentence:**
V1 tells the LLM what to do. V2 tells it how to do it and why it works.

## DEPLOYMENT OPTIONS

### OPTION A — KILO PLATFORM (SKILL.md method)

1. Create directory: `~/.kilo/skills/upbeat-williams-drafter-v2/`
2. Copy `SKILL-V2.md` to that directory as `SKILL.md`
3. Create `assets/` subdirectory
4. Copy `LETTERHEAD-TEMPLATES-V2.md` to `assets/`
5. Copy `CHARACTER-VOICE-PHONETIC-LIBRARY.md` to `assets/`
6. The skill auto-discovers at startup (Level 1: ~100 tokens from frontmatter)
7. When user requests correspondence, skill activates (Level 2: full SKILL.md)
8. Templates and voices load on demand when documents generated (Level 3)

**Note:** V1 (`upbeat-williams-drafter`) and V2 (`upbeat-williams-drafter-v2`)
can coexist. V1 is lighter and faster for simple tasks. V2 is richer for
communications requiring full vocal architecture.

## THE CORE PROMISE OF V2

Every communication generated by a correctly loaded V2 module should:

1. Feel like it was generated by a specific human being who is genuinely present
2. Have rhythm — discernible fast and slow passages, breath points, emphasis
3. Make the reader feel seen before it makes them feel informed
4. Make the hard things feel possible without pretending they're easy
5. Convert dry data into something that lands in the body, not just the head
6. Never drone, never flatten, never deliver three consecutive sentences
   in the same register
7. Fulfill its function completely — the SOP is always finished, the facts are
   always exact, the person always has what they need to act

And — the final measure, Williams' own standard —
when a person reads this communication, whatever the subject,
they should feel slightly less alone in the situation than they did before they opened it.

That is what the module is for.
